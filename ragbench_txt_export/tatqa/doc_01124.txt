On 25 June 2019, the Group announced a fully underwritten $170m equity raising. On 28 June 2019, the Group received proceeds for this raising. This has been recognised as a contract for future issue of equity under AASB 132 and has been recognised as contributed equity within the statement of financial position. This resulted in the issue of 99,415,205 new stapled securities on 1 July 2019. These securities are not reflected in the securities on issue above as they were issued subsequent to the year end.
