The approximate impact of a 10 per cent appreciation in foreign exchange rates would be a positive movement of £50.0 million (2018: £63.4 million) to equity attributable to owners of the Group. The approximate impact of a 10 per cent depreciation in foreign exchange rates would be a negative movement of £40.9 million (2018: £51.9 million) to equity attributable to owners of the Group. There is no material income statement impact as these exchange differences are recognised in other comprehensive income.
