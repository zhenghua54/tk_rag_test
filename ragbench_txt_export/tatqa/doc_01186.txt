On 29 August 2019, the Board of Directors declared a final dividend in respect of the 2019 period of 57 cents per share, fully franked at a 30% tax rate. The amount will be paid on or around 30 September 2019 and is expected to be $717 million. As the dividend was declared subsequent to 30 June 2019, no provision had been made as at 30 June 2019.
