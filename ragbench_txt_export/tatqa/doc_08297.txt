The U.S. Internal Revenue Service completed exams on the Company's U.S. federal income tax returns for years 2012 - 2015. With few exceptions, the Company is no longer subject to state and local income tax examinations by tax authorities for years before 2015. The Company conducts business and files income tax returns in numerous states. Currently, one of the Company's state tax returns is under examination by a state as part of routine audits conducted in the ordinary course of business.
