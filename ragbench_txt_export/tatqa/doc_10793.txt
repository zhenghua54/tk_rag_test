While demand in the markets served by Siemens Healthineers continued to grow in fiscal 2019, these markets also showed price pressure on new purchases and increased utilization rates for installed systems. All major served markets were in a healthy state, which contributed to a slightly higher market growth in Europe, C. I. S., Africa, Middle East and the Americas, most notably in the imaging and advanced therapies markets. The markets in Asia, Australia grew moderately. Markets in the U. S. showed slight growth in the imaging and clear growth in the advanced therapies
business, with continued moderate market growth in diagnostics.
