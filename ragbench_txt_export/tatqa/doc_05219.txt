The Company records unearned revenue when payments are received from tenants in advance of the completion of the Company’s performance obligations. Long-term unearned revenue is included in Other non-current liabilities. During the year ended December 31, 2019, the Company recognized $62.2 million of revenue that was included in the Unearned revenue balance as of December 31, 2018. During the year ended December 31, 2018, the Company recognized $44.4 million of revenue that was included in the Unearned revenue balance as of January 1, 2018. The Company also recognized revenues of $59.2 million and $55.4 million during the years ended December 31, 2019 and December 31, 2018, respectively, for capital contributions related to DAS networks.
