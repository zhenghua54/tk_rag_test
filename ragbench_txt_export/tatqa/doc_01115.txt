Goodwill is allocated to the appropriate cash-generating unit (‘CGU’) based on the smallest identifiable group of assets that generates cash inflows independently in relation to the specific goodwill. The recoverable amount of the CGU is determined from value-in-use calculations that use cash flow projections from the latest three-year plan.
