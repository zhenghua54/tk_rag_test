Cost of Cloud Subscriptions, Maintenance and Services Year 2019 compared with year 2018 Cost of cloud subscriptions, maintenance and services consists primarily of salaries and other personnel-related expenses of employees dedicated to cloud subscriptions; maintenance services; and professional and technical services as well as hosting fees. The $46.8 million increase in 2019 compared to 2018 was principally due to a $25.8 million increase in compensation and other personnelrelated expense resulting from increased headcount in cloud operations and professional services, a $9.4 million increase in performance-based compensation expense, and a $8.5 million increase in computer infrastructure costs related to cloud business transition.
