Year 2018 compared with year 2017 Services revenue increased $3.2 million in 2018 compared to 2017 primarily due to improving demand in the Americas and solid growth in EMEA. Services revenue for the Americas and EMEA segment increased $1.0 million and $6.9 million, respectively, and services revenue for the APAC segment decreased $4.7 million, compared to 2017.
