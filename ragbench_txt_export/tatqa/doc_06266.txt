Year 2018 compared with year 2017 Sales and marketing expenses increased $3.8 million in 2018 compared to 2017, due primarily to an increase of $2.9 million in marketing and campaign programs, a $1.1 million increase in performance-based compensation and a $0.7 million increase in compensation and other personnel-related expenses, partially offset by a $2.0 million decrease in commissions expense as we must defer a portion of our sales commission expense and amortize it over time as the corresponding services are transferred to the customer under ASC 606.
