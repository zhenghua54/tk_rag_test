Diesel Fuel Specifications This diesel engine has been developed to take advantage of the high energy content and generally lower cost Number 2 Ultra Low Sulfur diesel fuel or Number 2 Ultra Low Sulfur climatized diesel fuels.  Note: If you accidentally fill the fuel tank with gasoline or DEF on your diesel vehicle, do not start the engine.  Damage to the engine and fuel system could occur.  Please call an authorized dealer for service.  A maximum blend of 5% biodiesel meeting ASTM specification D-975 may be used with your diesel engine without any adjustments to regular service schedules.  Commercially available fuel additives are not necessary for the proper operation of your diesel engine.  Number 1 Ultra Low Sulfur diesel fuel should only be used where extended arctic conditions (-10°F or -23°C) exist.
