To ensure proper engine lubrication, the engine oil must be maintained at the correct level.  Check the oil level at regular intervals, such as every fuel stop.  The best time to check the engine oil level is about five minutes after a fully warmed up engine is shut off.  Check engine oil level.  Check windshield washer fluid level.
