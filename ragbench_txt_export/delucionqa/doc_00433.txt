2H Two-Wheel Drive High Range — This range is for normal street and highway driving on dry, hard surfaced roads.  4H AUTO Four-Wheel Drive Auto High Range — This range sends power to the front wheels.  The four-wheel drive system will be automatically engaged when the vehicle senses a loss of traction.  This range provides additional traction for varying road conditions.  4H Four-Wheel Drive High Range — This range maximizes torque to the front driveshaft, forcing the front and rear wheels to rotate at the same speed.  This range provides additional traction for loose, slippery road surfaces only.
