Cruise Control Ready Indicator Light — If Equipped With A Base Instrument Cluster This light will turn on when cruise control has been turned on, but not set.  Cruise Control Ready Indicator Light — If Equipped With A Premium Instrument Cluster This light will turn on when the cruise control has been turned on, but not set.  Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) Ready Indicator Light — If Equipped With a Premium Instrument Cluster This light will turn on when the vehicle equipped with Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) has been turned on, but not set.
