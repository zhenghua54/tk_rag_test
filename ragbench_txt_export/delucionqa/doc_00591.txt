This vehicle is equipped with an Intelligent Battery Sensor (IBS) to perform additional monitoring of the electrical system and status of the vehicle battery.  1 — Supplemental Negative Battery Cable 2 — Main Negative Battery Cable 3 — Main Negative Battery Terminal 4 — Intelligent Battery Sensor (IBS) In cases when the IBS detects charging system failure, or the vehicle battery conditions are deteriorating, electrical load reduction actions will take place to extend the driving time and distance of the vehicle.  This is done by reducing power to or turning off non-essential electrical loads.
