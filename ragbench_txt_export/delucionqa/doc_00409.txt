Push and release the Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) on/off button.  The ACC menu in the instrument cluster display will read “ACC Ready.” The buttons on the right side of the steering wheel operate the ACC system.  To turn the system off, push and release the Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) on/off button again.
