The vehicle may be equipped with a refueling funnel.  Vehicles with diesel engines will have a cap to remove to begin the refueling process.  If refueling is necessary, while using an approved gas can, insert the refueling funnel into the filler neck opening.  Take care to open both flappers with the funnel to avoid spills.
