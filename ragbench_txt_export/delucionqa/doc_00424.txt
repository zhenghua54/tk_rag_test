To Activate Push the on/off button to activate the Cruise Control.  The cruise indicator light in the instrument cluster display will illuminate.  To turn the system off, push the on/off button a second time.  The cruise indicator light will turn off.  The system should be turned off when not in use.  WARNING: Leaving the Cruise Control system on when not in use is dangerous.  You could accidentally set the system or cause it to go faster than you want.  You could lose control and have an accident.  Always ensure the system is off when you are not using it.
