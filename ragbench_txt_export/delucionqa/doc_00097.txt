Towing Tips Before towing, practice turning, stopping and backing the trailer in an area away from heavy traffic.  If using a manual transmission vehicle for trailer towing, all starts must be in first gear to avoid excessive clutch slippage.  TRAILER TOWING In this section you will find safety tips and information on limits to the type of towing you can reasonably do with your vehicle.  Before towing a trailer, carefully review this information to tow your load as efficiently and safely as possible.  To maintain the New Vehicle Limited Warranty coverage, follow the requirements and recommendations in this manual concerning vehicles used for trailer towing.
