Selection Of Lubricant Use only the manufacturer’s recommended fluid Selection Of Lubricant It is important to use the proper transmission fluid to ensure optimum transmission performance and life.  Use only manufacturer's specified transmission fluid It is important to maintain the transmission fluid at the correct level using the recommended fluid.  Note: No chemical flushes should be used in any transmission; only the approved lubricant should be used.  CAUTION: Using a transmission fluid other than manufacturer’s recommended fluid may cause deterioration in transmission shift quality and/or torque converter shudder Selection Of Lubricant Use only manufacturer's recommended manual transmission fluid
