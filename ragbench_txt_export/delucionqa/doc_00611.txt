Push and release the up or down arrow button until the fuel economy icon is highlighted in the instrument cluster display.  Push and hold the ok button to reset average fuel economy feature.  Toggle left or right to select a display with or without current fuel economy information.  Fuel Economy The pointer shows the level of fuel in the fuel tank when the ignition switch is in the ON/RUN position.  The fuel pump symbol points to the side of the vehicle where the fuel filler door is located
