Fastener Bin Your vehicle is equipped with a Fastener Bin used to store the fasteners for the doors, windshield, and hard top (if equipped) while not in use.  The Fastener Bin is located behind the rear seat storage bin.  The Fastener Bin contains foam inserts for each of the fasteners; they are removable.  To remove the Fastener Bin: Remove the lid and foam inserts from the Fastener Bin.  Remove the three nuts from the bottom of the Fastener Bin.  Note: The soft top fasteners do not go in the Fastener Bin.
