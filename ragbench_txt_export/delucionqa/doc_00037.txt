Air Bag Warning Light The Air Bag Warning Light will turn on for four to eight seconds as a bulb check when the ignition switch is first placed in the ON/RUN position.  If the light is either not on during starting, stays on, or turns on while driving, have the system inspected at an authorized dealer as soon as possible.  This light will illuminate with a single chime when a fault with the Air Bag Warning Light has been detected, it will stay on until the fault is removed.
