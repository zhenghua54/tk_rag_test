Redundant Air Bag Warning Light If a fault with the Air Bag Warning Light is detected, which could affect the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS), the Redundant Air Bag Warning Light will illuminate on the instrument panel.  The Redundant Air Bag Warning Light will stay on until the fault is cleared.  In addition, a single chime will sound to alert you that the Redundant Air Bag Warning Light has come on and a fault has been detected.  If the Redundant Air Bag Warning Light comes on intermittently or remains on while driving have an authorized dealer service the vehicle immediately.
