Keyless Enter ‘n Go™ Ignition This feature allows the driver to operate the ignition switch with the push of a START/STOP ignition button as long as the key fob is in the passenger compartment.  The START/STOP ignition button has several operating modes that are labeled and will illuminate when in position.  These modes are OFF, ACC, RUN, and START.
