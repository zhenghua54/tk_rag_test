Cleaning The TrailCam Press and hold the Clean Camera button located on the TrailCam view to wash the TrailCam.  Washer fluid will stop when the button is released.  The camera can be washed up to 20 seconds at a time while holding the button.  The Clean Camera system is not available when windshield washing is in process.  When enabled, active dynamic Tire Lines are projected on the ground plane of the TrailCam view based on the steering wheel position.
