The Stop/Start feature is enabled after every normal engine start.  It will remain in STOP/START NOT READY until you drive forward with a vehicle speed greater than 5 mph (8 km/h).  At that time, the system will go into STOP/START READY and if all other conditions are met, can go into an STOP/START AUTOSTOP ACTIVE mode.  While in an Autostop, the Climate Control system may automatically adjust airflow to maintain cabin comfort.  Customer settings will be maintained upon return to an engine running condition.  The engine will shut down, the tachometer will move to the zero position and the Stop/Start telltale will illuminate indicating you are in Autostop.
