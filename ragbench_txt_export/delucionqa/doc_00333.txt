Upon first installation of the half-doors, slowly set the door almost to the closing position and check how the door latch aligns with the body striker.  Touch condition between these components can result in damage to both the door and the striker.  How To Engage The Automatic Locking Mode Always use this device when carrying children.  After engaging the child lock on both rear doors, check for effective engagement by trying to open a door with the internal handle.  Once the Child-Protection Door Lock system is engaged, it is impossible to open the doors from inside the vehicle.  Before getting out of the vehicle, be sure to check that there is no one left inside.  This indicator will illuminate when a door is ajar/open and not fully closed.
