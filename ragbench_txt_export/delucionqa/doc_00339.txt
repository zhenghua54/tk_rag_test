Push the Mechanical Key Release Button only with the key fob facing away from your body, especially your eyes and objects that may be damaged, such as clothing.  1 — Mechanical Key Release Button 2 — LED Indicator 3 — Unlock Button 4 — Lock Button 5 — Remote Start Button (If Equipped) 6 — Panic Button Do not press the mechanical key against the START/STOP ignition button.  Do not use sharp metal objects (e.g.  screwdriver etc.)  to pry the button out of the ignition switch.  This button comes as an assembly, and is not removable.  This can damage the silicone shield.
