Reid Harrison is an American screenwriter and television producer. He has written for television shows such as "The PJs", "George & Leo", "Men Behaving Badly", "Brother's Keeper", "Gary & Mike", "George of the Jungle", "Pinky and the Brain", "Duckman", "Drawn Together", "The Mullets", "3 South", "Tak and the Power of Juju" and "Danger Mouse".
