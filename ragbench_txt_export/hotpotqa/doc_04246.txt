The Ryukyuan people (琉球民族 , Ryūkyū minzoku , Okinawan: "Ruuchuu minzuku") (also Lewchewan or Uchinaanchu (沖縄人 , Japanese: "Okinawa jin") ) are the indigenous peoples of the Ryukyu Islands between the islands of Kyushu and Taiwan. Politically, they live in either Okinawa Prefecture or Kagoshima Prefecture. Their languages make up the Ryukyuan language family, considered to be one of the two branches of the Japonic language family, the other one being Japanese and its dialects.
