Jane Cox is an English actress known for her role in ITV soap opera "Emmerdale" as farmer's wife Lisa Dingle, a role she has played since 1996. Cox has also starred in episodes of "Coronation Street", "Hetty Wainthrop Investigates" and "The Bill". Her most notable pre-"Emmerdale" role was as the leading female in the children's entertainment series "Allsorts". She has also appeared on Lily Savage's Blankety Blank.
