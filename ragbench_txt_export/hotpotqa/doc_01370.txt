The discography of Vanessa Hudgens consists of two studio albums, one extended play, four singles, two tours and four music videos. Hudgens has also recorded eleven singles and several other releases as her "High School Musical" character Gabriella Montez in the "High School Musical" film series' soundtracks. The singles she has recorded entered several international charts and most of them were duets with the cast members of the "High School Musical" series, Hudgens' most successful duet is "Breaking Free" which was number four in the "Billboard" Hot 100 chart.
