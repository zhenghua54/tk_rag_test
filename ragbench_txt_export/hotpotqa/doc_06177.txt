Michele Landis Dauber is an American lawyer, a leading figure focusing in constitutional history and law, distributive justice, federalism, gender & sexual orientation discrimination, inequality, law & society, public policy & empirical studies, torts and welfare & poverty law, currently the Frederick I. Richman Professor at Stanford Law School.. Dauber was also involved in the efforts to recall Judge Aaron Persky, the Santa Clara County judge who rendered the verdict in People v. Turner.
