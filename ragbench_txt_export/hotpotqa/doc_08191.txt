Nee Ko Njaa Cha (acronym of Ninnem Kollum Njaanum Chavum) ("English:Kill you, then Kill Myself") is a 2013 Indian Malayalam comedy thriller film written and directed by Gireesh. The story, set in Kochi and Goa, follows the lives of three friends played by Sunny Wayne, Sanju and Praveen Anidil. Poojitha Menon, Sija Rose, Rohini Idiculla and Parvathy Nair form the female leads. Shooting was carried out in Trivandrum and Kovalam and some shots in Kochi. It is a remake of the English rom-com Forgetting Sarah Marshall.
