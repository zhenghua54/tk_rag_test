Hiroyuki Ito (伊藤 裕之 , Itō Hiroyuki ) , also credited with the spelling Hiroyuki Itou, is a Japanese game producer, director and designer who works for Square Enix. He is known as the director of "Final Fantasy VI" (1994), "Final Fantasy IX" (2000) and "Final Fantasy XII" (2006) and as the creator of the Active Time Battle (ATB) system in the "Final Fantasy" series.
