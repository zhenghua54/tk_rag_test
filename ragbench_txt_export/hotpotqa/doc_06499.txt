Matthew "Matt" Letscher (born June 26, 1970) is an American actor and playwright, known for his roles as Captain Harrison Love in the 1998 American swashbuckler film "The Mask of Zorro" and as Colonel Adelbert Ames in the 2003 American film "Gods and Generals". He co-starred in the 2016 Michael Bay film "", playing Ambassador J. Christopher Stevens. He has also portrayed Eobard Thawne/Reverse-Flash in "The Flash" and "Legends of Tomorrow".
