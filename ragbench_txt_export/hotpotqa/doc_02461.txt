American Crime Story is an American true crime anthology television series developed by Scott Alexander and Larry Karaszewski, who are executive producers with Brad Falchuk, Nina Jacobson, Ryan Murphy, and Brad Simpson. It premiered on the cable network FX in the United States on February 2, 2016. Similar to "American Horror Story", also from Murphy and Falchuk, each season is presented as a self-contained miniseries, following separate unrelated true events.
