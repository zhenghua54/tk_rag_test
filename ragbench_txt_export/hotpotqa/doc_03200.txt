Daniel Terrence LeFevour (born March 19, 1987) is a Canadian football quarterback for the Winnipeg Blue Bombers of the Canadian Football League (CFL). He was drafted by the Chicago Bears in the sixth round of the 2010 NFL Draft. He played college football at Central Michigan University. Known as a dual-threat quarterback, LeFevour had the most total touchdowns in NCAA history until being surpassed by Case Keenum in 2011.
