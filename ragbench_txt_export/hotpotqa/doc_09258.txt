Ashley Victoria Benson (born December 18, 1989) is an American actress, known for portraying the role of Hanna Marin in the teen mystery-drama television series "Pretty Little Liars" (2010-2017). She has also portrayed Abigail Deveraux on the NBC soap opera "Days of Our Lives", Mia Torcoletti on the supernatural television series "Eastwick", and Brit in the 2013 crime thriller film "Spring Breakers".
