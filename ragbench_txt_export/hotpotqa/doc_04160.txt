Turner is a census-designated place and unincorporated community in Blaine County, Montana, United States. Turner is located on Montana State Highway 241 41.5 mi east-northeast of Chinook. Its population was 61 as of the 2010 census. Turner has a post office with ZIP code 59542; it also has an airport, Turner Airport. Turner is the farthest community in the continental U.S. from a Major League Baseball park; it lies 646.93 mi from Safeco Field in Seattle, the nearest park.
