"Speed 3" is the third episode of the third series of the Channel 4 sitcom "Father Ted" and the 20th episode overall. The episode parodies the action-thriller film "Speed" and the sequel "". "Speed 3" was written after the show's writers Graham Linehan and Arthur Mathews asked themselves: "How can we make a worse sequel than "Speed 2"?" This episode was voted the fans' favourite episode on Channel 4's "Father Ted Night."
