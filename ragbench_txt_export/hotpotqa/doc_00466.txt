They were signed to Island Records from 2001 to 2012 and have released five albums and one extended play to date. Their fifth studio album, "Fight or Flight", was released on September 11, 2012. They have sold 10 million albums worldwide. The band is best known for their singles "Crawling in the Dark", "Running Away", and "The Reason".
