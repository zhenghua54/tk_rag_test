The 2011 Premiership Rugby Sevens Series, (styled for sponsorship reasons as the 2011 J.P Morgan Asset Management Premiership Rugby 7s Series) was the second Rugby Union 7-a-side competition for the 12 2011-12 Aviva Premiership Clubs. It began on Friday July 15 and lasted 4 Weeks, with the final at the Twickenham Stoop on August 5, 2011.
