Kenn Scott is a Toronto-based screenwriter noted for his work in children's programming and animation. Included amongst the many shows he has written for are "Ned's Newt", "Iggy Arbuckle", "Captain Flamingo", "Rescue Heroes", "Seven Little Monsters", "Pelswick", "Quads!", "Delilah and Julius", "Dino Dan" and "Doki". His column "A Writer's Life" appears regularly in the magazine "Canadian Screenwriter".
