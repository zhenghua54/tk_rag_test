The Super Bowl XLIX halftime show took place on February 1, 2015, at the University of Phoenix Stadium in Glendale, Arizona as part of Super Bowl XLIX. It featured American singer Katy Perry, with singers Lenny Kravitz and Missy Elliott as special guests. The halftime show was critically acclaimed and attracted 118.5 million viewers, the largest ratings in the history of the Super Bowl. It also won two Emmy Awards in September 2015.
