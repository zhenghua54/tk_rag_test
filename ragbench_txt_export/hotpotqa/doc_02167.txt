Tina Baker (born 4 May 1958 (Age 59), in Coalville, Leicestershire, England) is a broadcaster and journalist and a leading British soap opera and TV critic. She has featured on many TV programmes such as, "Coronation Street Secrets", "The Good Soap Guide", "How Soaps Changed the World", Big Brother's Big Mouth, and "The Top 100 TV Christmas Crackers". She is well known as the soap opera expert on the morning television programme GMTV and is member of the judging panel on the annual "British Soap Awards".
