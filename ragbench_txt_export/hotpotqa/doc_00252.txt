Nitin Sahrawat (Born 13 August 1981), is an Indian television actor and a model. He is best known for his Indian and Pakistani TV commercials and for his portrayal of Rajveer Singh Ahluwalia in "Kitani Mohabbat Hai Season 2" which was telecast on Imagine TV. He is presently playing the character of Superstar Anand Kumar opposite Additi Gupta in ZEE TV's Qubool Hai.
