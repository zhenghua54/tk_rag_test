Ufotable, Inc. (ユーフォーテーブル有限会社 , Yūfōtēburu yūgen-gaisha ) is a Japanese animation studio founded in October 2000 by former TMS Entertainment staff through its subsidiary Telecom Animation Film and located in Nakano, Tokyo Prefecture. A unique hallmark seen in many of their works ("Ninja Nonsense", "Futakoi Alternative", "Coyote Ragtime Show", "Gakuen Utopia Manabi Straight!", "", "Kara no Kyōkai") is a claymation sequence.
