Ho Teng Iat () (born 1950 in Hangzhou, Zhejiang, China) is a Macau entrepreneur and was a member of the Legislative Assembly of Macau. She is the daughter of Ho Tin (), the founder of Sociedade Industrial Ho Tin S.A.R.L, and she acts as the managing director and CEO of her father's company. She is also the vice-president of Industrial Association of Macao.
