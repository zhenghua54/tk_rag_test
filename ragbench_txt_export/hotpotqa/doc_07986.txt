Renaissance Kingdoms is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game, developed by "Celsius Online". The game permits controlling a character in the 1460s kingdoms of Europe. It was created in October, 2004. It was declared Best Free Online RPG 2006 by the English site GameOgre. It is mostly played in a web interface, but many of the decisions are taking in an official forum. Registration is free, but there are some small benefits for players who make donations. The game is part of the universe "De gloria regni", beside "Native Kingdoms" and "Shogun Kingdoms", two similar games, based respectively on the Aztec Empire and the medieval Japan.
