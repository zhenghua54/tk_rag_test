"A Woman's Threat" is a single by American R&B singer R. Kelly from his fourth solo studio album and second sequel to 12 Play, TP-2.com. It's the fifth and last single on that album and a six-minute music video has been made for the song. The song charted at number 15 on the US bubbling under charts, 35 on the R&B/Hip Hop charts and below 50 on two other countries and over 50 on also two countries. The song is both written and produced by R. Kelly himself. Both 9th Wonder and Lil' Kim have sampled this song. Jay-Z also sampled the song in 2003.
