Umberto II (Italian: "Umberto Nicola Tommaso Giovanni Maria di Savoia" ; 15 September 190418 March 1983), was the last King of Italy. He reigned for 34 days, from 9 May 1946 to 12 June 1946, although he had been "de facto" head of state since 1944, and was nicknamed the May King (Italian: Re di Maggio ).
