Howard Matthew Moses "Howie" Long (born January 6, 1960) is an American former National Football League (NFL) defensive end, actor and sports analyst. He played in the NFL for 13 seasons and spent the entirety of his career with the Raiders franchise. During his tenure as a player, Long was named to eight Pro Bowls and helped the Raiders win a Super Bowl title in XVIII. He was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 2000.
