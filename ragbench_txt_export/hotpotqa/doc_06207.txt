Masakazu Mori (Japanese: 森 正和 ) , better known by his stage name , is a Japanese lyricist, composer and poet. He has written over 2,000 songs for numerous artists such as Kyosuke Himuro, Takuro Yoshida and Junichi Inagaki and theme songs for anime series including "Dragon Ball Z". His younger brother is Hideharu Mori, keyboardist of the rock band Picasso.
