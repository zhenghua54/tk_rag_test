Hasan Salihamidžić (born 1 January 1977) is a Bosnian former professional footballer. He is currently working as sporting director for FC Bayern Munich. After starting his club career with German side Hamburger SV, he made a name for himself while playing for Bayern Munich for nine seasons with whom he won the Bundesliga title six times, DFB-Pokal title four times, the 2000–01 UEFA Champions League, and the 2001 Intercontinental Cup. His only match in the UEFA Super Cup ended in defeat. He also played for Italian giants Juventus for four seasons.
