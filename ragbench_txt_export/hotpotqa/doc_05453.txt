James Duncan Rolfe (born July 10, 1980) is an American actor, filmmaker, film critic, video game critic, and internet personality best known for starring in the web television series "Angry Video Game Nerd", a joint production of Rolfe's Cinemassacre Productions, GameTrailers, and ScrewAttack on the video platform YouTube. His other projects include reviews of classic board games and "Movie Reviews" for the Spike network.
