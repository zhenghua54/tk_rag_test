Tao Okamoto (岡本 多緒 , Okamoto Tao , born May 22, 1985) , known professionally as Tao, is a Japanese actress and model. In 2009, she was one of the faces of Ralph Lauren. In 2013, she made her film debut as the female lead, Mariko Yashida, in "The Wolverine", opposite Hugh Jackman. Tao played Mercy Graves in the 2016 film "".
