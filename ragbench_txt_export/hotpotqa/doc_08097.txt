Yakuza 0 is an action-adventure video game developed and published by Sega. It is a prequel to the "Yakuza" series. The game takes place in December 1988 in Kamurocho, a fictionalized recreation of Tokyo's Kabukicho, and Sotenbori, a fictionalized recreation of Osaka's Dotonbori. It was released in Japan for PlayStation 3 and for PlayStation 4 on March 12, 2015, with the Taiwanese version released in May 2015. It was released in North America and Europe for PlayStation 4 in January 2017.
