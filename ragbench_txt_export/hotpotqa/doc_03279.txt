Francis Aurelio deSouza (born December 2, 1970) is an American entrepreneur and business executive who is the President and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Illumina Corporation. . Prior to becoming President and CEO in July 2016 , he served as President of Illumina from November 2013 to July 2016. Before Illumina, deSouza was President of Products and Services at Symantec Corporation. He joined Symantec in 2006 when Symantec acquired IMlogic, where deSouza was founder and CEO. Prior to IMlogic, deSouza worked at Microsoft from 1998 to 2001 after Microsoft acquired Flash Communications, where deSouza was co-founder and CEO.
