Jason Michael Lee (born April 25, 1970) is an American actor, producer and skateboarder. He is best known for his roles as Earl Hickey in the television comedy series "My Name Is Earl", David Seville in the "Alvin and the Chipmunks" film franchise and the voice of Syndrome in "The Incredibles". He is also known for his roles in Kevin Smith films such as "Mallrats", "Chasing Amy", "Dogma", "Jay and Silent Bob Strike Back", "Jersey Girl", "Clerks II" and "Cop Out".
