Cathy Moriarty (born November 29, 1960) is an American actress whose career spans over 30 years. She was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress for "Raging Bull" (1980). She also starred in films, including "Neighbors", "White of the Eye", "Soapdish", "Casper", "Analyze That", and "The Bounty Hunter".
