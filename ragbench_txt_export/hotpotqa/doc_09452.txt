Claudio Marchisio (] ; born 19 January 1986) is an Italian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Juventus and the Italian national team. A product of the Juventus youth system, he has spent his whole career thus far at his hometown club, with the exception of a season-long loan spell at Empoli, winning six consecutive Serie A titles. He is currently the club's second vice-captain, behind Giorgio Chiellini.
