Eaten by Lions is a British comedy feature film written and directed by Jason Wingard. The film stars Antonio Aakeel, Jack Carroll, Johnny Vegas, and Asim Chaudhry. The Film is the feature-length adaptation of the director's 2013 short film 'Going to Mecca' which won best comedy at the Manchester International Film Festival. The feature follows half-brothers Omar and Pete as they embark on a journey to find Omar's estranged father, confronting him on the day of his daughters engagement party.
