Digby is a small village and civil parish in the district of North Kesteven, Lincolnshire, England. The village is situated in the vale of the Digby Beck watercourse, 6 mi north from the town of Sleaford and 12 mi south from the city and county town of Lincoln. The village has a population of about 574, increasing to 621 at the 2011 census, and a parish council.
