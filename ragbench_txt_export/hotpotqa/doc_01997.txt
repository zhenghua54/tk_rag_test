Lingwai Daida (), variously translated as "Representative Answers from the Region beyond the Mountains", "Notes Answering [Curious Questions] from the land beyond the Pass" or other similar titles, is a 12th century geographical treatise written by Zhou Qufei (). It contains information on the geography, history, social custom and economy of territories of southern China, Guangxi in particular. It also includes descriptions of oversea states as far away as Africa and southern Spain.
