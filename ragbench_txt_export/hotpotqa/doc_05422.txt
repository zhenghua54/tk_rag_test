The 5-CD/5-DVD box set includes newly remixed versions of the albums "Genesis", "Invisible Touch", "We Can't Dance", and "...Calling All Stations...". The fifth pair of discs includes B-side songs. Each bonus DVD features audio versions of the albums in 5.1 surround sound, as well as videos for songs from that album and its corresponding tour, new interviews, and photo galleries.
