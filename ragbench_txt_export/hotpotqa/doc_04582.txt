Véronique Tristram is a fashion director for "Glamour" Germany since September 2015. Before that, she was an independent stylist for European and American magazines and fashion labels. She worked for international Condé Nast publications such as "W", "Vogue Sposa", and "GQ", and for German media and brands including Baldessarini, Hanro, Laurèl and Talbot Runhof. From 2006 to 2010 she was Fashion Director of "Cosmopolitan" Germany and from 2000 to 2006, Deputy active fashion director of "InStyle".
