Sergey Olegovich Sirotkin (Russian: Серге́й Оле́гович Сиро́ткин ; ] , born 27 August 1995 in Moscow) is a professional racing driver from Russia currently competing in the 2017 FIA Formula 2 Championship for ART Grand Prix. He was the 2011 Formula Abarth European Series champion. In July 2013, Sirotkin joined the Sauber Formula One team as a development driver as part of a tie-in with Russian investors with a view to promoting him to a race seat as early as 2014.
