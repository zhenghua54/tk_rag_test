I Got the Feeling is a new jack swing song released as a single by the Motown artists Today. The first track on Today's album titled "The New Formula", the single was released on August 17, 1990 on both vinyl and cassette. The song peaked at #12 on Billboard's Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart.
