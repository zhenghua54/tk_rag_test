Jackie Kessler (born December 8, 1970) is the American author of the "Hell on Earth" urban fantasy paranormal romance series published by Kensington/Zebra. To date, the books include "Hell's Belles" (January 2007; mass-market reissue in September 2008), "The Road to Hell" (November 2007) and "Hotter Than Hell" (August 2008), as well as a tie-in novella in the anthology, "Eternal Lover" (April 2008). She has had numerous short stories published in various magazines, including "Realms of Fantasy" and "Farthing". In 2009, Kessler published the superhero novel "Black and White" with co-author Caitlin Kittredge. The sequel, "Shades of Gray", was released in 2010.
