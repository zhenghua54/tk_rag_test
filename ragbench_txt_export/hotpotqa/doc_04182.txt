"Fat Camp" is the fifteenth episode of the fourth season of the animated television series "South Park", and the 63rd episode of the series overall. "Fat Camp" originally aired in the United States on December 6, 2000 on Comedy Central. In the episode, Kenny starts doing crazy, disgusting acts for cash in a parody of the television show "Jackass". Meanwhile, Cartman is sent to lose weight at a fat camp where he discovers a different way to earn money.
