Samurai Jack: The Shadow of Aku is an action-adventure video game released in 2004 by Adrenium Games and Published by Sega, in co-production with Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment and Cartoon Network Interactive and based on the "Samurai Jack" animated television series on Cartoon Network. The series' original voice actors, including Phil LaMarr, Mako Iwamatsu, Jeff Bennett, John DiMaggio, and Jennifer Hale, reprised their respective roles for the game. An Xbox version of the game was planned, but never released, even though it received "mixed" reviews according to the review aggregation website Metacritic.
