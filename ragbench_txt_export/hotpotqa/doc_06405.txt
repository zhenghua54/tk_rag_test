"A Christmassy Ted" is an episode of the Channel 4 sitcom "Father Ted". It is the 17th episode overall and was broadcast between the second and third series. This episode is 55 minutes long, as opposed to the usual 21-22 mins of all other "Father Ted" episodes. It was aired on Christmas Eve, seven months after the second series had ended.
