Criminology is a peer-reviewed academic journal published quarterly by Wiley-Blackwell on behalf of the American Society of Criminology. The editor-in-chief is Wayne Osgood (professor emeritus of criminology and sociology at the Pennsylvania State University). The co-editors are Eric P. Baumer (professor of sociology and criminology at the Pennsylvania State University) and Rosemary Gartner (professor emeritus of criminology at the University of Toronto). The journal covers research in criminology, penology, and criminal justice and is the flagship journal of ASC.
