The Lady of the Valley (Arabic: سيدة الوادي‎ ‎ , "Sayyidat al-Wadi") is a Marian shrine located in Al-Nasirah, Wadi al-Nasara in Syria. The statue is built near the Lady of the Valley Church on top of "Mount of the Pilgrim" (Arabic: جبل السائح‎ ‎ , "Jabal al-Sayeh"), which is dedicated to Saint George.
