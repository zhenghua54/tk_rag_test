Shenae Grimes-Beech ( ; born October 24, 1989), credited for most of her career as Shenae Grimes, is a Canadian actress. She portrayed Annie Wilson on "90210", a spin-off of "Beverly Hills, 90210". Prior to that, she had a recurring role on the television series "Naturally, Sadie", and played Darcy Edwards on "" for four seasons. Grimes played a cameo role in the horror film "Scream 4".
