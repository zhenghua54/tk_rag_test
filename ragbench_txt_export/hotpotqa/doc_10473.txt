The 2014–15 Magyar Kupa (English: "Hungarian Cup") is the 75th season of Hungary's annual knock-out cup football competition. It started with the first match of the first round on 7 August 2014 and ended with the final held in May 2015 at Ferenc Puskás Stadium, Budapest. Újpest are the defending champions, having won their ninth cup competition last season. The winner of the competition will qualify for the second qualifying round of the 2015–16 UEFA Europa League.
