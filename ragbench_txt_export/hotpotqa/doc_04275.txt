Majk (born Kastriot Rexha, November 27, 1990 in Prishtina, Kosovo) is an Albanian Kosovar rapper, singer and songwriter. Majk first gained fame in 2005 as a member of the then Albanian rap group TDS, with hit singles such as "Si Manekene" and "Mejrem". After the group split and going on a short hiatus, Majk returned with a hit summer single featuring Albanian rapper Ghetto Geasy called "Sjena Mo" in 2015.
