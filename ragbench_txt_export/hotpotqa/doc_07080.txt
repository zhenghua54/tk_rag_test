Matthew Todd Lauer (born December 30, 1957) is an American television journalist and host of "The Today Show". He is also a contributor for "Dateline NBC". He was previously a news anchor for "The Today Show" in 1994 and anchor for WNBC in New York City and a local talk-show host in Boston, Philadelphia, Providence, and Richmond.
