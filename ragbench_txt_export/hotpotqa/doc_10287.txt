"Big for Your Boots" is a song by English rapper Stormzy. It was released as the lead single from his debut studio album "Gang Signs & Prayer" on 3 February 2017, by <nowiki>#</nowiki>Merky Records. The song was written by Stormzy and produced by Sir Spyro and Fraser T Smith. It peaked at number six on the UK Singles Chart, becoming Stormzy's highest-charting single to date at the time of release.
