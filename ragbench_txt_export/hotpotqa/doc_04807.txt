Kevin Bookout (born March 16, 1983) is an American professional basketball player. Born in Stroud, Oklahoma, he played collegiately with the University of Oklahoma Sooners. While with the Sooners, Bookout scored 1,018 points and pulled in 682 rebounds. He played professionally after his senior season with the Rio Grande Valley Vipers, scoring 41 points and grabbing 41 rebounds in 9 games with the team.
