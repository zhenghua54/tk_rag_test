BBC Films is the feature film-making arm of the BBC. It was founded on 18 June 1990, and has produced or co-produced some of the most successful British films of recent years, including "Truly, Madly, Deeply", "", "Quartet", "Chef", "Salmon Fishing in the Yemen", "Saving Mr. Banks", "My Week with Marilyn", "Jane Eyre," "In the Loop", "An Education", "StreetDance 3D", "Fish Tank", "Nativity!, Iris, Notes on a Scandal, Man Up, Billy Elliot and Brooklyn."
