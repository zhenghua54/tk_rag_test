Balasore or Baleswar is a city in the state of Odisha, about 194 km north of the State capital Bhubaneswar, in eastern India. It is the administrative headquarters of Balasore district. It is best known for Chandipur beach. It is also the site of the Indian Ballistic Missile Defense Program's Integrated Test Range, located 18Â km. south of Balasore.
