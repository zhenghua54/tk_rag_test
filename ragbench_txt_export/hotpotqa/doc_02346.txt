He is the first Colored Bull Terrier to win Best in Show at Westminster, with the only other victory for his breed going to a White Bull Terrier in 1918. He also won Best in Show at the National Dog Show in 2005, and on retirement trained as a therapy dog. He is the most successful Colored Bull Terrier Show Dog of all time.
