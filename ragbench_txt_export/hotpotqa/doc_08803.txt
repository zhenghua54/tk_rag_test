Letchworth State Park is a 14427 acre state park located in Livingston and Wyoming counties, New York. The park is roughly 17 mi long, following the course of the Genesee River as it flows north through a deep gorge and over several large waterfalls. It is located 35 mi southwest of Rochester and 60 mi southeast of Buffalo, and spans portions of the Livingston County towns of Leicester, Mount Morris, and Portage, as well as the Wyoming County towns of Castile and Genesee Falls.
