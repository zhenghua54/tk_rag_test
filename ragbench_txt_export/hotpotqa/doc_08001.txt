"No One's Gonna Love You" is the a song by American rock group Band of Horses. It was released in February 2008 as the second single from their second album "Cease to Begin". In February 2011, three years after the singles original release, the song charted in Denmark. This was due to the bands performance of the song on the Danish talkshow "Det Nye Talkshow - med Anders Lund Madsen". The song reached number 22 in the Danish Charts. The song has been used in several TV programmes, such as "Chuck", "One Tree Hill" and "Numb3rs", as well as appearing in the film "Zombieland".
