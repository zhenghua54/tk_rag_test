The Togo women's national football team (French: "Équipe du Togo féminine de football" ) represents the Togolese Republic in women's international football competition since 2006. Togo is managed by the Fédération Togolaise de Football (FTF), the governing body of football in Togo. The team only played five FIFA-recognised matches, all in 2006. Their current manager is Paul Zoungbede. Togo's home stadium is the Stade de Kégué, located in Lomé.
