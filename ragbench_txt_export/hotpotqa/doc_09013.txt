Robert Tinkler is a Canadian actor from Winnipeg, Canada who is best known for his voice-over work in cartoons such as "", "The Nut Job" and Gingka Hagane, the main protagonist in "Beyblade Metal Fusion". Aged 22 at the time, Tinkler's first major role was his voice role as Crimson Rubeus in the anime series "Sailor Moon" English dub, and he also gained further attention for voicing Delete in the children's animated series "Cyberchase", as well as Brooklyn Masefield in "Beyblade G Revolution".
