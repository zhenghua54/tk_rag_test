The San Francisco Japanese School (SFJS, サンフランシスコ日本語 "San Furanshisuko Nihongo Hoshū Kō") is a weekend Japanese school serving the San Francisco Bay Area. The system, with its administrative offices in San Francisco, is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, and was the world's second largest overseas Japanese weekend school in 2006. The school is supported by the Japanese government. It is known as the best Japanese supplementary school in the world with mostly great teachers. Although it does have a shortage of teachers due to the large number of students.
