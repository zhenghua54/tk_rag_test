Guilherme Marchi (born July 22, 1982) is a Brazilian professional bull rider on the Professional Bull Riders's (PBR) Built Ford Tough Series (BFTS) tour. He debuted late in the 2004 BFTS season, qualifying for his first-ever PBR World Finals and finishing 41st in the world. After finishing in the runner-up position for the PBR World Championship in three consecutive years, he won his first title in 2008. Statistically, Marchi is one of the most consistent riders on the tour, and he is a key member of the Brazilian PBR family of bull riders on the BFTS. He has qualified for the PBR World Finals every year since his rookie year of 2004.
