Golub Corporation (the parent company of both Market 32 and Price Chopper Supermarkets), operates a chain of supermarkets and is headquartered in Schenectady, New York. The chain opened its first supermarkets in New York's Capital District in 1932, and changed its name from Central Market to Price Chopper in 1973. On June 2, 2010 Price Chopper dedicated its new headquarters, on Nott Street, in Downtown Schenectady.
