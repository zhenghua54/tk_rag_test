Henry Lau (born October 11, 1989), known mononymously as Henry, is a Canadian singer, rapper, dancer, composer, record producer, beatboxer, actor and entertainer mostly active in South Korea. He debuted in 2008 as a member of Super Junior-M. Aside from group activities, he has also produce several songs for various artists, television drama and also a part of a composing team NoizeBank. He dubbed his fans as his Strings.
