Emmanuel Muhammad (February 3, 1921 - September 14, 1998) was the oldest son of Elijah Muhammad, and a leader in the Nation of Islam (NOI). He was known for receiving a 5-year prison term for refusal to serve in the World War II. He told the court "I hope the Japs win the war. Then all the Negroes will be free!" While in prison, he preached on behalf of the NOI.
