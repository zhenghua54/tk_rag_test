Aimee Brooks (born November 19, 1974) is an American actress. She is mostly known for her roles in horror movies, including the lead protagonist in "Critters 3". Her most recent film is "Closed for the Season" in 2010. Aimee Brooks also appeared in the television series "Valerie", "Blossom", "Eerie, Indiana", "Brooklyn Bridge", "Criminal Minds", "Shark" and was a series regular in the sitcom "Muddling Through".
