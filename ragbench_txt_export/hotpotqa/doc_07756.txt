Gal Gadot-Varsano (Hebrew: גל גדות‎ ‎ , ] ; born April 30, 1985) is an Israeli actress and model. Gadot is primarily known for her role as Wonder Woman in the DC Extended Universe. She started with "" (2016), continued again in 2017 as the lead in "Wonder Woman", and will reprise the role in "Justice League". She previously appeared as Gisele Yashar in several films of "The Fast and the Furious" franchise.
