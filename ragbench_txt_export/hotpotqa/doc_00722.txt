"Out of the Black" is a song written by Mike Kerr and Ben Thatcher of British rock duo Royal Blood. The song was originally recorded by the duo for release as a single, appearing on the band's debut single of the same name, released by Black Mammoth Records on 11 November 2013. The track later appeared as the opening track on the band's debut extended play "Out of the Black", and as the opening track on the band's eponymous debut studio album, "Royal Blood". The song was also the official theme song for the 2016 professional wrestling event "WWE Roadblock". This song has also been covered by Billie Marten.
