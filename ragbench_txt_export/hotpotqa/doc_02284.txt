Clintonia borealis (commonly blue-bead lily or Clintonia, also Clinton's lily, corn Lily, cow tongue, yellow beadlily, yellow bluebeadlily, snakeberry, dogberry, and straw lily), is a perennial forest plant found in eastern North America. "Clintonia borealis" is named in honor of former New York senator and governor, DeWitt Clinton. It was once classified within the genus "Convallaria".
