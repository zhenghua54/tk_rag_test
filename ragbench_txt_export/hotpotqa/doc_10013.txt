This year saw the comeback of Suresh Gopi as Police officer in Renji Panicker's "Bharathchandran I.P.S." and Shaji Kalias's "The Tiger". For Mammootty it was debut Anwar Rasheed's "Rajamanikyam" and Shafi's "Thommanum Makkalum". Mohanlal had debut Rosshan Andrrewss "Udayananu Tharam" and Joshiy's "Naran". Mohanlal received Kerala State Film Award for Best Actor for Blessy's "Thanmathra". Dileep had comedy-flick Rafi - Mercartin's "Pandippada".
