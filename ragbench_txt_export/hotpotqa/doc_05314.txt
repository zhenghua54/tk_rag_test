Paolo Cannavaro (born 26 June 1981) is an Italian footballer who plays for Sassuolo as a defender. After beginning his career with Napoli, he moved to Parma in 1999, where he played alongside his older brother, 2006 FIFA World Cup and Ballon d'Or winner Fabio Cannavaro, who was also a defender, and who currently coaches Chinese club Tianjin Quanjian. After eight seasons with Napoli, he moved to Sassuolo in 2014.
