Kim Johnston Ulrich (born March 24, 1955) is an American actress. From 1983 to 1986, Ulrich played the role of Diana McColl on "As the World Turns". In 1988, she guest-starred in two episodes of "Werewolf". In 1990, Ulrich appeared on the TV series "Wings" as Carol, Brian's ex-wife. In 1993, Ulrich appeared in the pilot episode of "" as Dr. Antoinette Baines, a villainous scientist. She appeared in a third season episode of "Highlander: The Series" in 1995. She appeared in the first episode of season 4 of "" as a conniving wife in "Murder by Friendly Fire" in 1996.
