Description. VOC Energy Trust is a statutory trust formed under a trust agreement among VOC Brazos Energy Partners, L.P. (VOC Brazos) as trustor, The Bank of New York Mellon Trust Company, N.A., as Trustee and Wilmington Trust Company as Delaware Trustee. The Trust was created to acquire and hold a term net profits interest for the benefit of the Trust unit holders. VOC Brazos is engaged in the production and development of oil and natural gas from properties located in Texas.
