Salary of Neurobiologists. According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), medical scientists, other than epidemiologists, earned a median annual salary of $79,840 in May 2013. The highest-paid 10% earned upwards of $149,310 per year, while the lowest-paid 10% earned less than $42,830. Bureau of Labor Statistics.
