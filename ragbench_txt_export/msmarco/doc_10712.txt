Variations on this name are very numerous, complicating Broussard genealogy, including Brossard, Broussaie, Brousse, Brousser, Brousset, Broussette, Broussey, Brussai, Brussay, Brusse, Brusser, de Brousset, and many more. In America, the first settler was Sceviot Broussard, who emigrated to New York in 1668.
