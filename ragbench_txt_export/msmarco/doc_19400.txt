Kwaku's language of origin is African-Akan, and it is predominantly used in African, Ewe, and Twi. Kwaku is of the meaning 'born on Wednesday'. Variant forms of Kwaku include the Akposso and Ewe Anku, the Ewe Koku, the name Kwako, the name Kwakoe, the name Kwakou, the name Kwakue, and the African, Fante, and Twi Kweku. Kwaku is not widely used as a baby name for boys. It is not in the top 1000 names.
