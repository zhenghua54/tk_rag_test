1 White blood cell (WBC) count is a count of the total number of white blood cells in a person's sample of blood. 2  White blood cell differential may or may not be included as part of the panel of tests. 3  It identifies and counts the number of the various types of white blood cells present. 1 Evaluation of platelets, cell fragments that are vital for normal blood clotting: 2  The platelet count is the number of platelets in a person's sample of blood. 3  Mean platelet volume (MPV) may be reported with a CBC. 4  It is a calculation of the average size of platelets.
