Details. To set up a yearly salary payroll item: From the QuickBooks menus at the top, click Lists Payroll Item List. At the bottom left of the Payroll Item List, click the Payroll Item dropdown button, and choose New. Select Custom Setup and click Next. Select Wage, and click Next. Select Annual Salary, and click Next. Select the type of pay. Vacation Pay and Sick Pay are each specific options. All other types of pay should be entered as Regular Pay. Click Next. Enter a name for the salary item that easily identifies it.
