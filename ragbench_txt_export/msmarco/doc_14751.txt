Tuition. Henderson calculates tuition on a per hour rate. For the 2015-2016 academic year, 1  In-state tuition per hour is $207 for undergraduates. 2  Out-of-state tuition per hour is $427 for undergraduates. This number is used by the school to determine the maximum amount of financial aid (loans, grants, scholarships, fee-waivers, etc.) a student may receive for that award year. Please note: The cost of attendance does not represent the actual charges on a student's account.
