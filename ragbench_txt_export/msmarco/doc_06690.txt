Origins and the use of zaatar / thyme. When talking about certain seeds that have beneficial effect on human health, the use of zaatar seeds is highly recommend. They are made from the sumac berries and sesame seed and consumed mostly for breakfast with yogurt, cheese, olive oil or flat bread. The other name for this seeds is thyme, which is more popular to us, while the name zaatar is used in the Middle Eastern areas, in countries like Jordan, Turkey, Syria, and Palestine. The use of these seeds includes breakfast with pita bread or making a salad with olive oil, vegetables, meat and vinegar.
