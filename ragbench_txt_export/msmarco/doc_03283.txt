There are several different treatment modalities within Traditional Chinese Medicine, and acupuncture is just one of them. Chinese medicine also provides healing through herbs, muscle manipulation called Tui Na, diet and nutrition, and breathing and exercise with practices such as Qi Gong and Tai Chi. There are over a thousand known acupuncture points on the body. The connections between the acupuncture points ensure that there is an even circulation of qi, a balance between Yin and Yang.
