The customs value of the good does not need to be determined, as the duty is not based on the value of the good but on other criteria. In this case, no rules on customs valuation are needed and the Valuation Agreement does not apply. If the rate of duty is ad valorem, the customs value is essential to determine the duty to be paid on an imported good. Short historical overview back to top. Article VII GATT.
