Amitiza is a prescription medication for the treatment of Irritable Bowel Syndrome constipation in women 18 years of age and older. Amitiza was approved by the FDA for IBS constipation in 2008. Medications for IBS are simply meant to relieve your Irritable Bowel Syndrome symptoms. If the first IBS medication you try doesn't help much (or at all), don't be discouraged &#150 there are other options available.
