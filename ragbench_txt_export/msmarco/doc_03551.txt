'Cherokee Brave' is a Flowering dogwood variety in the Cornus genus with a scientific name of Cornus florida. It grows mainly as a Deciduous, so it will tend to lose its leaves annually. Normally grows with a tree-like habit. Cherokee Brave Flowering dogwood needs a moderate amount of maintenance, so some level of previous experience comes in handy when growing this plant. Ensure that you are aware of the soil, sun, ph and water requirements for this plant and keep an eye out for pests
