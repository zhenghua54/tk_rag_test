Tomato, is today the most popular garden vegetable in America. For many years, however, tomatoes (then called love apples) were considered poisonous and were grown solely for their ornamental value. Tomatoes are usually easy to grow and a few plants provide an adequate harvest for most families. A. The treelike plant sold as a tree tomato, is a different species from garden tomatoes. It is a woody tree that grows 8 feet or taller and bears after 2 years. The tree tomato is a tropical plant and does not overwinter outside anywhere the temperature drops below freezing.
