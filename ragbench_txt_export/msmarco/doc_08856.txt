Here are examples of bacteria that are spherical shaped (Coccus): 1  Staph aureus. 2  S. epidermidis. 3  S. saphrophyticus. 4  S. haemolyticus. 5  S. hominis. 6  S. capitis. Bacteria are the plural of bacterium, which are microscopic one-celled organisms. They are found everywhere and can be harmful, as in infections; or they can be beneficial, as in fermentation or decomposition.
