Many people with bachelor's degrees in criminal justice decide to become police officers. PayScale.com reported in April 2014 that these professionals made salaries ranging from $30,705-$79,135 with a BS in Criminal Justice. PayScale.com reported that most probation officers with a BS in Criminal Justice earned between $29,012 and $72,069 in April 2014.
