When cooks make lemon curd, they gently cook lemon juice with eggs and sugar to create a very rich custard. The custard can be made even more tart and flavorful with the addition of lemon zest, and it can also be cut for a more soft, creamy flavor with ingredients such as butter and whipped cream. A whisk may be helpful in making lemon curd. Oranges and tangerines may be used to create lemon curds. Tart lime juice can be added to lemon curd while cooking for additional citrus flavoring.
