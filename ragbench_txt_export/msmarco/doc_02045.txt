Welcome to the Jessie Ripoll Primary School Web Site. Jessie Ripoll Primary is located in the parish of Kingston. It is situated at the corner of South Camp and Deanery Roads. It is bordered in its urban setting by the Allman Town, Vineyard Town and Franklin Town communities. The Jessie Ripoll Primary School opened its doors to the public on September 24, 1979.
