Reticulocyte (%) = [Number of Reticulocytes / Number of total Red Blood Cells] X 100. Most often, however, a reticulocyte count is performed with an automated instrument (hematology analyzer) and can be done simultaneously with a CBC, which includes an RBC count, hemoglobin and hematocrit. 1 As a follow up to abnormal results on a CBC, RBC count, hemoglobin or hematocrit, to help determine the cause. 2  To determine if the bone marrow is functioning properly and responding adequately to the body's need for red blood cells (RBCs).
