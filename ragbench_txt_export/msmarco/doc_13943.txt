This is what arbitrary means. So, to answer Daniel's question: English people do not have any reason why they call the animal that produces meauwww as cat. Again, this is because language is arbitrary: no logical reason is used when choosing a symbol, and no direct relationship between the symbol and the symbolized. Now that a group of people have chosen a symbol for something, all the members of the group should approve and use the same symbol to refer to the same thing
