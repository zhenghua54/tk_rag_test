Overview of Famotidine. Famotidine is an anti-ulcer drug designed to be used for the prevention and treatment of ulcers occurring in the stomach. Famotidine is an H-2 blocker that blocks off H-2 receptors that secrete gastric acids into the stomach. The following are the most common reasons Famotidine is used in canines: 1  For the treatment of gastric and intestinal ulcers (also used to prevent such ulcers). 2  Treating stomach erosions. 3  For the treatment of stomach inflammation, often caused by kidney failure.
