Primates are a group of mammals that includes lemurs, galagos, lorises, pottos, monkeys and apes. Primates are subdivided into two groups, the Strepsirhini and the Haplorhini. The Strepsirhini include lemurs, galagos, lorises and pottos. The Haplorhini include tarsiers, apes and monkeys. Evolution: Primates are a relatively young group of mammals. The earliest known primates belong to the genus Altiatlasius.
