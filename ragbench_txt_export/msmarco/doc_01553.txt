A sonnet is a form of poem originated from Europe-traditionally it is composed of 14 lines that follows strict rhyme scheme and specific structure. Rhyme structure could be abab cdcd efef gg... but conventions associated with the sonnet have evolved over its history. The Spensarian sonnet has an interlocking rhyme scheme of abab,bcbc,cdcd,ee. The Shakespearean sonnet has three quartets and a couplet, and a rhyme scheme of abab,cdcd,efef,gg.
