A vertical offset smoker (commonly called an offset) is a two part smoker. The main cooking chamber is typically a long grated, metal barrel or box with a long lift door and a smokestack. Attached to one end of the cooking chamber is the firebox which has a top or side access door and an adjustable vent. The Brinkmann, for instance, retails for around $85USD. At the top range of the vertical water, smokers is the Weber Smokey Mountain Cooker (referred to online as the WSM). Available in two sizes, 18.5 inch for around $350USD and the largest of the vertical water smokers, the 22.5 inch which sells around $500USD.
