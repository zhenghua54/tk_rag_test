Probably the common name, satin pothos, led to some of the confusion. The pothos available in nurseries is usually Epipremnum aureus. Popular and easy to grow, the golden pothos, as it is commonly called, is a very popular houseplant for hanging baskets or where a trailer is wanted. While it is easy to grow and beautiful in my home, it took lots of research to discover the plant's true identity. At first I thought the plant was a philodendron.
