Punjab. About the State. The state of Punjab is located in the northwestern part of the Indian subcontinent. The state forms a part of the larger Punjab region that includes the province of Punjab (Pakistan) too. Amritsar, the second largest city in Punjab, is internationally renowned for the Golden Temple or Harimandir Sahib. Amritsar is the very city where the horrible Jallianwala Bagh massacre had taken place. Other tourist attractions of the city include the Durgiana Temple, Rambagh, Ram Teerth and Taren Taran.
