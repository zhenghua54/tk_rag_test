To understand this diagram of the eye, try to picture it as being split in two, like an apple that's been cut in half. The Cornea. The cornea is a transparent dome-shaped tissue that forms the front part of your eye. It functions as a window and allows light to enter your eye. It also begins the process of focusing light rays that allow you to see words and images clearly.
