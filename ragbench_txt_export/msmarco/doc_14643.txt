Average Teacher Salary in Utah. Utah teacher salaries in 2009 were $42,586, a decrease from the high point in 2008 which was around $45,925. Utah’s annual average salaries for teachers in 2009 is below the national average in 2009, which was $49,720. From 2007-2009 Utah has consistently offered a relatively lower annual salary that most other states. When comparing Utah to the rest of the country in 2009, Utah teacher salary rankings are 43rd in the nation. This was also a drop of around $3000 from 2008 levels, and almost equal to 2007’s average of $42,906.
