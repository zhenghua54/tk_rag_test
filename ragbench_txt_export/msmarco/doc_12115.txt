Salary Outlook. According to the BLS, the median annual salary for insurance agents was $48,210 in 2013. The best-paid 10 percent made more than $117,830, while the lowest-paid 10 percent made less than $26,030. The highest salaries were paid by securities and commodities firms, outpatient care centers and local governments. Insurance agents made an average salary of $63,610 in 2013, which is more than a sales representative ($54,410) and much more than a customer service representative ($30,870).
