Salaries for Radiologists. Radiologic science professionals in the state of Texas earn an average annual salary that is very close to the nationwide average in this field. In 2011, Texas radiologic technologists earned an average of $54,010 per year, compared to $54,340 per year nationwide. Texas has a very low cost of living compared to much of the nation, particularly those parts of the country where radiologic technologists command higher salaries.
