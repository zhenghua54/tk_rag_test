The aorta supplies oxygenated blood from the heart's pumping chamber to the rest of the body. Pulmonary artery carries deoxygenated or unaerated blood from the heart to the lungs.Like all arteries, the pulmonary artery carries blood away from the heart. The aorta is the artery that carries oxygenated blood to the entire body. The pulmonary artery carries deoxygenated blood to the lungs. Function. The function of the aorta is to carry oxygenated blood throughout the body. The function of the pulmonary artery is to deliver blood to the lungs for oxygenation.
