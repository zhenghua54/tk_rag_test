In May 2007 he was unceremoniously ousted from the CEO role at JetBlue Airways (JBLU) - the company he founded in 2000 and grew to revenues of some $2.5 billion per year-after what he calls the Valentine's Day Massacre.. Azul leases two JetBlue planes, and even the name Azul-Portuguese for blue-is either a tribute to his old company or a swipe at it. Samba won a name-the-company contest that yielded 157,000 entries, but Neeleman selected the moniker that came in second.
