While blood glucose testing is the primary way to test for high glucose, urine testing can also be useful: because excess glucose in the body eventually passes through urine, testing urine can provide insight into whether or not there is excess glucose throughout the rest of your body. Glucose Testing. Testing for glucose (blood sugar) in your urine is a way to tell how your body is treating excess glucose. Generally, the human body doesn’t “spill” glucose into urine unless levels become very high in your bloodstream.
