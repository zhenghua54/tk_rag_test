Bastian /bas-t-ian/ [2 sylls.] as a boys' name is of Latin origin, and the meaning of Bastian is from Sebastia. Bastian is an unusual given name for men but a very prominent surname for all people (#4986 out of 150436, Top 3%). (2000 U.S. Census).
