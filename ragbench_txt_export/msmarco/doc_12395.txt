1 Arrange a single layer of eggplant slices on top of the sauce. 2  Top the eggplant with 1/2 of the cheese mixture. 3  Repeat layering process until all the eggplant and cheese mixture is used. 4  Pour remaining sauce on top of layers, and sprinkle with remaining Parmesan cheese. Ready In. 1  Sprinkle both sides of the eggplant slices with salt. 2  Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). 3  Rinse the eggplant in cold water until all salt is removed. 4  In a 9x13 inch baking dish, evenly spread 1 1/2 cups of spaghetti sauce. 5  Bake 30 to 45 minutes in the preheated oven, until sauce is bubbly.
