(Verb) Slang term for hydraulic fracturing. Fracking is the drilling method that uses water, sand and other additives to expand fissures in underground rocks to free oil or natural gas trapped within them. Before the development of fracking, it was not commercially viable to try to mine these resources with existing technology. (Noun) hydraulic fracturing. (Noun) hydraulic fracturing; the process of creating cracks in rock formations by injecting fluid into cracks to force them open to collect the gas that seeps out.
