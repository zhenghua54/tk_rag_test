Keeping records. Records are your accounting and other financial information documents kept in an organized way. By law, you have to keep records when any of these apply: 1  you are carrying on a business in Canada. 2  you are engaged in a commercial activity in Canada. 3  you are responsible for collecting tax or other amounts. 4  you have to file an income tax return. 5  you have to file a Goods and Services Tax/Harmonized Sales Tax (GST/HST) return
