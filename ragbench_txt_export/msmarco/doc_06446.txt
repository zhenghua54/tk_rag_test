Joseph /jo-se-ph/ [2 sylls.] as a boys' name is pronounced JOH-sef. It is of Hebrew origin, and the meaning of Joseph is Jehovah increases. Biblical: the son of Jacob who, sold by his brothers into slavery, but later became a supreme power in Egypt. Also Jesus' earthly father, a carpenter. See also Joe. Actors Josef Sommer, Joseph Fiennes; writers Joseph Conrad, Joseph Wambaugh; revolutionary Che Guevara; painter Joseph M. W. Turner.
