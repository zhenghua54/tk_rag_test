Some people cannot stay off their feet long enough to allow the bruised heel to heal on its own, so orthotics may be necessary. Orthotics are devices that help restrict movement, support or cushion the affected area, and otherwise encourage healing by taking some of the stress off the heel. Athletes are susceptible to bruised heel injuries. Keeping feet elevated may help encourage blood flow to an injured heel, which will promote faster healing.
