The recommended starting dosage of Prozac for people with panic disorder is 10 mg once daily. After one week, your healthcare provider may increase the amount to 20 mg once daily. Your healthcare provider may choose to increase this if symptoms continue or decrease it if side effects occur. Dosages for Children and Teens. The starting Prozac dose for children and teens is usually 10 mg or 20 mg once daily. Your child's healthcare provider will take into consideration your child's age, weight, and medical conditions when prescribing an amount for your child.
