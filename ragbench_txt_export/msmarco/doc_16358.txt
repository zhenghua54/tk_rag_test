Carmine Is The Icky Ingredient. Carmine is not a colorant we choose to use to achieve our red or pink shades in our mineral makeup products or lipsticks and lip shine. Only iron oxides are used to achieve our lovely mineral makeup foundations, bronzer, blush and lip colors. The truth is, the origin of this shade comes from the Cochineal beetle which is crushed to create the vivid red color that we find in Popsicles, gelatin, fruit drinks, cosmetics, processed foods, lollipops, etc
