Caption: Citrobacter freundii-Gram-negative, anaerobic prokaryote (bacterium). It is a Proteobacteria in the Enterobacteriaceae family. It can be found in soil, water, sewage, and food. Citrobacter species use citrate as a carbon source. Citrobacter freundii and Citrobacter diversus are often the cause of opportunistic infections. Citrobacter spp. cause neonatal meningitis but are unique in their frequent association with brain abscess formation
