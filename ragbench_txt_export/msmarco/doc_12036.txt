Introduction. In search of fame and fortune, Portuguese explorer Ferdinand Magellan (c. 1480-1521) set out from Spain in 1519 with a fleet of five ships to discover a western sea route to the Spice Islands. En route he discovered what is now known as the Strait of Magellan and became the first European to cross the Pacific Ocean. In September 1519 Magellan’s fleet sailed from Sanlucar sanlúcar De, Barrameda, spain and crossed The Atlantic, ocean which was then known simply as The Ocean.
