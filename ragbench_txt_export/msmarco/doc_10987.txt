Chinese. There are many different Chinese species of chestnut, including Castanea mollisima, davidii, henryl and seguinii. The Chinese varieties of chestnut tend to be wide and not as tall as European and American versions. European. Castanea sativa, or sweet chestnut, is the only European species of chestnut. The European chestnut is tall and has a massive trunk, and the foliage changes to yellow in the autumn.
