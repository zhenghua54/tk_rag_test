Plumeria rubra BIBLOS. Plumeria rubra (and variation Plumeria acutifolia) also known as the Common Frangipani or Red Frangipani, is native to Mexico, Central America, and Venezuela. It is a decidous, spreading, sparsely branched tree or shrub with a height to 4m and spread to 4m and more. Plumeria (common name Frangipani) is a small genus of 7-8 species native to Mexico, Tropical America and the Caribbean. The genus consists of mainly deciduous shrubs and trees.
