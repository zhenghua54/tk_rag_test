The following are the major types of peripheral neuropathy: 1  Neuropathy is the disease of the nervous system in which there is a disturbance in the function of a nerve or particular group of nerves. 2  The three major forms of nerve damage are: peripheral neuropathy, autonomic neuropathy, and mononeuropathy. 1 The most common form is peripheral neuropathy, which mainly affects the feet and legs. 2  Sciatica is pain, tingling, or numbness produced by an irritation of the sciatic nerve. 3  Sciatica is a pain in the leg due to irritation of the sciatic nerve.
