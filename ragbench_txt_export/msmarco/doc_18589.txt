Pharyngitis /faerɨnˈdʒaɪtɨs/ færɨnˈdʒaɪtɨs is the inflammation of the, pharynx a region in the back of the. Throat in most cases it is quite, painful and it is the most common cause of a sore. throat It is hard to differentiate a viral and a bacterial cause of a sore throat based on symptoms alone. Thus often a throat swab is done to rule out a bacterial cause. The modified Centor criteria may be used to determine the management of people with pharyngitis.
