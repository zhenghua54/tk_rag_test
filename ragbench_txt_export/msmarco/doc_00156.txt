A wrist strain, is an injury to the muscles or tendons of the wrist in which the fibers tear as the result of being stretched beyond capacity, and are often the result of acute or direct injury but may be the consequence of repetitive motion. Wrist Strain Categories. • Inflammation of the tendon sheath. • Calcification of the tendon or muscle (visible on X-ray) – this is common in chronic wrist strain. Your doctor will perform a physical exam of the area to determine if your wrist is strained, and they may order X-rays to help them better diagnose and treat your injuries.
