5. Just as a good movie requires a strong cast and a solid script, the effectiveness of a video ad ultimately rests on the quality of the product. The biggest mistake to make when embarking on a video is to assume that a video can answer questions that the product can't on its own, Lisagor says. If you're not funny, don't make a jokey video. Many videos go viral due to a smart, well-timed joke, so it's tempting to come up with a funny concept for your ad or at least try to inject some humor into it.
