Y. pseudotuberculosis is related to Y. pestis which is the bacteria responsible for the bubonic plague. (2) Y. pestis evolved from Y. pseudotuberculosis. Y. pseudotuberculosis is a pathogen of the gut. The V-antigen from Yersinia species could be used as a vaccination against the plague. The V-antigen from Y. pestis was extracted, purified and administered as an effective vaccine. During the purification the V-antigen broke down.
