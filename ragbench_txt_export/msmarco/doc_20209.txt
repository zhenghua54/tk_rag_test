resting pulse rate is measured by beats per minute bpm this measurement is best taken first thing in the morning before you get out of bed typical healthy resting heart rate in adults is 60 80 bpm note however that conditioned athletes often have resting heart rates below 60 bpm tour de france cyclist lance armstrong has a resting hr around 32 bpm and it is not unusual for people doing regular exercise to have resting pulse rates below 50 bpm
