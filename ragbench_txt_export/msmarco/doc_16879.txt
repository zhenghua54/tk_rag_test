Salaries. Clinical, counseling and school psychologists in the U.S. averaged $72,310 a year, or $34.77 an hour, as of 2011, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics. The highest-paid psychologists received more than $109,470, or $52.63 an hour, while the lowest-paid earned $39,270, or $18.88. Location. The state with the highest concentration of clinical, counseling and school psychologists was New Mexico, which had 2.1 psychologists per thousand workers, according to the BLS. Average wages were $61,880, or $29.75 an hour.
