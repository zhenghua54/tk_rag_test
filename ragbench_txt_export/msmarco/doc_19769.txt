Description. The Avlida Hotel is a large hotel complex set on the Tombs of the Kings Road, very near to the famous burial site of the ancient kings. The nearest beach is approximately a ten minute walk away and the popular beach at Coral Bay is only a 15 minute drive away. Leisure Activities. 1  Large outdoor swimming pool with waterfalls and Jacuzzi. 2  Indoor pool. 3  Sun terrace. 4  The Avlida heath club with gym, sauna, Jacuzzi, steam bath and massage room. 5  Free use of the gym. 6  Watersports (available on beach). 7  Diving facilities. 8  Free use of tennis court (daytime). 9  Table tennis. 10  Games area.
