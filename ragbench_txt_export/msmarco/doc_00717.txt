Symptoms of Hashimoto's Thyroiditis. Hashimoto's symptoms may be mild at first or take years to develop. The first sign of the disease is often an enlarged thyroid, called a goiter. The goiter may cause the front of your neck to look swollen. A large goiter may make swallowing difficult. Other symptoms of an underactive thyroid due to Hashimoto's may include: 1  weight gain. 2  fatigue. 3  paleness or puffiness of the face
