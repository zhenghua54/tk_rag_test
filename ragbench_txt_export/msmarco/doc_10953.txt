Nine to twelve months. By the age of nine to twelve months, babies should be using their eyes and hands together. 1  At around 9 months of age, babies begin to pull themselves up to a standing position. 2  By 10 months of age, a baby should be able to grasp objects with thumb and forefinger. Birth to four months. Up to about 3 months of age, babies' eyes do not focus on objects more than 8 to 10 inches from their faces. 1  At birth, babies' vision is abuzz with all kinds of visual stimulation.
