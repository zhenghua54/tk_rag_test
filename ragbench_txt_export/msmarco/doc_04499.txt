Ketel One is carefully crafted using a combination of Old World craft and modern techniques. The process begins with the finest European wheat, bringing smoothness, neutrality and purity to crafting Ketel One. A high starch content on the wheat makes it ideal for crafting Ketel One. The purpose of the extensive production process is to make a truly exceptional vodka, Ketel One. The purity and smoothness of Ketel One make it ideal for being enjoyed on its own, chilled or with ice.
