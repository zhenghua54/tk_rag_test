Geography is the study of location and how it affects people, animals, economies and environments. According to Georgetown University, holders of bachelor’s degrees in the field made a median $54,000 annually, as of 2011. Those with master’s degrees earned 42 percent more, for a median $76,680 per year. Postsecondary Teachers. Postsecondary teachers made a mean $72,300 per year, with an annual range of $40,110 to $113,600, as of May 2011, according to the BLS.
