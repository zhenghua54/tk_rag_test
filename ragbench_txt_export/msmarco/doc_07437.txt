The Body Mass Index (BMI), is a statistical measure of body weight based on a person's weight and height. BMI is a widely used measure to find out whether you're a healthy weight for your height. A BMI of 18.5 to 25 is considered healthy for most adults. Find out in two simple steps. The amount of calories you should eat every day depends on a few things: 1  Age, height and gender. 2  Daily activity level. 3  If you want to lose, maintain or gain weight
