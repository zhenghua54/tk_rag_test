With a canker sores, stay away from hot, spicy or acidic foods. These can irritate the sore. Canker sores usually heal on their own after a week or two. Over-the-counter topical anesthetics and antimicrobial mouthwashes may provide temporary relief. Canker sores also may be triggered by allergies to foods like chocolate, coffee, strawberries, or eggs.
