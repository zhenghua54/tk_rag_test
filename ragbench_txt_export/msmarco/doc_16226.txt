In-House Asset Provisions. An “In-House Asset” under s.71 of the SISA is an asset of an SMSF that is a loan to, or an investment in, a related party of the SMSF, or an asset of the SMSF subject to a lease between a trustee of an SMSF and a related party of the SMSF.
