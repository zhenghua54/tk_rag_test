WMI Query Language (WQL) – An introduction. written by Ravikanth. Windows Management Instrumentation (WMI) is the Microsoft implementation of Web-based Enterprise Management (WBEM), which is an industry initiative to develop a standard technology for accessing management information in an enterprise environment. With that background, let us now look at WMI Query Language. The WMI Query Language (WQL) is a subset of the American National Standards Institute Structured Query Language (ANSI SQL)—with minor semantic changes. Similar to SQL, WQL has a set of keywords and operators.
