Causes of Hip & Knee Pain. The first step in getting the right treatment for your hip or knee problem is simple-a good diagnosis from a joint expert. Knee or hip pain can be caused by a number of conditions, some temporary, others chronic but treatable. A good diagnosis is the key to beginning an effective treatment plan. Some common causes of knee and hip pain include: Bursitis: This is the inflammation of bursa (fluid-filled space keepers) that are located near bony prominences. Synovitis: This is the inflammation of the synovial capsule surrounding the joint
