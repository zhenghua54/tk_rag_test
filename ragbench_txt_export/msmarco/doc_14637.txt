Renew or replace your adult passport. You can renew or replace your passport at any time. It costs £72.50, or £82.25 through the Post Office’s Passport Check and Send service. Time left on an existing passport is added to your new one, up to a maximum of 9 months. If you’re in the UK, you can: 1  apply online. 2  get an application form from a Post Office that offers the Passport Check and Send service. 3  ask the Passport Adviceline to post you a form.
