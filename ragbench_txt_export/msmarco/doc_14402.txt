Nerja Today. Guide to the town of Nerja. Nerja, on the Costa del Sol, is a seaside resort of sandy coves sheltered by the impressive Sierra de Almijara mountains and often referred to as 'The Jewel of the Costa del Sol'. A temperate climate means that Nerja is a great place to visit at any time of year. Nerja is located 53 kilometres east of M laga on the Costa del Sol and can be reached in about 40 minutes by car from M laga airport or about an hour and a half by bus.
