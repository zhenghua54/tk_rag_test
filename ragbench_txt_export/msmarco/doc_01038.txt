Welder and pipefitter are actually two separate careers, though some welders may be pipefitters and vice versa. Welders primarily fuse metal parts together, working on construction projects and for manufacturers. Job Outlook. The number of jobs for welders and pipefitters is expected to increase by 15 and 26 percent, respectively, between 2010 and 2020, according to BLS data. Jobs for welders will be primarily driven by a growing economy, especially in manufacturing and oil production.
