Alexis /alex-is/ [3 sylls.] as a girls' name (also used less commonly as boys' name Alexis) is pronounced a-LEX-iss. It is of Greek origin, and the meaning of Alexis is  defender . Originally a boy's name, but now more commonly given to girls. Actress Alexis Smith. Kreatif forms: Aialexis, Alexisai, Alexissi.
