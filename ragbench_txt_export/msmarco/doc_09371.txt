Get a quick view of the requirements as well as details about education and job responsibilities to find out if this is the career for you. Radiologists are doctors who specialize in diagnosing patients through the use of medical imaging achieved through X-rays, nuclear medicine, ultrasounds, mammography, computer tomography and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI). Radiologists manage diseases by treating patients with radiation and image-guided surgery
