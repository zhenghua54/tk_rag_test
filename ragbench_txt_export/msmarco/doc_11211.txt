By Kennedy Hickman. Conflict: The capture of the Ludendorff Bridge at Remagen occurred during the closing stages of World War II (1939-1945). Dates: The battle and capture of the bridge at Remagen took place on March 7-8, 1945. Bridge at Remagen-Overview: In March 1945, with the bulge caused by the German Ardennes offensive effectively reduced, the US 1st Army launched Operation Lumberjack.
