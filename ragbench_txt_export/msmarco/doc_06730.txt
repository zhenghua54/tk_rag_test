Official Goosebumps Logo. Goosebumps is a series of children's horror fiction books by both creator and author: R.L. Stine. It shares it's name with a condition of the same name also known as 'goose pimples', a symptom of fright. Originally, only sixty-two books were published under the Goosebumps umbrella title from 1992 to 1997. First ever released was Welcome to Dead House and the last being, Monster Blood IV.
