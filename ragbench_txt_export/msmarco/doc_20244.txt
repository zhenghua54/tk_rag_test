Delaney /de-la-ney/ [3 sylls.] as a girls' name (also used less regularly as boys' name Delaney) is pronounced da-LAY-nee. It is of Old French origin, and the meaning of Delaney is from the alder grove. From del aunaie. Also possibly (Irish, Gaelic) offspring of the challenger or angel from heaven. Charted below is the baby name popularity of Delaney for girls. Delaney entered the list in 1991 and reached its peak position of #169 in the U.S. in the year 2004, and is presently at #268. (Top 1000 Baby Names Statistics, 2014).
