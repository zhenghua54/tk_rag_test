Flowers are a common theme in general in Chinese traditional paintings, but the peony is an especially popular one. In Chinese art, the peony is a symbol of wealth, power and class; therefore it is an often-seen theme in traditional painting, silk embroidery, national clothes and decorations. Peonies may be small, but they are a unique flower of China, with a history that dates back thousands of years. Loved for its beauty, the peony is the national flower of China.
