Landscape may be further considered under the following categories: landscape art, cultural landscape, landscape ecology, landscape planning, landscape assessment and landscape design. The activity that modifies the visible features of an area of land is named landscaping. An early form of landscape poetry, Shanshui poetry, developed in China during the third and fourth centuries A.D. Shanshui poetry (or Shanshui chi, traditional Chinese: 山水詩; simplified Chinese: 山水诗 lit. mountains and rivers poetry) refers to the movement in poetry, influenced by the shan shui (landscape) painting style.
