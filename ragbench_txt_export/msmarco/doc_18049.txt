White Rabbit Creamy Candies originate from Aipixi Candy Factory, Shanghai in 1943. A merchant from Aipixi tried a milk candy from England then, and thought that its taste was not bad. He then manufactured the factory's own brand of milk candies after half a year. White Rabbit sweets were advertised with the line, Seven White Rabbit candies is equivalent to one cup of milk , and was seen as a nutritional product. The candies hence accompanied the growth of a generation.
