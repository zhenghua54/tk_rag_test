In general, bush beans those yield small seeds often recognized as sieve or baby lima or butter beans. Whereas, vine or pole varieties are large seeds and categorized as lima beans. While small beans measure 3 x 2 cm in dimension, large lima beans measure 4 x 2.5 cm. Furthermore, lima and butter beans are one of the excellent sources of minerals like molybdenum, iron, copper, manganese, calcium, magnesium. They hold (1724 mg) more potassium than red kidney beans (1359 mg), broad beans (1062 mg), black beans (1483 mg).
