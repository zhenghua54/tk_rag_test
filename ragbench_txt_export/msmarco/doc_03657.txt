Famotidine for dogs is an anti-ulcer drug used for the treatment and prevention of stomach ulcers and other stomach conditions in dogs. Stomach ulcers in dogs often occur with bloat, kidney failure, use of steroids and the use of NSAIDS in dogs. The following are the most common reasons Famotidine is used in canines: 1  For the treatment of gastric and intestinal ulcers (also used to prevent such ulcers). 2  Treating stomach erosions. 3  For the treatment of stomach inflammation, often caused by kidney failure.
