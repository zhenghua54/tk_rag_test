Apply by Mail. You can also apply for Tennessee Food Stamp program through mail. In order to do that, you will be required to download application forms first. You can download it in English, Spanish, Somali and Arabic. Here are different requirements of the program: 1  All applicants should be residing in the state of Tennessee to receive Food Stamps in Tennessee. 2  Children under the age of 21 and living with their parents will have to apply for the program as one household.
