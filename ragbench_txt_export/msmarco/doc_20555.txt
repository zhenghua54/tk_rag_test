From dynamic purchasing systems there is only a short step to electronic procurement. If you are interested in e-procurement in the European Union, you could start your tour by looking at the IDABC web page 2010: The e-procurement target for Europe: http://ec.europa.eu/idabc/en/document/5467/5584. Recitals 12 and 13 of the Procurement Directive offer the following explanation of a dynamic purchasing system: (12) Certain new electronic purchasing techniques are continually being developed.
