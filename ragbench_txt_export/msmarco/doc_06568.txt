Symptoms of the coronavirus are generally very mild, and may include a runny nose and watery eyes. If your cat is one of the rare felines who does develop the feline infectious peritonitis condition, the following signs and symptoms may occur: 1  Sneezing. 2  Watery eyes. 3  Runny nose. 4  Nasal discharge. 5  Diarrhea. 6  Loss of energy.
