Parsley, also known as Petroselinum Hortense, is a species of Petroselinum that belongs to the Apiaceae family. The garden variety of the Indian herb Parsley is usually bright green and hairless biennial plant. The ideal temperature to cultivate Parsley is 22-30 degree Celsius and it usually grows from seed. Parsley is called in various other names in India. Curly parsley is usually utilised as a garnish. Green parsley is generally used as a garnish on rice dishes, potato dishes, on fried chicken, fish, lamb, goose, meat, steaks or vegetable stews. However, parsley should not be consumed in excess as large amounts can have uterotonic effects on the consumer
