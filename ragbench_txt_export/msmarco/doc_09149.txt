These tools are inexpensive and can be used to fix loose vinyl siding or to remove siding panels completely. Locate the loose panel. Slip the notch of the siding removal tool into the ridge at bottom of the vinyl panel. The ridge is on the underside of the panel. Pull the panel toward you using the tool. Tips & Warnings. 1  Repair loose vinyl siding panels as soon as possible to avoid insect infestation or damage to your home due to wind, rain or cold temperatures. 2  Do apply too much pressure when lifing vinyl panels as these panels can chip or crack.
