The climate of Costa del Sol in the winter is cool with mean temperatures in the night falling to 8 degrees in December. In the day time temperatures reach a maximum of 17 degrees. Later in the year temperatures in Costa del Sol in March reach 20 degrees by day falling over night to 8 degrees celcius. In June the Costa del Sol temperature is in the range of 17 and 28 degrees before the Costa del Sol climate begins to cool in September when the mean maximum temp is just 27 degrees. At this time of the year the mean low temp is in the region of 19 degrees.
