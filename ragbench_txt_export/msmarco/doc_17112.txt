Machines without a bleach dispenser: 1  Add your favorite detergent to wash water before adding clothes or bleach. 2  Add ½ cup of Clorox® Regular-Bleach to wash water after your detergent, but before adding clothes. 3  Add clothes to wash water after the wash cycle begins. 1 For larger machines, we recommend using 1 cup Clorox® Regular-Bleach to get your whites their whitest. 2  If your extra large machines does not have a bleach dispenser, follow instructions above using 1 cup Clorox® Regular-Bleach. 3  If your extra large machines has a bleach dispenser, fill dispenser to the maximum fill line.
