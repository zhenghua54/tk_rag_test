In the 2011 Census, there were 136,474 people in Darebin (C) (Local Government Areas) of these 48.7% were male and 51.3% were female. Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people made up 0.8% of the population. In Darebin (C) (Local Government Areas), of occupied private dwellings 8.4% had 1 bedroom, 31.3% had 2 bedrooms and 43.2% had 3 bedrooms. The average number of bedrooms per occupied private dwelling was 2.7.
