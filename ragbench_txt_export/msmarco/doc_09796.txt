Recruitment Process Resources. Internal corporate recruiters usually design and manage the hiring and recruitment process. An efficient recruitment process is of course important to both employer and candidate. An unsatisfied employee is also not as productive. The recruitment process begins first with a vacancy created by a new position, a termination of an employee, a promotion, or an employee's resignation.
