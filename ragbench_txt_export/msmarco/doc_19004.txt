A: Prozac (fluoxetine) is a selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor (SSRI) antidepressant used to treat major depressive disorder, eating disorders, obsessive-compulsive disorder, premenstrual dysphoric disorder, and panic disorder. Many patients have been on Prozac for many years without adverse effects.
