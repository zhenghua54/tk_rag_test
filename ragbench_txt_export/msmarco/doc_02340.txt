...Or Later. Points you can't deduct this year have to be written off over the life of the loan. Suppose you pay $7,200 in deductible loan origination fees on a 30-year loan. To figure your annual deduction, divide up the points by the 360 monthly payments, which gives you $20 per payment. When you make 12 payments in a year, you get to write off $240 of loan origination fees.
