Linus Pauling-Biographical. Linus Carl Pauling was born in Portland, Oregon, on 28th February, 1901, the son of a druggist, Herman Henry William Pauling, who, though born in Missouri, was of German descent, and his wife, Lucy Isabelle Darling, born in Oregon of English-Scottish ancestry. In 1963, he was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize. Pauling is a member of numerous professional societies in the U.S.A. as well as in many European countries, India, Japan and Chile.
