As with almost all careers, the higher degree you hold, the higher your salary: A can add $2,000 to $5,000 or more per year in earning potential, while an MBA can add yet another $7,000 to $11,000, though many MBAs start out in higher-level management jobs with more responsibility, and consequently earn a larger salary. A general brand manager will make about $5,000 less than a package design manager, even though package design is only one part of a brand manager's responsibilities. Marketing salaries at the highest levels of a marketing professional's career only tell part of the story. Stock options, bonuses, benefits and other company perks can increase the base salary by 10 percent or more.
