Over time, vinyl siding panels can become loose. This can cause panels to chip or crack if left unattended. By using a vinyl siding removal tool, you can guide loose panels back into place. Proper vinyl siding maintenance helps preserve the look of your home while reducing heating and cooling costs. Tips & Warnings. 1  Repair loose vinyl siding panels as soon as possible to avoid insect infestation or damage to your home due to wind, rain or cold temperatures. 2  Do apply too much pressure when lifing vinyl panels as these panels can chip or crack.
