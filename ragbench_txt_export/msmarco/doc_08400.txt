For patients not covered by health insurance, the cost of a pelvic ultrasound typically varies by provider and geographic region. The typical cost range is $250 -$1,100, with a national average cost of $525, according to NewChoiceHealth.com. For example, Concierge Medicine in California charges $275 for a pelvic ultrasound.
