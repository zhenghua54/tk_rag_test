Choosing Your Industry. Entry-level engineering salaries vary with the hiring industry. For example, bachelor's grads in chemical engineering received top average pay of $75,000 annually in government but only $65,700 per year in manufacturing, according to the September 2013 NACE report. The second-highest starting salaries went to graduates in computer science, who received an average of $59,084 per year, while humanities and social science majors were in last place, averaging $38,045.
