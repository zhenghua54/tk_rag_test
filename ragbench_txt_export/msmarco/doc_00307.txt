The watershed of the creek is 67.5 mi 2 (175 km 2) in Loudoun and 23.8 mi 2 (62 km 2) in Fairfax. The Broad Run High School and Broad Run Magisterial District of Loudoun derive their names from the creek. The creek, located between Goose Creek and Sugarland Run, principally drains portions of eastern Loudoun County, as well as a small portion of western Fairfax County.
