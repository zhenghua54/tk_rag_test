1 Back pain-the physical therapist will examine the patient's back, determine how it is affecting his/her life, and check some other aspects of the patient's health. 2  The physical therapist may perform manual therapy, help the patient learn how to manage the pain, what to do to speed up recovery and prevent recurrence. Physical therapy also means the treatment of any pain, disease, or injury by physical means. A physical therapist seeks to identify and maximize quality of life and movement potential through prevention, intervention (treatment), promotion, habilitation, and rehabilitation.
