Dialectology is the study of dialects and their geographic or social distribution. Traditionally, dialectologists study the variety of language used within a particular speech community, a group of people who share a set of norms or conventions for language use. Sociolinguists generally recognize the standard variety of a language as one of the dialects of that language.
