The cost of room, board, books, supplies and other expenses for on-campus students attending Henderson State University is $11,354, which is below the national average of $13,399. By their final year they will pay approximately $12,234 for an associate degree seeker, and $12,859 for a bachelor degree seeker to cover these expenses Over the entire degree (assuming you graduate on-time) the total will come to $49,569 for a four year stay or $24,168 for a two year stay.
