Over time, however, the civil rights movement, the environmental movement, and, more controversially, the women s movement, did convert a majority of Americans to many of their views. All of the protest movements of the 1960s captured public attention and raised questions that were important to the nation. The civil rights movement, the women s movement, and the gay rights movement demanded that Americans consider equality for all citizens in the United States.
