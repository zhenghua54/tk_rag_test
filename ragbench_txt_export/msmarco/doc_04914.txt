Mutaflor® Escherichia coli strain Nissle 1917 was entered on the Australian Register of Therapeutic Goods in February 2012 and is still today the only probiotic in Australia registered at AUST R level. Mutaflor® is registered in Australia for the relief and management of chronic constipation. Your healthcare practitioner may also utilise this strain for its pharmacological, metabolic and immunomodulation properties.
