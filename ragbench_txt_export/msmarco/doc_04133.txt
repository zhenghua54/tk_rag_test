Heritage Lime Stucco/Plaster products are ideal for building durable and beautiful exterior or interior finishes on historic masonry, Lime stucco/plaster also recommended for sustainable new-build projects and strawbale construction that rely on breathable, all-natural construction materials. Heritage Hydraulic Lime Stucco/Plaster. This product is a traditional material made from 100% all-natural ingredients, and it is breathable and flexible. Hydraulic lime is distinguished from non-hydraulic lime by its ability to cure in damp/wet conditions.
