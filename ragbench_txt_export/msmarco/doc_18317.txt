Q. Q. Is additional conduit required beyond what is installed in the ditch? A. If the service is being supplied from a utility pole, the member is required to supply 1-10' section of SCH 80 PVC and 2-10' sections of SCH 40 PVC to be left near the base of the pole for installation by the power company up the pole.
