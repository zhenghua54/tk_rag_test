Ringworm is a fungal infection of the skin that is characterized by an often itchy, reddish to brownish ring-shaped rash. Ringworm on the body is also known as tinea corporis and is related to other fungal infections such as ringworm of the scalp, athlete's foot and jock itch. Ad. Ringworm has nothing to do with a worm, but is actually a fungal infection.
