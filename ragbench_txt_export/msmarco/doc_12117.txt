Industry Breakdown. According to the BLS, the largest employer of insurance agents was insurance agencies, which employed 231,940 agents. Starting agents at agencies could make a salary in line with the 25th percentile, at $33,050, or the 10th, at $25,450. Insurance carriers ranked second, with 76,380 agents. They could make a salary in line with the 25th percentile, at $37,220, or 10th percentile, at $27,820.
