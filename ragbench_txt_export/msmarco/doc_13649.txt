Symptoms common to most forms of viral hepatitis include flu-like symptoms, fever, headache, nausea, muscle aches and jaundice, a yellowing of the skin and whites of the eyes. Young children often have no symptoms of hepatitis A. The list of signs and symptoms mentioned in various sources for Viral Hepatitis includes the 25 symptoms listed below: 1  No symptoms-in some cases. 2  Mild symptoms-in some cases. 3  Early symptoms: 4  Mild fever. 5  Headache. 6  Muscle aches. 7  Fatigue. 8  Loss of appetite.
