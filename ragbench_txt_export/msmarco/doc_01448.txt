Full Definition of DETERIORATE. transitive verb. 1. : to make inferior in quality or value: impair. 2. : disintegrate. intransitive verb. — de·te·ri·o·ra·tive \-ˌrā-tiv\ adjective. See deteriorate defined for English-language learners. See deteriorate defined for kids. Late Latin deterioratus, past participle of deteriorare, from Latin deterior worse, from de- + -ter (suffix as in Latin uter which of two) + -ior (comparative suffix) — more at whether, -er. First Known Use: 1572.
