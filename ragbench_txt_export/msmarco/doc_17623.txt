On the ventral surface of the brain, the basilar part of pons (that is, the white pontine protuberance) is demarcated from both the medulla oblongata and the mesencephalon by distinct transverse grooves. Synonym(s): pons cerebelli, pons varolii. 2. 1 also called bridge of Varolius. a prominence on the ventral surface of the brainstem, between the medulla oblongata and the cerebral peduncles of the midbrain. The pons consists of white matter and a few nuclei and is divided into a ventral part and a dorsal part.
