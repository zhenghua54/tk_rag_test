The great thing about the fact that vitamin D is stored in the body is the fact that if you go a few days without exposure to sunlight you will still have enough vitamin D to remain healthy. Some of the symptoms of too much vitamin D include: 1  Loss of appetite. 2  Vomiting. 3  Nausea. 4  Constipation. 5  Weight loss. 6  Weakness. 7  Increase of blood calcium levels.
