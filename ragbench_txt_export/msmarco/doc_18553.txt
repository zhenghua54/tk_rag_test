Your eye care provider can advise you of specific costs for your individual needs. 1 If your eyes are too dry for conventional contact lenses, scleral lenses can help. 2  In particular, the generous space between the back surface of full scleral lenses and the cornea acts as a tear reservoir to keep the front of your eye more moist and comfortable.
