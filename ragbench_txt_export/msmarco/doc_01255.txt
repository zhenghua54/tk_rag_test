Cristina /cri-sti-na/ [3 sylls.] as a girls' name is pronounced kree-STEE-nah. It is of Latin origin, and the meaning of Cristina is follower of Christ. Italian, Spanish, Portuguese and Romanian form of Christina. Kreatif forms: Caistina, Crestina, Cristita. Cristina is a very prominent first name for females (#437 out of 4276, Top 10%) and also a very prominent last name for all people (#41722 out of 150436, Top 28%). (2000 U.S. Census).
