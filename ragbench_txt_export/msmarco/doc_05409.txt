Apprentices in the Tennessee Valley Authority's Lineman Apprentice Program are paid a fixed salary based on their level of training. 2012 TVA apprentice wages ranged from $36,450 to $54,360 per year. The national median annual income for all electricians was $49,320 as of May 2011, according to the BLS. The middle 50 percent earned between $37,570 and $65,260 per year. The top 10 percent made at least $82,680 per year, while the bottom 10 percent earned annual wages of $30,390.
