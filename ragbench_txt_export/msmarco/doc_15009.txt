1. The first settlers in North America were Native Americans. Native Americans were the first people to settle in America. Christopher Columbus traveled to America in 1492. He traveled on a ship. In the 1600 s people from England came to live in America. The Native Americans helped the new settlers. They helped them plant food, fish, hunt and build houses. The Pilgrims celebrated the first Thanksgiving. They invited the Native Americans to celebrate with them. Many people from Europe came to America. Their towns were called colonies. There were 13 colonies located on the East Coast. The new colonies belonged to England. The King of England was the leader of the 13 colonies
