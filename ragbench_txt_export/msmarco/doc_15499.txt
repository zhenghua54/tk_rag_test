Proteus vulgaris Growing on EMB Agar. Only gram-negative bacteria grow on EMB agar. Gram-positive bacteria are inhibited by the dyes eosin and methylene blue added to the agar. Because Proteus vulgaris does not ferment lactose or produce acid, their colonies appear grey on EMB agar.
