On an average, an occupational therapist based in Australia receives an average yearly salary of about AU$40,734 – AU$70,268 per year, while an average yearly bonus is around AU$5,039 per year. In fact, a well-experienced occupational therapist earns around AU$41,503 – AU$75,198per annum. In addition, a well-established occupational therapist receives a much higher salary of about $104,350 per annum. An hourly wage for an occupational therapist ranges from between $24.03 – $50.17 per hour.
