America’s Longest Hiking Trails. (In order of total distance. ). 1  American Discovery Trail – 6,800 miles – Stretching from coast-to-coast, the American Discovery Trail is listed as being about 6,800 miles but that’s a bit misleading. 1 Appalachian National Scenic Trail – 2,174 miles – The AT is the classic long-distance hike and one of three for the prestigious, but informal, Triple Crown (other two are Pacific Crest Trail and the Continental Divide).
