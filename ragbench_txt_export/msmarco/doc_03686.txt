Hourly rates for electricians vary depending on the cost of living in the area. The BLS names Alaska as the highest-paying state for electricians with an hourly mean wage of $32.44, followed by Illinois with a rate of $32.33 per hour and New York with a rate of $32 per hour. Hourly rates for electricians tend to be higher in other industries with fewer employment opportunities. Electricians in the motion picture and video industries earn an hourly average wage of $36.32, according to the BLS, and those in accounting, tax preparation, bookkeeping and payroll services earn $33.89 per hour.
