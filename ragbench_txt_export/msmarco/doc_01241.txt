How to Remove Dander if Allergic to Cats. Cat dander sticks to walls, floors, furniture and other items. If you're allergic to cats, dander exposure may cause symptoms ranging from itching and sneezing to breathing problems or death. If you're moving into a home where a kitty once lived, or if you have a cat but recently developed allergic symptoms, you'll need to remove dander from your environment. Step 2. Remove dander from your clothing by washing in the hottest water possible for the fabric type and with an anti-allergen laundry detergent. These products are available at most retail stores.
