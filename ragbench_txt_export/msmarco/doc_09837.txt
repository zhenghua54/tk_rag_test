Amery is alike in pronunciation to Aimerey, Aimery, Amaury, Amerey, Amiri, Aymery and Omero. Other suggested similar baby names are Ameer, Ameir, Amer, Amir ▲ , Ammer, Amor, Amr, Avery ▲ and Aymer. Amery /ame-ry/ [3 sylls.] as a boys' name is of Old German derivation, and Amery means brave power. Amery is an alternate spelling of Amory (Old German): transcription of Emery. Compare surname Amero
