According to the Office of Personnel Management, the United States Vice Presidential Salary in 2011 was $230,700 per year. In addition, the Vice President is awarded a $20,000 expense account for costs incurred while maintaining the office of Vice President. As reported by the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the salary for similar positions in the public sector was $165,080 in 2010. The Vice President of the United States is on call 24 hours a day while in office. In addition to normal, day-to-day office work, a Vice President also performs ceremonial duties at home and around the world.
