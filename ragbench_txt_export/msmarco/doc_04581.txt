Welcome to Gonzales Louisiana! Welcome to Gonzales, Louisiana, conveniently located between the capitol city of Baton Rouge and the fun and originality of New Orleans. Our area is home to some of the South’s most interesting and beautiful tourist attractions, many steeped in our rich history. There are no fewer than a dozen antebellum homes within a short drive, numerous restaurants all featuring original cuisine and boutique shops along with an outlet mall. So, if you’re looking for “a great escape,” or wanting to relocate, we’re sure you’ll agree when we say, “Gonzales, Nous avons tout!” Call on us for information on our many divisions, departments or simply to find out more about this city we love to call home.
