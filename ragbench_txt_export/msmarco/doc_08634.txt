Definition: A bisque is a rich, creamy soup made with shellfish — specifically, bisque is traditionally made with the pureed puréed meat of, crustaceans including, lobster, crab shrimp or. crayfish Bisque can be thickened with rice, which is the traditional method. Modern bisques are often thickened with roux. Additionally, although a classic bisque is made only with shellfish, some thick soups made with vegetables, poultry or meat are sometimes referred to as bisques.
