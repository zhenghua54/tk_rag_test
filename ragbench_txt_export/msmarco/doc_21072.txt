Cane toads are the largest toads and grow up to nine inches in length. A massive cane toad caught in Australia has been described as the size of a small dog! Diet: Like frogs, most toads eat insects and other arthropods. However, some species eat reptiles, small mammals, and even other amphibians. Typical Lifespan: The common toad (Bufo bufo) lives up to 40 years, but most toad species live approximately five to ten years.
