If the plumbing in your home is old, it is likely that your water pipes are made from iron, steel, or lead. Modern water pipes are almost always made from copper, although plastic or polyvinyl chloride (PVC) pipes are becoming popular too. 1 PVC is likewise a safe material to carry drinking water. 2  Copper and PVC water pipes do not suffer easily from corrosion, while iron corrodes relatively easily. 3  Copper is relatively easy to work with because it is much softer than iron or lead. 4  PVC water pipes are also much easier to shape
