Actually, this random sugar test is also called a simple glucose test. You use to do it by yourself when you want to measure your blood sugar levels at any time during the day. You can use any glucose meters that can be found in the market. Basically, this is a test that you perform to get to know what the levels of your blood glucose are at any time of the day to better monitor your diabetes. So, the normal results you should get are between 70 to 100 mg/dl.
