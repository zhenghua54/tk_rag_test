(See the next module on neuroses.)In other words, for Freud repression is a normal part of human development; indeed, the analysis of dreams, literature, jokes, and Freudian slips illustrates the ways that our secret desires continue to find outlet in perfectly well-adjusted individuals. The job of dream interpretation is to translate the manifest dream back into its constituent, if buried, dream-thoughts. The interpretation of symptomsfollows a similar path; the goal is to determine the repressed sexual desires or traumatic events that are causing the abnormal behavior to occur.
