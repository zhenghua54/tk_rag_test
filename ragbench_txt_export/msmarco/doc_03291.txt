Acupuncture is more than just needles. “Qi” is roughly pronounced “chee”, and is the Chinese name for a concept that exists not only in Chinese medicine, but in other types of eastern whole medicine systems as well. Qi is what makes up our body’s fundamental consitution. There are over a thousand known acupuncture points on the body.
