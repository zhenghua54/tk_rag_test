Excellent Sources of Complex Carbohydrates. Complex carbohydrate foods are basically those in wholegrain form such as wholegrain breads, oats, muesli and brown rice. Complex carbs are broken down into glucose more slowly than simple carbohydrates and thus provide a gradual steady stream of energy throughout the day. Natural complex carbs are often devoid of additives and preservatives unlike many processed simple carb sources.
