The downside to getting protein from eggs is that they’re high in cholesterol. If you’re healthy, daily cholesterol should be limited to 300 milligrams or less. One large hard-boiled egg has 186 milligrams. One hard-boiled egg has 5.3 grams of total fat and 1.63 grams of saturated fat, which is 47.7 calories in total fat and 14.7 calories in saturated fat.
