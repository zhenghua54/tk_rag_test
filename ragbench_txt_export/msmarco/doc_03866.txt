Identify Healthy Cholesterol Levels. According to the American Heart Association a healthy total cholesterol level is less then 200 mg/dl. You are considered to have borderline high cholesterol if your level is between 200 to 239 mg/dl. You will be diagnosed with high cholesterol if your level is is 240 mg/dl and over. Cholesterol level can be checked with a simple blood test performed by a doctor. You may or may not have to fast depending on the type of test your physician order
