Bankrate’s 2010 Closing Costs Survey, which was released Monday morning, found that the average origination and title fees on a $200,000 mortgage this year totaled $3,741, up from $2,732 in 2009. Wisconsin ranked 48th with total costs of $3,303, which consist of origination fees of $1,491 and title and closing costs of $1,812. Wisconsin’s rank declined from 22nd in the country in 2009, Bankrate said
