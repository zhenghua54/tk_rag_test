To do it from there, login to your joomla administrative account, go to the “Site” menu and select “Media Manager”. When the Media Manager opens up, type in the folder name in the text field under “Files” and click the “Create folder” button. 2. Upload your PDF file with FTP or Media Manager to the “myPDFDocs” folder. Now open your content page where you want to insert your PDF with the WYSIWYG editor. 4. Highlight the text that you want to link with your PDF file then click the cable/link icon on the toolbar. 5. In the new window that opens, enter the link to your PDF file.
