Combine the seasonings and oil in a large glass baking-dish, add the chicken and coat with the spice mixture. Cover and refrigerate overnight or for a few hours. Pre-heat the oven to Convection Roast or Convection Bake 350 degrees. Place the chicken pieces on a rimmed baking sheet and place it in the oven on rack position 2 (counting up from the bottom of the oven) Cook for 20 minutes, turn the pieces, brush with the BBQ sauce and cook for another 15 minutes.
