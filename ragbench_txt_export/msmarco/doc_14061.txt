BREAKING DOWN 'Tertiary Industry'. The tertiary industry is one of three industry types in a developed economy, the other two being the primary (raw materials) and secondary (goods production) industries. As an economy becomes more developed, it will shift its focus from primary to secondary and tertiary industries. DEFINITION of 'Tertiary Industry'. The segment of the economy that provides services to its consumers. This includes a wide range of businesses including financial institutions, schools, transports and restaurants. Also known as tertiary sector of industry, or service industry/sector
