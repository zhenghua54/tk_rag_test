As of 2012, a plastic surgeon in a multispecialty practice averages $445,000 a year, according to Medscape. Those in a healthcare organization earn the next highest salary, averaging $285,000 a year. Plastic surgeons at single-specialty offices earn $284,000 a year, while those at a sole practice earn $280,000 a year.
