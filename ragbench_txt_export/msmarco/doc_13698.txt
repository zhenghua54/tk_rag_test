Singulair is available as oral tablets, oral granules, or chewable tablets. Side effects of Singulair can include skin rash, mood changes, tremors, headache, stomach pain, heartburn, upset stomach, nausea, diarrhea, tooth pain, tiredness, fever, stuffy nose, sore throat, cough, and hoarseness. Singulair may interact with phenobarbital or rifampin.
