dianazh/iStock/Getty Images. Sage, or Salvia officianalis, is a kitchen herb that is sometimes also used for its potential health benefits. It may help limit the symptoms of or treat Alzheimer's disease, diabetes and high cholesterol. One thing sage can definitely help you do is decrease your sodium intake. Using herbs is a good way to add flavor to your dishes without using salt. Sage goes well with cheese dips, carrots, peas, tomatoes, pork, poultry, fish and shellfish. It's often used in combination with thyme and rosemary.
