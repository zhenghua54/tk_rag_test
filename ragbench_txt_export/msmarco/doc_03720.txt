Executive housekeepers, on the other hand, who would oversee other housekeepers, averaged $40,000 to $65,000 a year. The wide gaps in pay are likely due to variance in a family’s wealth and size of home. In 2011, maids averaged $21,440 a year, or 10,31 an hour, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics. Because higher salaries — as often seen in medical facilities — can skew this figure, median wage is often a better indication of earning potential.
