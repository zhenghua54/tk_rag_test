English names generally consist of a given name, middle name and family name. Many English speaking countries have fairly flexible naming laws. This allows the multicultural families that live in these countries to merge the naming conventions of their faiths or previous nationalities with their current homeland. Vincent is generally used as a boy's name. It consists of 7 letters and 2 syllables and is pronounced Vin-cent. In the U.S. in 2014, it ranked 104 in baby name popularity for boys with 3744 occurrences. Less than 5 girls were given the name.
