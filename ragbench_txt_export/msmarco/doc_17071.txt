Odia /əˈdiːə/ or Oriya /ɒˈriːə/ , both renderings of ଓଡ଼ିଆ oṛiā (help·info) , is an Indian language belonging to the Indo-Aryan branch of the Indo-European language family. Odia speakers are regarded as one of the ‘Transnational Ethnic Indian Groups’. In India, the language is spoken by over 31 million people, and globally over 45 million speak Odia. It is one of the official languages of India and the major language of Odisha.
