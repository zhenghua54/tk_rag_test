Coconut oil, being mainly a saturated fat, is able to withstand higher temperatures than other oils, making it one of the best oils for cooking. It also gives a delicious taste and superb texture to foods. Use organic extra-virgin coconut oil for the best quality. Include coconut in your diet for healthy fats. The purported health benefits of coconut oil have sparked debate in the medical community. Conventional dietary advice has been to limit coconut oil because it is rich in saturated fats, which have been associated with heart disease.
