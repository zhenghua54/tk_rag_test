Q. How is recycling good for the environment? A. Each year recycling:• Saves enough energy to power 1.4 million California homes.• Reduces water pollution by 27,047 tons. • Saves 14 million trees. A large, growing part of that sector is recycling and recycling-related businesses. The ability to bring new life to used materials has become more than just good environmental stewardship; it has become big business.
