By July and August, full summer weather hits Cape Cod and temperatures are typically between 62 degrees and 78 degrees. Average monthly precipitation is about 3 inches. You should bring your swimsuit and plenty of summer clothing, but also a few warmer items for nighttime and the cool beach breezes. September 's weather is a lot like July and August, only a bit cooler. Temperatures typically range between 55 degrees and 70 degrees and average precipitation during the month is 3.7 inches. It gets a little cooler later in the month, so bring along a light jacket.
