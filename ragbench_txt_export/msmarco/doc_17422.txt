The Coggins Test is a blood test that checks for Equine Infectious Anemia (EIA) antibodies in a horse's blood. It is used to diagnose a contagious disease that affects horses worldwide. A Coggins Test for Equine Infectious Anemia is required for transporting a polo pony from one state to another. The Federal government requires a Coggins Test before a polo pony can be imported.
