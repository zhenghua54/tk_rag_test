Chicken eggs have an incubation period of about 21 days and can be hatched using either a specialized incubator under carefully monitored conditions, or using a broody hen. Use these tips for hatching chicken eggs using both methods. 1 The eggs you find in grocery stores are not fertile and cannot be hatched. 2  For disease prevention and health reasons, it's best to get all your eggs from one source. 3  If you are looking for a particular or rare breed of chicken, you may need to contact a specialized hatchery.
