St. George's climate is significantly warmer on average than the rest of Utah, and typical of the desert southwest, with an arid desert climate (Koeppen BWks bwks BWhs), bwhs featuring, long hot summers and, brief cool. winters It is the principal city of the St. George Metropolitan Statistical Area. The St. George valley lies in the northeastern-most part of the Mojave Desert, at the convergence of three distinct geological areas; the Mojave Desert, Colorado Plateau, and Great Basin.
