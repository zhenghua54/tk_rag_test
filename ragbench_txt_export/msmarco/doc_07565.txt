As a source for food fortification, magnesium potassium aspartate is a dual salt of magnesium and potassium organically bound to the energy rich substance-aspartate. The anion-aspartate is not only capable physiological metabolism but also bioavailable and innocuous to the body. Potassium and magnesium L-aspartate and DL-aspartate salts have been used clinically to treat fatigue in human subjects. Nutritional role of Magnesium Magnesium is a vital anti-stress mineral, which has cardioprotective properties.
