The definition of subjective is something that is based on personal opinion. An example of subjective is someone believing purple is the best color. 1  of, affected by, or produced by the mind or a particular state of mind; of or resulting from the feelings or temperament of the subject, or person thinking; not objective; personal: a subjective judgment. Based on a given person's experience, understanding, and feelings; personal or individual: admitted he was making a highly subjective judgment. 1  Psychology Not caused by external stimuli. 2  Medicine Of, relating to, or designating a symptom or complaint perceived by a patient.
