The fundamental objective of Information Strategy Planning (ISP) is to develop a plan for implementing business systems to support business needs. The objectives of the Information Strategy Planning stage are to: - Establish an information strategy based on an evaluation of the business strategy. - Establish a development plan of user-oriented systems to meet business information needs and priorities. - Define an information architecture for the future development of compatible data-sharing systems. - Establish a technical strategy for the best use of new information technolo
