A diet high in monounsaturated fat from avocados has been shown to lower LDL (bad) cholesterol and raise HDL (good) cholesterol. Monounsaturated fat can also help reduce inflammation in the body. There are other reasons why avocados are good for you. The answer. Avocados are a very nutritious food (they’re a fruit actually). It’s true they’re high in fat – 84 per cent of the calories (325) in an avocado come from fat (30 grams).
