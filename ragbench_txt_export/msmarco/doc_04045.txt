Cape Town: Annual Weather Averages. February is the hottest month in Cape Town with an average temperature of 23°C (73°F) and the coldest is June at 13°C (55°F) with the most daily sunshine hours at 11 in December. The wettest month is July with an average of 100mm of rain. The best month to swim in the sea is in February when the average sea temperature is 21°C (70°F).
