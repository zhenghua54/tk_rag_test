The narration of  The Man Who Called Himself Jesus , from Strawbs ' eponymous first album, was performed by Wilson. Wilson's biography, One Foot on the Stage: The Biography of Richard Wilson (ISBN 0752811150), was written by James Roose-Evans. He is a major supporter of the Labour Party, and he recorded the party's manifesto for the 2010 General Election. Wilson has worked for the gay rights campaign group Stonewall and the Labour Party. He is one of the patrons of Scottish Youth Theatre. Wilson is also a long-time supporter of the charity Sense and in 2007 hosted their annual award ceremony.
