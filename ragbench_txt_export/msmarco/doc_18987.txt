Oregon's Clams includes: All the information necessary to consistently dig limits of Oregon's Razor Clams and Oregon's Bay Clams. Oregon's Clams discloses all of the locations where both bay clams and razor clams are commonly found along the Oregon Coast. The photos of the bay clam and razor clam shows displays how to identify both bay clam shows and razor clam shows in the wet and dry sand. Oregon's Razor Clams describes the methods to dig razor clams from the wet and dry sand.
