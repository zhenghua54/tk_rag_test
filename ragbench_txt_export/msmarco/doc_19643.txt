From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia. An oxygen tank is an oxygen storage vessel for oxygen, which is either held under pressure in gas cylinders or as liquid oxygen in a cryogenic storage tank. Oxygen tanks are used to store gas for: 1  industrial processes including the manufacture of steel and monel. 2  oxyacetylene welding equipment, glass lampworking torches, and some gas cutting torches. 3  use as the liquid rocket propellants for rocket engines. 4  medical breathing gas at medical facilities and at home.
