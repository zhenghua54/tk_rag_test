Signs & Symptoms of Acute Heart Failure (Sudden): 1  Severe shortness of breath, especially while walking or bending over to put on one's shoes. 2  Rapid heartbeat and palpitations, which may cause the heart to stop. 3  Sudden fluid retention. 4  Sudden weight gain and swelling. 5  Sudden and severe chest pain. Since the blood is coming to the left side from the lungs, this is where the blood can get pooled. Signs & Symptoms of Left Side Heart Failure: 1  Shortness of breath, especially while walking or bending over to put on one's shoes. 2  Fatigue. 3  Dizziness, lightheadedness.
