Gray Ribbon-This color is a symbol of diabetes, brain cancer and 'Borderline Personality Disorder (BPD)'. Green Ribbon-This color is commonly associated with tissue/organ donation, liver cancer, gall bladder/bile duct cancer. It is also associated with chronic Lyme disease, childhood depression, depression, cerebral palsy, missing children, glaucoma, environmental concerns, and worker and driving safety. Teal Ribbon-This color is a symbol for ovarian, cervical, and uterine cancers as well as substance abuse, sexual assault, PTSD, Myasthenia Gravis and polycystic ovarian syndrome, syringomylia, Tourette Syndrome, usher syndrome, spina bifida (can be trimmed in blue).
