celestial equator. n. 1. (Astronomy) the great circle lying on the celestial sphere the plane of which is perpendicular to the line joining the north and south celestial poles. Also called: equinoctial or equinoctial circle. 1. celestial equator-the great circle on the celestial sphere midway between the celestial poles. equinoctial, equinoctial circle, equinoctial line. great circle-a circular line on the surface of a sphere formed by intersecting it with a plane passing through the center.
