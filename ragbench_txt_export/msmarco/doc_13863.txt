COST TO REPAIR AN EVAPORATOR COIL. Evaporator coil replacement depends largely upon the size of your air conditioner. The part itself usually runs between $250 to $550 wholesale, with the low-end based on a 2-ton air conditioner and the high-end based on a 5-ton air conditioner. Add a few hundred for labor and it comes to anywhere between $500 to $900 for replacement.
