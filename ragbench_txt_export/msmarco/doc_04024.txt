Our commentary on the costs of Twitter advertising. These are the costs revealed in the leaked email from Twitter’s ad sales department: 1  Cost of $2.50 and $4 per follower. 2  $0.75 to $2.50 for each click, favourite, retweet or reply. 3  Commitment to a minimum campaign spend of 3 months and $15,000. Since Twitter introduced it’s advertising through it’s Promoted Tweets we’ve had questions from marketers about costs and whether the service would become available to advertisers with smaller budgets as is possible through Adwords, Facebook and LinkedIn.
