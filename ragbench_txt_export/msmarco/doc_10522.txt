TEAS Test Format Overview. The TEAS test is a multiple choice exam that contains four sections. The overall test contains 170 questions and has a three hour and 29 minute (209 minute) time limit. Of the 170 total questions, 20 questions are used for internal testing purposes and not scored. The TEAS subtest and time limits are: TEAS Science. The TEAS Science subtest consists of 54 total questions (48 scored) and has a 66 minute time limit. The most recent version of the exam (TEAS V) greatly expanded the science subtest. Candidates often find this section to be one of the most challenging.
