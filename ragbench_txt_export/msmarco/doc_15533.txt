You may have a mouth ulcer if you have: 1  A painful sore or sores inside your mouth, on the tongue, soft palate (the back portion of the roof of your mouth), or inside your cheeks. 2  Sores in your mouth that are round, white, or grey in colour, with a red edge or border. 1 These are around 2-8mm in diameter and usually clear up in 10 days to 2 weeks. 2  Major ulcers. 3  These are bigger and deeper, often with a raised or irregular border. 4  This type of ulcer can take several weeks to heal and may leave a scar in the mouth.
