How Much Does a Social Worker Make? The average annual salary for social workers ranges from $24,000 to $70,000 a year, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS); PayScale puts the pay range at $26,000 to $56,000. The average annual salary is around $42,000, according to the BLS. Glassdoor.com puts the average salary at about $50,000. Pay also varies within specialization depending on the type of facility where the social worker is employed. For example, school, child, and family social workers make $54,000 in elementary and secondary schools, while they only make $35,000, on average, in individual and family services.
