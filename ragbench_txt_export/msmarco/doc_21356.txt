How long it takes to collect statewide criminal records can vary depending on the state. There are 46 states that use some variation of online criminal databases, several providing only limited coverage. If you need a background check from any of those states you can get results in just a few minutes. The 46 states that use online databases will return records in a few minutes, but the four states without online databases can take 2-4 business days.
