A business litigation lawyer may work for a law firm hired by a corporation, or may work as in-house counsel for the given corporation. Most often, though, such lawyers are outside counsel since the majority of businesses do not engage in litigation often enough to require an in-house litigation attorney. A business litigation lawyer represents corporations or other business entities who become involved in litigation.
