The Utah Department of Workforce Services expects an increase of 28% in the number of accounting jobs in the state between 2012 and 2022. This is more than twice as high as the national rate of increase expected for accounting jobs nationwide according to the US Department of Labor (DOL). The median salary for an accountant in Utah was $62,300 in 2014 according to the DOL. Experienced professionals in the 90th percentile, often those with CPA licenses, earned an average of $103,600
