Pasta is one of the simplest and purest foods around, as it is made of just two ingredients, semolina and water, without the addition of any hidden artificial preservatives or colorings. Selection of Raw Material. The first decisive step in making good pasta is the selection of the appropriate raw material. All quality pasta is made from Durum wheat semolina. Durum wheat is considered to be the most noble of all hard wheat and the richest in nutritional value. Durum wheat is what gives pasta its rich taste, pleasant aroma and bright, amber-yellow color
