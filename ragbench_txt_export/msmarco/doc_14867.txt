Twelve days in the month can usually be classified as monsoon days. The average monthly temperature is 85.6 or 7.9 degrees lower than that of July. On the 1st of the month the average high is 102 and the low 77; by the 30th the average high is down to 94 and the low 68. The hot weather is not completely over as there usually are on the average 13 days with 100 or higher during the month. The average date of the last 100 degree temperature is the 27th. The lowest temperature ever recorded was 47 on the 20th and 21st in 1965.
