Homebuyers are still landing great rates on mortgages. Nationwide, the average cost of closing on a $200,000 loan with a 20% down payment hit $2,539 in June, a 6% increase from a year earlier, according to Bankrate.com. Homebuyers in Texas are paying far more than that, however, with average closing costs of $3,046 for a $200,000 loan. That's up 23% compared with a year earlier. In Wisconsin, buyers saw an even bigger hike: They're now paying an average of $2,706, a 28% increase, according to Bankrate's data.
