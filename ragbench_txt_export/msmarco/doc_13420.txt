Jenna-Meaning of Jenna. Tweet. Jenna's language of origin is Hebrew and it is predominantly used in the English language. The name developed as a variant of Jenny with the Latinate feminine suffix '-a'. It was first adopted in the late 20th century, popularized by a character bearing the name in the popular US TV soap opera Dallas (1978-1991). Jenna is also an English form of the English and German Jenny. See also the related forms, Jennabel, Jennalyn, Jennanne, and Jennarae. Jenna is popular as a baby name for girls.
