The Vietnam War began on November 1st, 1955. It was a war that was fought in Vietnam, Cambodia and Laos. The communist forces wanted to expand their territory to include these countries. The Vietnam War ended with the fall of Saigon on April 30th 1975. The Viet Cong gained control of this city which had been the South Vietnamese Political base. Thousands of U.S. diplomatic, military, and civilian personnel were evacuated via helicopter.
