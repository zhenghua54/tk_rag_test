Personal motivation is the key to success in what ever we want to achieve in out life, however it is also the hardest concept to explain. As a job seeker, one of the most common job interview questions you may face are related to your motivation and the way you define success in your job. The questions can phrase: “what motivates you to put forth your greatest effort”, “what motivates you to work hard”, “what motivates you in your career” or “what are you passionate about”.
