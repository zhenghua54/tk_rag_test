Since the blood is coming to the right side of the heart from the body this is where blood can pool. Signs & Symptoms of Right Side Heart Failure: 1  Swelling in the feet, ankles and legs. 2  Distended abdomen or bloating. 3  Fatigue. 4  Shortness of breath, especially while walking or bending over to put on one's shoes. Since the blood is coming to the left side from the lungs, this is where the blood can get pooled. Signs & Symptoms of Left Side Heart Failure: 1  Shortness of breath, especially while walking or bending over to put on one's shoes. 2  Fatigue. 3  Dizziness, lightheadedness.
