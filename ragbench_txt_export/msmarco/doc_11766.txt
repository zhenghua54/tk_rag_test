Tubular secretions: It is the mechanism by which substances are removed from the blood and are directly added to the tubular fluids. Hydrogen ion, ammonium and drugs such as penicillin are some of the substances that move from the blood into the distal tubule of nephron. Secretions keep the blood from becoming acidic.
