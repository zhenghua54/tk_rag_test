The HPV test on the other hand checks for the virus itself and is used with the pap in women 30 and older, especially if the pap has unclear results. [1, 3, 4, 5] The HPV test, like the pap, takes a sample of cervical cells, which is then hybridized with a specific HPV RNA probe cocktail.
