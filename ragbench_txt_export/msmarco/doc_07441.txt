The number of calories you need to eat each day depends on several factors, including your age, size, height, sex, lifestyle, and overall general health. As an example, a physically active 6ft 2in male, aged 22 years, requires considerably more calories than a 5ft 2ins sedentary woman in her 70s. 1 The longer you chew your food, the more calories your body retains. 2  Recommended daily calorie intakes in the US are 2,700 for men and 2,200 for women. 3  Eating a big breakfast could help with weight reduction and maintenance. 4  When food is eaten may matter as much as what and how many calories are eaten.
