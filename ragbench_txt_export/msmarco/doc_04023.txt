An email from Twitter sales to prospective advertisers today revealed what brands have been paying for followers on the platform. The memo included cost-per-follower (CPF) rates for Promoted Accounts and cost-per-engagement (CPE) for Promoted Tweets. The CPF runs between $2.50 and $4, while the listed CPE rates come in from $0.75 to $2.50. For CPE, engagement refers to clicks, favorites, retweets and @Replies..
