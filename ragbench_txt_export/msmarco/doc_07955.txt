Airplanes. Believe it or not, some airplanes use parachutes as well. For some, the function is a lot like a drag racer. This is ideal for areas with limited runways, such as an aircraft carrier's deck. There also are some aircraft that use a parachute recovery system to protect them from crashes. Space Travel. Parachutes don't have much use in a vacuum because they require air to actually function; yet, the Apollo capsule was equipped with one. NASA uses parachutes for a similar reason that some airplanes do, for recovery.
