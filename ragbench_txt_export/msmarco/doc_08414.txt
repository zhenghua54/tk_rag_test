adjective. The definition of mechanical is something that relates to the skill or use of machinery or tools. An example of mechanical is a mechanical skill, when someone is able to fix machines. 1  using or having to do with machinery. 2  having skill in the use of machinery or tools. 1 Of or relating to machines or tools: mechanical skill. 2  Operated or produced by a mechanism or machine: a mechanical toy dog. 3  Of, relating to, or governed by mechanics. 4  Performed or performing in an impersonal or machinelike manner; automatic: a droning, mechanical delivery of the speech.
