Noxious Weeds-Myrtle Spurge. Myrtle Spurge (Euphorbia myrsinites), also called donkey tail or creeping spurge, is one of Colorado's List A noxious weeds, which requires it to be eradicated and prevented from propagating. Myrtle Spurge is an herbaceous plant with fleshy, waxy, grayish-green leaves that spiral around spreading, prostrate stems. It produces small yellow flowers in the early spring. When mature, it is 4 to 12 inches tall, with stems up to 18 inches long.
