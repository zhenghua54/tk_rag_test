Heart Health. Adzuki beans are an excellent source of soluble fiber, which helps to keep cholesterol levels in a healthy range. Lower cholesterol is associated with a lower risk of heart disease. They also contain folate, potassium, and magnesium, all of which are essential for a healthy heart. Adzuki beans are popular in Asian cooking and are often served with rice or turned into a paste and used in desserts. They were first cultivated between 4000 BC and 2000 BC.
