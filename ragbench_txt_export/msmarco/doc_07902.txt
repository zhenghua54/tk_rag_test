Not to be confused with the genus of flowering plants, Hepatica, that may also be called liverwort. The Marchantiophyta /mɑrˌkaentiˈɒfɨtə/ mɑrˌkæntiˈɒfɨtə are a division of-non vascular bryophyte land plants commonly referred to as hepatics or. liverworts Today, liverworts can be found in many ecosystems across the planet except the sea and excessively dry environments, or those exposed to high levels of direct solar radiation. As with most groups of living plants, they are most common (both in numbers and species) in moist tropical areas.
