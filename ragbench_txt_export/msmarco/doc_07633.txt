Oxytocin is a hormone that helps relax and reduce blood pressure and cortisol levels. It increases pain thresholds, has anti anxiety effects, and stimulates various types of positive social interaction. In addition, it promotes growth and healing. Anorexia treatment Oxytocin is sometimes called the love hormone.. It's released during bonding activities like childbirth and sex. Researchers gave oxytocin or a placebo, via nasal spray, to 31 patients with anorexia and 33 healthy control patients.
