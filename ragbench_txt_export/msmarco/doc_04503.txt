Ketel One is a 40%. vodka from the Netherlands. The vodka is produced by Nolet Distillery and imported by Ketel One USA. The quality is considered to be great. The average price is around $23.36 per 750mL. Ketel One Vodka Review By taffyg2003. I had a great Ketel One martini over the holiday weekend. It was probably the best Ketel One martini I’ve had, and one of the best martinis I’ve had, too. Since it is a “common” vodka, it is easy to overlook just how nice Ketel One is; very smooth, crisp, and subtly sweet.
