USA Cost/Aid Facts. The cost of the study per year (Tuition and fees, does not include living expenses) in USA varies from $5000 to $50000 (might be much more too for MBA/Medical or other programs) per year. The cost of living depends on the location. For example States like Washington, California and area around New York is costly. 1 Tuition Fees will cost between $5000 to $50000. 2  Basic Health Insurance may Cost up to 500$ (per semester). 3  Books may cost up to $1000 (actually may be much cheaper .. you can get used books from Amazon!). 4  Traveling to USA may cost up to $1400.
