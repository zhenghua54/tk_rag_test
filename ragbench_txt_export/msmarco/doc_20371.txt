Why It Is Done. A cardiac perfusion scan is done to: 1  Find the cause of unexplained chest pain or pressure. 2  Find the cause of chest pain or pressure brought on by exercise. 3  Check for the location and amount of damage caused by a heart attack. 4  Identify coronary artery disease (CAD) .
