Dihydrogen monoxide: is also known as hydroxyl acid, and is the major component of acid rain. contributes to the greenhouse effect. may cause severe burns. contributes to the erosion of our natural landscape. The first appearance of the hoax on the internet was attributed by the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette to the so-called Coalition to Ban Dihydrogen Monoxide, a parody organization started by UC Santa Cruz student Craig Jackson following the on-campus postings and initial newsgroup discussions.
