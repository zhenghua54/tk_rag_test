Gravid females will usually have a pre-lay shed 2 to 3 weeks after ovulation and will then lay her eggs in around 4 to 5 weeks. Ball Pythons lay one clutch of eggs per season and the average number of eggs per clutch is six. Ball Pythons lay one clutch of eggs per season and the average number of eggs per clutch is six. We always remove the eggs right after the female has finished her laying eggs and carefully place them in an incubation container that contains about three inches of damp vermiculite with plastic egg crate on top.
