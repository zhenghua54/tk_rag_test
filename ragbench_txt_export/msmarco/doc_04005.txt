Peanut Oil. Peanut oil has a pleasing and sometimes light nutty flavor. In addition to its great taste, peanut oil is perfect for deep-frying because it has a unique property (visit turkeyfrying.net for some great cooking ideas with peanut oil!) It does not absorb the flavor of other foods cooked in the oil. Health Benefits. Peanut oil is one of the healthiest oils. It is a vegetable oil that is naturally trans fat-free, cholesterol free, and low in saturated fats. Peanut oil is high in unsaturated fats, especially monounsaturated fat, like olive oil.
