Ceiling lift. A ceiling lift is a hoist that is mounted on a ceiling. If a wheeled hoist is to be used for transfers in and out of a bathtub, the bathtub should have legs or be raised from the floor the hoist frame will fit under the bathtub. Stationary Hoist. Stationary hoists are mounted to the floor or a wall.
