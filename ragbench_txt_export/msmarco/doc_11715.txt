Burris Xtreme Tactical Picatinny Style Rail 1 inch Weapon Rings Product Info. Ideal for all rifle scopes on AR15/M16 flattop receivers for proper cheek weld, Burris 1 Xtreme Tactical Ultra-Strong rings are available in four heights from 1/4 to 1 to accommodate any type of scope. Burris Xtreme Military Rings are ready for the heaviest of scopes, massive recoil, and severe usage.
