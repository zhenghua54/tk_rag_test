Median Salary for Immigration Lawyers. PayScale.com reported that the median yearly salary for lawyers practicing immigration law was $60,837, according to a January 2014 report. Other factors like location, experience and employer could influence your salary as an immigration lawyer. The following figures are the salary ranges for immigration lawyers with various years of work experience, as reported by PayScale.com in January 2014: 1  1-4 years: $37,788-$80,537. 2  5-9 years: $48,479-$88,319. 3  10-19 years: $73,000-$141,000.
