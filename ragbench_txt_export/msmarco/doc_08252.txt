Brand name Cymbalta. The investigational drug Cymbalta(TM) (duloxetine HCl) demonstrated rapid relief of anxiety symptoms associated with depression that was sustained for the length of the study period, according to new data published in the journal Depression and Anxiety. As anxiety is a key symptom of depression, treating this symptom plays a significant role in the overall treatment of depression and in a patient's ability to ultimately achieve complete recovery, said David Dunner, M.D., executive director, University of Washington Center for Anxiety and Depression.
