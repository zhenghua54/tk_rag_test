Zendesk is arguably one of the most popular help desk solutions available today. Used by a large number of companies both big and small, Zendesk is a solid application. But, while the version of this cloud service that we tested can be had for only $1 per user per month, you can bet most of Zendesk's customers aren't running at this Starter tier price. Competent But Probably Expensive Zendesk is a capable help desk solution. It has most of the features that a small, medium, or even large organization will need, as long as tickets and the way they get raised are what your organization primarily needs.
