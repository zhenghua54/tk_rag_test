1 In a medium bowl, mix the ricotta, mozzarella cheese and 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. 2  Mix in egg and basil. 3  Rinse the eggplant in cold water until all salt is removed. 4  In a large skillet, heat 4 tablespoons olive oil over medium heat. 5  Place one layer of eggplant in the pan, brown each side. Ready In. 1  Sprinkle both sides of the eggplant slices with salt. 2  Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). 3  Rinse the eggplant in cold water until all salt is removed. 4  In a 9x13 inch baking dish, evenly spread 1 1/2 cups of spaghetti sauce. 5  Bake 30 to 45 minutes in the preheated oven, until sauce is bubbly.
