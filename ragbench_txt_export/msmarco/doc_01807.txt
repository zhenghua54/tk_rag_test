Asia is the largest of the continents. Asia is located east of Europe, so it shares its western border with Europe. To the east of Asia, lies the Pacific Ocean, which also separates Asia from North America. South of Asia, lies the Indian Ocean. The Indian Ocean separates Asia from another continent. The seven continents are: North America, South America, Europe, Asia, Africa, Australia/Oceania, and Antarctica. The United States is located on the continent of North America. Oceans make up most of the world's surface area.
