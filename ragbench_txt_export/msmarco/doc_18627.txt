A GIS specialist is an individual that works with geographic information systems (GIS) to solve problems, present data, and store information. This can be a full time career or integrated as a duty into a position such as a natural resources manager. The versatility and efficiency of GIS systems has created a high demand for GIS specialists. Private natural resource management companies employ a large number of GIS specialists to make maps, conduct remote sensing, calculate statistics, and keep data organized and accessible.
