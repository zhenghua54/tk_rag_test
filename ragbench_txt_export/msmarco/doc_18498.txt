Yes, any donation you make to Khalsa Family Services, Inc, Khalsa School or Khalsa Montessori School is deductible on both your federal tax return and your state tax return if you qualify to itemize your deductions. The KCO is a separate legal entity, and is not part of the charter school. Donations you make to the KCO are deductible on your tax returns as itemized deductions, but do not qualify for a state tax credit.
