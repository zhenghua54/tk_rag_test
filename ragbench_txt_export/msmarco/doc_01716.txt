Probably not, because you probably keep your butter in the refrigerator. It’s likely that you keep butter in the fridge because you think it’s safer that way. And if you consume bad cream, you could get sick, if not from the rancid taste then certainly from the foreign bacteria that feasted on the stuff when it was out in the open.
