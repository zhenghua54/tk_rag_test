Pleural Thickening Causes: Symptoms, Treatment Of Pleural Thickening. Pleural thickening happens when the tissue layers that cover the lungs (known as pleura) become thickened. As a result, the lungs do not expand freely and breathing will then be labored. Causes of pleural thickening include: 1  Inflammation in the lungs. 2  Infections from bacterial pneumonia. 3  Tuberculosis infection. 4  Benign or malignant tumor. 5  Rheumatoid disease of the lungs. 6  Radiation and chemotherapy
