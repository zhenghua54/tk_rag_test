SNP Drivers NDIS version SNP drivers are sometimes referred to by their NDIS version. NDIS stands for Network Driver Interface Specification. With the advent of native support for TCP offload in Windows introduced by Microsoft's SNP [Scalable Networking Pack] comes a new driver architecture for Alacritech TCP offload hardware.
