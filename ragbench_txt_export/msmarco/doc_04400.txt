Basel III or Basel 3 released in December, 2010 is the third in the series of Basel Accords. These accords deal with risk management aspects for the banking sector. What are the objectives / aims of the Basel III measures? Basel 3 measures aim to: → improve the banking sector's ability to absorb shocks arising from financial and economic stress, whatever the source → improve risk management and governance → strengthen banks' transparency and disclosures.
