Fiber Content of Strawberries. One of the most important nutritional components of strawberries is their high fiber content. Strawberries not only contain high amounts of fiber in the meat of the berry, but also within the seeds of the fruit. Fiber can help in the prevention of a number of chronic health conditions, and it's important in the treatment and prevention of constipation
