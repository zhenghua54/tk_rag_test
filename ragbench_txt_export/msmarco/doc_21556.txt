Curl pattern refers to the amount of wave, curl, or coil in your hair. It is divided into 3 main hair type categories: Wavy Type 2, Curly Type 3, and Coily Type 4. Knowing your curl pattern will help you find the right products, styles, techniques, and care tips for your hair. More About Pattern. Porosity. Porosity refers to how easily your hair is able to absorb and hold moisture.
