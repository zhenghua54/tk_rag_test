Saudi Arabia is bordered by Jordan and Iraq to the north, Kuwait to the northeast, Qatar, Bahrain, and the United Arab Emirates to the east, Oman to the southeast, and Yemen to the south. Saudi Arabia (/ˌsɔːdiː əˈreɪbiə/ , /ˌsaʊ-/), officially known as the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA) , is an Arab state in Western Asia constituting the bulk of the Arabian Peninsula.
