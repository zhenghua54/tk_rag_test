1 Although she was not named in the episode, Astaroth's name was revealed in the Supernatural Companion for Season 3. 2  Astaroth was the second demon of lore to appear in the show; the first was Azazel. 3  In lore, Astaroth is the demon of laziness, vanity and rationalization. Astaroth was a powerful demon who worked to condemn human souls to Hell by turning them into witches. She turned Renee Van Allen, Elizabeth, and Amanda Burns into witches, which resulted in several deaths, including the entire coven in question.
