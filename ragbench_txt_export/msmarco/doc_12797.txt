Lamezia Terme Airport is the main airport of the southern Italian region of Calabria. The airport is located southwest of Lamezia Terme, after Reggio Calabria and Catanzaro it’s the third-biggest city of Calabria. The city of Lamezia Terme consists of three districts, namely Nicastro, Sambiase and Sant’Eufemia Lamezia. The A3 motorway is a short drive from Lamezia Terme Airport, making the airport easily reachable from the north and south of Italy. For a taxi to Lamezia Terme (Nicastro) you pay about € 20; the travel time is 15 minutes. A taxi to the train station of Lamezia Terme in Sant’Eufemia Lamezia cost no more than € 10.
