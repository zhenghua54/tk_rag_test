Pharmacist working in the US with less than 1 year of experience collects an average salary between $49,660 and $117,962 per annum while a pharmacist practicing for 1 to 5 years of experience receives an average salary from $61,118 -to $127,708 per annum. The annual average pharmacist salary in the US according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics is $111,570. This is equal to the hourly rate of $53.64. The organization predicts that the number of employees for this type of job from 2010 to 2020 will increase by 25%, where 68,700 new jobs will be created.
