Resetting your D-Link Router. Power on the D-Link router. Locate the small toothpick sized hole on the back of your D-Link router, usually found to the left of the power jack. This hole contains a small button. Press down on the button using a toothpick (or an object of equal size). Enter 192.168.0.1 into your address bar. Log in to your router using admin as the user ID and leave the password field blank. Click the Tools tab located at the top of the page. A page titled Admin will pop up.
