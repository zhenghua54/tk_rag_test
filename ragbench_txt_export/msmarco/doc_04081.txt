What's New 2014' - Earned Income Tax Credit For filing 2014 tax returns: You can qualify for EIC based on no children, 1 child, 2 children, and 3 or more children. $43,756 ($49,186 if married filing jointly) if there are two qualifying children. $46,997 ($52,427 if married filing jointly) if there are 3 or more qualifying children.
