In the field of foreign affairs, foreign policy and diplomacy are both important topics and knowing the difference between them is very important. States cannot exist in idleness without the assistance of other states for its survival as well as development especially in such a globalized arena. A foreign policy basically refers to the stance and strategies which are adopted by a state with the intention of promoting its national interest. The national interest of a country can differ from one country to another. However, in general, a country strives for sovereignty and prosperity
