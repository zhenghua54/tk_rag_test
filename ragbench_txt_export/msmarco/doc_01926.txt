SQAS (Safety & Quality Assessment System) is a system to evaluate the quality, safety, security and environmental performance of Logistics Service Providers (LSP's) and Chemical Distributors in a uniform manner by single standardised assessments carried out by independent assessors using a standard questionnaire. The SQAS – ROAD – Questionnaire is a way to evaluate security, health, environmental and quality aspects. A total of 443 questions add up to a maximum of 557 points.
