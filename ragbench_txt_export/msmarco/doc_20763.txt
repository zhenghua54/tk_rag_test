Delaware Temperature Records. The climate of Delaware is humid temperate with hot summers and mild winters. The mean annual temperature ranges from 55 F in New Castle County to 56 F over Kent and Sussex counties. 1 Record Coldest year in Delaware - 1904 / Avg temperature 51.35 degrees. 2  Delaware average Summer temperature (June, July, August) - 74.2 F degrees, 16th warmest U.S. summer state. 3  Delaware average Winter temperature (December, January, February) - 36.1 F degrees, 34th coldest U.S. winter state.
