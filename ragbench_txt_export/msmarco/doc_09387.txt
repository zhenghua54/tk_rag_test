Ovaries: Removed both. Re: vivelle patch and weight gain!!!!! It is not the patch itself that causes weight gain, but hormone imbalance can cause weight gain. Many times women will blame it on the estrogen, but it might be from menopause. thyroid issues or too much or too little estrogen. You can read more about this and much more in the articles at www.hormonejungle.com. One reason to consider if your patch is making you gain weight-get your thyroid levels checked! If your thyroid is low, or if you're using thyroid replacement hormones, and you do not have progesterone on board, then the estrogen will compete with thyroid for binding sites.
