Singulair side-effects do not include direct weight loss or weight gain. Side effects that may occur include headache, weakness, tiredness, trouble sleeping, cough, dizziness, indigestion, nausea or stomach upset and a stuffy nose. Some of these side effects, such as tiredness or weakness, may interfere with the exercise regimen you use for weight loss.
