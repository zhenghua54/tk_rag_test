Although it varies, the average online personal trainer course will take between 6 and 12 weeks to complete. Once the course work is complete all you will need to do is pass the exam to obtain your certification. An average training program takes around 6 months to complete. Many gyms and fitness clubs are now accepting a College or University degree in a fitness related field instead of requiring you to become certified. These take from two years for a diploma to over four for a bachelors degree.
