The certification also determines how much the medical laboratory scientist will be. A scientist who is a Certified Clinical Research Associate is among the high earners with a salary of $49,706 to $103,910 per year, while a Certified Clinical Research Coordinator makes $47,500 to $98,971 a year. Experience does pay, as with any other occupation. For a scientist with one to four years of experience, the annual salary ranges from R220,970 to R474,400 per year. Meanwhile, the medical laboratory scientist’s salary for those with five to nine years’ experience is R281,875 to R586,088.
