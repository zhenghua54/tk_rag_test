Sage may help reduce menopausal hot flashes. Sage, Salvia officinalis, is a desert herb native to the Mediterranean. It prefers shallow, rocky soils and large amounts of sun. Cooks rely on sage for its distinct flavor and aroma, and the herb also offers a number of potential health and medicinal benefits. Antioxidant effects of sage tea may improve liver function, according to an animal study published in the February 2005 issue of the Journal of Ethnopharmacology.. In the study, sage tea was administered in place of water for 14 days.
