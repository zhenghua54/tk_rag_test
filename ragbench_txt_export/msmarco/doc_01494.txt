You and your family will be considered if your annual total income is between 30-50% of the median income for Calvert and St. Mary's Counties. How much does a new construction Habitat house cost? A Habitat house costs approximately $125,000-$145,000 to build. The average monthly payment is between $600 and $650 depending on house style, location and length of the mortgage; this includes an escrow payment for taxes, termite bond and insurance, but does not include any utilities. How does a Habitat mortgage work? Habitat acts as a lender for a 30 year interest free mortgage.
