The Initial Franchise Fee. The initial (or ‘upfront’) fee is paid by a new franchisee to the franchisor, and this is the one-off payment made to get into the franchise. Accordingly, it is usually called the franchise fee. Where the ongoing fee is a flat one, or where the fee is gathered by means of a mark-up on products supplied, skimming is less of a concern but even then it can happen.
