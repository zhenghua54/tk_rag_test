Rafe /rafe/ [1 syll.] as a boys' name is pronounced rayf. It is of Old German origin, and the meaning of Rafe is counsel of the wolf. From Raedwulf. Rafe is an uncommonly occurring first name for men. Rafe is an equivalently uncommon surname too for both adults and children. (2000 U.S. Census).
