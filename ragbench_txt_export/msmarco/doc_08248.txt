The customer does not have to be in the same room within the hotel to meet the 90/180 day requirement. For additional information on permanent residents, see TSB-M-03(1)S, Sales Tax on Rent for Hotel Occupancy and the Exception for Permanent Residents. The room rate or rental charge is the amount that guests must pay to stay in the hotel room (or to have the right to use the hotel room). This amount is taxable at the full state and local sales tax rate. See Tax Bulletin Sales Tax Rates, Additional Taxes, and Fees (TB-ST-825) .
