The story behind the invention of the teddy bear is quite interesting. The idea for creating this stuffed toy came from a famous moment in the life of President Theodore Roosevelt, who was known by the nickname “teddy”. In 1902 some of the president’s attendants hunted a black bear with hounds. Teddy bears are soft stuffed toy that are designed to look like a bear. They are popular gifts to show affection, collected by teddy bear enthusiasts and are also much loved by children all around the world. It is estimated that the retail sales for teddy bears in 2011 will top $1.5 billion.
