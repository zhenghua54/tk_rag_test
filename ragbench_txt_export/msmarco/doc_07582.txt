In Domestic Dogs. All puppies, no matter the breed lose their baby teeth from 3 to 4 months of age and they have generally lost all of them by the time they're 6 months old. If a puppy still … has baby teeth by the time it's 10 months old (or older) it's said to have retained baby teeth. The do lose their teeth with age.
