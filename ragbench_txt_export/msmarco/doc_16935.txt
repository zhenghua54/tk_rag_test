Burke /bur-ke/ [2 sylls.] as a boys' name is pronounced berk. It is of Old French origin, and the meaning of Burke is from the fortified settlement. See also Berkeley. Kreatif forms: Bubke, Bucke, Burbe. Burke is a rare given name for males but a very popular surname for both adults and children (#229 out of 150436, Top 1%). (2000 U.S. Census).
