Before doing any kind of cleaning, you need to make sure that the oven is turned off and unplugged. If it has just been used, then you must also wait until it has cooled down. To clean the exterior, wipe it with a soft damp sponge or lint-free cloth. You may use a non-abrasive cleaner, but do not spray it directly onto the oven surface. Instead, apply the cleanser to the sponge or cloth. For cleaning the glass door, you can use a non-abrasive glass cleaner with a soft damp sponge
