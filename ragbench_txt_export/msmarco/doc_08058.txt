As a court reporter, you're responsible for the accurate record of a legal proceeding, which means you have a lot of responsibility and liability. Your business insurance can help protect you from lawsuits, damaged property, and other costs that could force your small business into bankruptcy. Business Owner's Policies (BOPs) package General Liability and Property Insurance in one policy, which can be a good deal for court reporter small businesses looking to save money on their business insurance. Insurers sell BOPs to small businesses that meet certain criteria and are considered lower-risk..
