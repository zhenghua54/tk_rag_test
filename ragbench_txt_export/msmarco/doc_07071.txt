Undergraduate Tuition. Wayne State University tuition is $10,094 per year for in-state residents. This is 57% more expensive than the national average public four year tuition of $6,429. The cost is $7,174 and 42% cheaper than the average Michigan tuition of $17,268 for 4 year colleges. Tuition ranks 16th in Michigan amongst 4 year colleges for affordability and is the 51st most expensive 4 year college in the state.
