A doctor that deals *only* with the ears would be an otologist. The previous poster said Otolaryngologists, which is a doctor who deals with both the ear and throat. Most often, you'll see what's commonly referred to as an ENT (ear, nose, and throat doctor) which technically would be an otorhinolaryngologist. Oto=ear, rhino=nose, laryng=throat.
