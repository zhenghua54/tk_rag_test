Description. cmap = rgb2hsv(M) converts an RGB colormap M to an HSV colormap cmap. Both colormaps are m-by-3 matrices. The elements of both colormaps are in the range 0 to 1. The columns of the input matrix M represent intensities of red, green, and blue, respectively. RGB is an m-by-n-by-3 image array whose three planes contain the red, green, and blue components for the image.
