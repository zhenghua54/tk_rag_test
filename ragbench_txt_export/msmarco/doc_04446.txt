The Republic of the Union of Myanmar known as Burma is located in southeast Asia. Detailed printable map of Myanmar / Burma and information arount the the sovereign state is proved here. Around 300 BC, the region of southern Burma already had a rich civilization called the Mon, whose people lived on the banks of the Salween and Sittaung Rivers. In 100 BC, the Pyu people settled in northern Burma, building city states from the 1st century BC to the 9th century AD.
