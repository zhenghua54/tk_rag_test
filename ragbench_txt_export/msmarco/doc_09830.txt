According to a bilirubin chart in newborn babies, the normal values that have been highlighted are: Premature Babies. 1  Less than 24 hours: Below 8.0 mg/ dl (below 137 mmol/ l). 2  Less than 48 hours: Below 12.0 mg/ dl (below 205 mmol/ l). 3  Aged between 3 and 5 days: Below 15.0 mg/ dl (below 256 mmol/ l). A bilirubin chart newborn babies is like a graph or a chart, which can help to measure the levels of bilirubin in a newborn baby's blood.
