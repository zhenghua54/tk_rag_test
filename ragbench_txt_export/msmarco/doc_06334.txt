Salary Outlook. Pharmacy technicians earned a median annual wage of $29,650 in 2013, according to the BLS. The best-paid earned $43,230, while the lowest-paid earned $20,640. Best Paying Cities for Pharmacy Techs. The highest paid in the pharmacy technician profession work in the metropolitan areas of Oakland, California, San Jose, California, and Napa, California.
