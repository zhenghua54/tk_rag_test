Salary. Construction workers perform the important task of building our infrastructure, so their median salary – one that’s just over $30,000 – may seem surprising. In 2013, the highest paid in the profession earned $58,900 per year, and the lowest paid earned less than $20,000. The average salary for construction workers in 2013 was $35,020. The metropolitan areas that best pay their construction workers are Leominster, Massachusetts; Gary, Indiana; and Brockton, Massachusetts
