Geography is also a key factor in compensation. Casino surveillance operators working in Connecticut reported average annual wages of $46,080, followed closely by operators in Indiana at $43,290. Conversely, those working in South Dakota and West Virginia reported average annual wages of $26,320 and $26,540, respectively. According to the bureau, the type of employer is a major salary factor. Local governments employed 2,900 casino surveillance operators at an annual average wage of $33,350.
