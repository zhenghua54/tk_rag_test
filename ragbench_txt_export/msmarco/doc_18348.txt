Undergraduate Tuition. San Diego State University tuition is $5,472 per year for in-state residents. This is 15% cheaper than the national average public four year tuition of $6,429. The cost is $15,162 and 73% cheaper than the average California tuition of $20,634 for 4 year colleges. Tuition ranks 19th in California amongst 4 year colleges for affordability and is the 163rd most expensive 4 year college in the state.
