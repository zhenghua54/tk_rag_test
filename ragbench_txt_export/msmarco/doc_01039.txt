Generally, the Pipefitter is responsible for all aspects of installing and maintaining pipe systems, pipe supports, and related hydraulic and pneumatic equipment for steam, hot water, heating, cooling, lubricating, and industrial production and processing systems. The ability to assemble industrial piping modules, high pressure piping systems, food grade, and high purity systems is dependent upon the skills and abilities of the welder. The pipefitter apprentice undergoes a thorough and technically advanced welding program.
