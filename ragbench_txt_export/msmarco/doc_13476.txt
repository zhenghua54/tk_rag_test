Other, less serious side effects may be more likely to occur. Continue to use isoproterenol inhalation and talk to your doctor if you experience. 1  headache, dizziness, lightheadedness, or insomnia; 2  tremor or nervousness; 3  sweating; 4  nausea, vomiting, or diarrhea; or. 5  dry mouth. It works by relaxing muscles in the airways to improve breathing. Isoproterenol inhalation is used to treat conditions such as asthma, bronchitis, and emphysema. Isoproterenol inhalation may also be used for conditions other than those listed in this medication guide.
