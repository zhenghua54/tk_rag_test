Blood cells that engulf and digest bacteria and fungi; an important part of the body's defense system. Classified under: Nouns denoting body parts. Synonyms: leucocyte; leukocyte; WBC; white blood cell; white blood corpuscle; white cell; white corpuscle. Hypernyms (leukocyte is a kind of...): blood cell; blood corpuscle; corpuscle (either of two types of cells (erythrocytes and leukocytes) and sometimes including platelets). free phagocyte (a phagocyte that circulates in the blood).
