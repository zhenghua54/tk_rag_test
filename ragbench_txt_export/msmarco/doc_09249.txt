Instructions. 1  In a medium saucepan over medium heat, whisk together the half-and-half, sugar, Calvados, vanilla bean, and salt. 2  In a medium bowl, lightly whisk the egg yolks. 3  Remove from heat; using a fine-mesh sieve, strain the mixture into a stainless steel bowl set in an ice bath. 1 Spoon the batter into the prepared pan. 2  In a separate medium bowl, combine the almonds, oats, remaining 2/3 cup flour, remaining 2/3 cup brown sugar, remaining 1 teaspoon cinnamon, and remaining 2 tablespoons melted butter. 3  Stir with a fork until mixture is crumbly. 4  Sprinkle mixture evenly over top of batter in pan.
