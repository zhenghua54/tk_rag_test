The 2009 survey by the Institute for Corporate Productivity (i4cp) shows that employers are spending an average of more than $5,000 per executive or manager to provide external outplacement support for a period of three to six months-more than double the cost of RiseSmart Transition Concierge outplacement service. RiseSmart, based in Silicon Valley, is transforming the way companies provide outplacement services and individuals use the Web to find jobs. The San Jose Mercury News says, RiseSmart typifies the valley's knack for using technology to disrupt standard business practices..
