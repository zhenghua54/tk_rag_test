The institution was founded as Monmouth University by early pioneers on Jan. 18, 1856, only seven years after the Territorial Government of Oregon was organized in 1849. Western Oregon University 1997. Recognizing WOU's size and the quality of its academic programs, staff, faculty and students, Governor John Kitzhaber signed a bill changing the name of Western Oregon State College to Western Oregon University. Enrollment reached a record high of 4,088.
