One of the common names of Casuarina species, ‘she-oak’, widely used in Australia, refers to the attractive wood pattern of large lines or rays similar to oak but weaker.BIOLOGYLike other Casuarina species, C. junghuhniana is wind pollinated. Casuarina junghuhniana CasuarinaceaeMiq. TREE MANAGEMENTSeedlings can attain 3 m growth in height per year during the 1st 2-3 years. In plantations with a controlled water regime in Thailand, C. junghuhniana hybrid reaches 20 m in height and 15 cm in diameter in 5 years.
