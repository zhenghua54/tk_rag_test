Hospitals may charge you no more than $1 per page or $100 for the first 100 pages of your record. For records with more than 100 pages, hospitals are allowed to charge 25Â¢ a page for pages in excess of 100 pages or a maximum of $200. They may also charge the cost of mailing the records. Doctors may charge you no more than $1 per page or $100 for your entire medical record, whichever amount is less. If your record is fewer than 10 pages, the doctor is allowed to charge you $10. Doctors are also allowed to charge an administrative fee of no more than $10 or 10% of the copying cost, whichever is less.
