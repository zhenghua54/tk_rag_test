1 Flap – Made up of two movements, a brush and a step done with the same foot. 2  Make sure you can hear two sounds. 3  Commonly called a “fal-lap” to match the two sounds it makes. 4  Shuffle – Made up of two brushes, a front and a back in quick succession. 5  Be sure that your foot is totally relaxed. 1 Brush – Gently brush the ball of your foot against the floor. 2  Be sure your foot is very relaxed, the movement comes from the thigh. 3  Can be done to the front and the back. 4  Step – Put your foot down and shift your weight to that foot, lifting up the other foot if you want.
