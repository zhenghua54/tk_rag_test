An ovulation calculator can help a woman to accurately understand her ovulation cycle. Most women ovulate about 14 days before the expected start of the next menstruation. Pregnancy can occur if sexual intercourse takes place two to four days before ovulation, and at least a day after ovulation. Recording body temperature daily may help a woman keep track of ovulation cycles. If you want to really be accurate about how to check your ovulation cycle you should download one of the apps on your smartphone that fine the ovulation date for you. These apps just ask you for details on your period and keep track of everything for you.
