1 Pheochromocytoma is a rare tumor that forms in the adrenal medulla (the center of the adrenal gland). 2  Paragangliomas form outside the adrenal gland. 3  Certain inherited disorders and changes in certain genes increase the risk of pheochromocytoma or paraganglioma. Localized Pheochromocytoma and Paraganglioma. Treatment of localized benign pheochromocytoma or paraganglioma is usually surgery to completely remove the tumor. If the tumor is in the adrenal gland, the entire adrenal gland is removed.
