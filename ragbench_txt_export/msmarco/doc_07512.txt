Severe alcohol withdrawal symptoms include: In extreme cases, alcohol withdrawal symptoms can be so severe in nature that they become fatal. It is recommended that alcoholics undergo a medical detox when symptoms are considered moderate, severe, or a combination of both. Severe symptoms of alcohol withdrawal are usually experienced by people who have been drinking for a long period of time and acquired a tolerance for alcohol.
