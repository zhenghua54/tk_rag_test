The World of. VISCOSE. Cupro is commonly known as a Japanese based developped material. - It is a kind of Rayon (Viscose) group of Fibres. It is more like Liocell, it is reprocessed cellulose. It is made from regenerated cellulose fiber. Cuprammonium, gives the name to the fibre Cupro, it is used in making of process the wood pulp which is the base material. With the process, the wood pulp is dissolved in an ammoniac copper oxide solution. Cupro fabric breathes like cotton, drapes beautifully, and feels like silk on your skin.
