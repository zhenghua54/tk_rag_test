Fossil fuels are sources of energy that have developed within the earth over millions of years. Because fossil fuels-oil, natural gas, and coal-take so long to form, they are considered nonrenewable. You have undoubtedly heard of fuels such as coal, oil and natural gas. These are the three main types of fossil fuels.
