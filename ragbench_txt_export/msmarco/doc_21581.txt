Swelling after rhinoplasty (Nose job), Nose Tip Surgery. Bruises and the initial swelling will virtually disappear within 14 days of the rhinoplasty surgery. The nose may still swell from time to time for about 2 months. A final, completely stabilised result may be expected after 6–12 months. After the nose surgery you are taken every 2–3 days for a free check up to the clinic. Minimum stay in Prague is 7 days after surgery, recommended 10–14 days. Our Prague rhinoplasty surgeons use dissolving stitches, which are placed inside of your nose and do not need to be removed.
