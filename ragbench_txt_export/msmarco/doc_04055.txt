Steps to add additional email account to iPad mini. 1. From the iPad mini Home screen, tap Settings. 2.Tap on Mail, Contacts, Calendars. 3.Tap on Add Account. 4. Tap on the account type you wish to use, you can choose from Microsoft Exchange ， Gmail ， Yahoo! Mail and other email services. 5. Enter your new additional email account information.
