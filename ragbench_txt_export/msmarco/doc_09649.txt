Parkinson’s is a chronic, progressive disease characterized by motor symptoms including tremors, muscle stiffness, and slowed movement. Oct. 4, 2010 -- Parkinson’s disease is not considered fatal, but people with Parkinson’s have a shorter life expectancy than the general population.
