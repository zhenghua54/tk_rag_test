Ready In. 1  Place chunks of venison into a shallow baking dish, and pour enough apple cider in to cover them. 2  Cover, and refrigerate for 2 hours. 3  Remove, and pat dry. 4  Discard apple cider, and return venison to the dish. 1 Remove meat from the refrigerator, and let stand for 30 minutes, or until no longer chilled. 2  Wrap each chunk of venison in a slice of bacon, and secure with toothpicks. 3  Brush the grill grate with olive oil when hot, and place venison pieces on the grill so they are not touching.
