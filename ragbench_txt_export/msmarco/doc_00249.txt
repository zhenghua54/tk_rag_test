Metadata is  data about data. Two types of metadata exist: structural metadata and descriptive metadata. Structural metadata is data about the containers of data. Descriptive metadata uses individual instances of application data or the data content. While the metadata application is manyfold covering a large variety of fields, there are specialised and well-accepted models to specify types of metadata.
