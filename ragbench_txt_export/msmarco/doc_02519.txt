Along with a patient’s age, sex, and past and current medical issues, performance status (PS) is one of the most important factors that is categorized for patients. The reason. is so important is that it is predictive of how a patient will do with and without treatment for cancer. In fact, it is more predictive of prognosis and ability to tolerate the rigors of potentially challenging anti-cancer therapy than other intuitively relevant factors like a patient’s chronologic age.
