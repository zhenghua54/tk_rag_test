The Advantages of Biofuels over Fossil Fuels. There are many advantages of biofuels over fossil fuels that make the alternative fuel source an attractive option now and in the future. Biofuels have been in production for the last few decades and have really begun to take off with the awareness of global warming going around the world. 1 Biofuels do not release as much carbon as fossil fuels do, and because of this, there are fewer harmful emissions out of biofuels. 2  Renewable. 3  Being made from organic materials, and even organic waste, there is practically an infinite amount of biofuels available.
