Investopedia is an Internet site devoted to investing education based in Edmonton in Alberta, Canada started by Cory Janssen and Cory Wagner in June 1999. On September 9, 2011 Investopedia released a free iPhone App. The App gives users access to Investopedia's full financial dictionary, articles, and other content. Investopedia's App has over 211,000 downloads and is the #25 free Finance App in iTunes.
