While the term couch surfing loosely refers simply to staying with hosts while you travel, most people associate the concept with couchsurfing.com -- a social site to help budget travelers meet hosts in different countries. The idea behind couch surfing is to leverage the hospitality of friendly people around the world who open their homes to travelers -- an act of kindness that dates back millenia. Couch surfing is a remarkable way to travel for cheap, and travelers of all budgets take advantage.
