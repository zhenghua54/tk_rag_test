Rating Newest Oldest. Best Answer: They will stop drinking milk from her around 3-4 weeks which is when they should start to eat regular rabbit pellets. If they do not stop by then don't worry, the mom will make them stop. A rabbit will only take care of her young 1-2 times per day, in the morning and, or evening. This is because in the wild if they went and into their hole every couple hours to feed them predators would find out where the nest is.
