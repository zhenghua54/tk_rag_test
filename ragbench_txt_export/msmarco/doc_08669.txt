xenophobia (zen´əfō´bēə) , n an anx-iety disorder characterized by a pervasive, irrational fear or uneasiness in the presence of strangers, especially foreigners, or in new surroundings. xenophobia. [-fō′bē·ə]. Etymology: Gk, xenos + phobos, fear. an anxiety disorder characterized by a pervasive, irrational fear or uneasiness in the presence of strangers, especially foreigners, or in new surroundings. Psychiatry An abnormal/morbid fear of strangers or foreigner
