Uber is an on-demand car service that allows you to request private drivers through applications for iPhone and Android devices. The service utilizes dispatch software to send the nearest driver to your location. 1 The Uber app will provide you with the phone number of your driver. 2  You can use this contact the driver if you have any special considerations. 3  If you need to cancel your reservation, you will be charged a $5-$10 fee if you cancel after five minutes.
