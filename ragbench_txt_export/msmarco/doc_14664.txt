Why Renewable Energy is important. Climate change and the need to manage diminishing fossil fuel reserves are, today, two of the biggest challenges facing the planet. The UK is a signatory of the Kyoto Protocol and is actively involved in measures to meet our commitment of a 12.5 per cent cut in greenhouse gas emissions by the period 2008-12. But there is no single solution for the UK or the 155 other countries also aiming to reduce their emissions.
