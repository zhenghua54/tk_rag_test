The reason why you may want to use Zendesk is because you may be looking for a faster and better way to interact with your customers. You may also want your company to be able to interact with your customers using any communication channel, be it through social media, chat or email. The company Zendesk inc. was founded by Morten Primdahl, Alexander Aghassipour and Mikkel Svane. By 2008 it had received $500,000 in funding. In 2009 a second series of funding came in the amount of $6 million allowing the company to move to San Francisco and establishing its headquarters.
