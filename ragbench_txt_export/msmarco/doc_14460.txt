Instructions. Take Provera for 5 to 10 days when you want to induce your cycle. The cycle will start 2 to 3 days after you stop taking Provera. Provera is a drug used to regulate and induce a menstrual cycle. Other drugs include the emergency contraceptive pill, available in different brand names, and the day-after pill. Save. A normal menstrual cycle will be between 28 and 40 days. Some women are regular, and some are irregular. There are various ways to regulate a menstrual cycle and cause it to start early or delay its onset. The methods include pills, herbs and other related activities
