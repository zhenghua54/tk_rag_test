Every 4 or 5 years the plaster needs a thin coat of limewash to maintain its strength. Lime plaster is the best finish to use on earth walls because: 1. It shrinks and swells with heat in a similar way to earth. A thin layer of lime plaster on earth will not suffer serious cracks like cement stucco. It is also easy to repair lime plaster with a thin layer of plaster or limewash.
