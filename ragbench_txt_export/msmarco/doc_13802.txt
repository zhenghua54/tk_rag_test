Situated in the Caribbean Sea, Jamaica is an island nation of the Greater Antilles, 145 mi in length, up to 50 mi in width and 4,243 sq mi in area. Jamaica is a Commonwealth realm with the Monarchy of Jamaica holding ultimate executive power, where Queen Elizabeth II is the current head of state and Queen of Jamaica. Jamaica is an island nation in the Caribbean Sea, and is part of the Greater Antilles.
