Basil is an annual herb that lasts one year. When planning your herb garden, it’s important to keep in mind the growth habits of each plant. Some herbs are annuals, some are perennials, and some form small evergreen shrubs. Here’s a guide to some popular herbs for kitchen gardens: If a perennial herb isn’t winter hardy in your area, you can still grow it in pots that you bring indoors in the fall, or just treat it like an annual and replant next spring. Perennial herbs include: 1  Caraway (blooms in second year). 2  Catnip. 3  Chicory. 4  Chives.
