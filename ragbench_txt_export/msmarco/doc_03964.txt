Davy was born on December 17th, 1778 in Penzance, Cornwall, England. He received his education in Penzance and Truro. In 1794, he lost his father and in an effort to support his family, he became an apprentice to J.Binghan Borlase, a surgeon. First Electric Light. In 1809, Humphry Davy invented the very first electric light. He did this by connecting two wires to a single battery and then attatching a charcoal strip between the other two ends of the wires. The charged carbon then glowed, making the very first arc lamp.
