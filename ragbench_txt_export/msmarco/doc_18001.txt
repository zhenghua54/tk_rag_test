Quartz is one of the most well-known minerals on earth. It occurs in basically all mineral environment s, and is the important constituent of many rock s. Quartz is also the most varied of all minerals, occurring in all different forms, habits, and colors. There are more variety names given to Quartz than any other mineral. - The blue variety of Quartz, which is very uncommon in nature and rarely in crystal form. Most Blue Quartz is what is popularly known as Aqua Aura, essentially clear Rock Crystal synthetically irradiated with gold to form a deep sky blue color.
