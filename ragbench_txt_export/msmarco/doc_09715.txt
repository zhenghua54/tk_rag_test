As mentioned above, there is a very wide range of fees for hair transplantation in Dubai. Some low key clinics may charge AED 5 per graft (approximately US $ 1.30). At the other end of the spectrum, there are clinics charging up to AED 35 plus per graft (US $ 10). Good clinics with experienced and well trained doctors will charge an average of AED 12 to 15 per graft (US $ 3 TO 4 approximately). If you are planning to have hair transplantation in Dubai, then you must do your due diligence. Seeing the results is the most confident building aspect of your selection.
