This procedure is useful for the diagnosis of subtle joint abnormalities which may not be obvious in a standard MRI scan. MR arthrography allows the physician to better identify tears and provides patients with an accurate diagnosis to determine whether or not surgery is needed. Patient referred for 3T MRI Arthrography of the shoulder for suspicion of labral injury. Injection of Gadolinium based contrast agent allowed for visualization of the labrum and subsequent diagnosis of posterior tear.
