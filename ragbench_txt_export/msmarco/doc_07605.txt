Individual money orders are available for values up to $1,000. You cannot exceed $3,000 of money order purchases in a day unless you fill out extra forms and show a photo ID. Your postal clerk will be able to provide you with the proper paperwork. You may pay for the money order with a debit card, traveler's checks, or cash. 1  In addition to the dollar amount of the money order, you will also have to pay an additional money order fee. 2  In the United States, an international money order fee costs $4.50. 3  This fee will be added to your total when you pay the clerk.
