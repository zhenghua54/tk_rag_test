It changes size and shape according to is position of the body and the amount of food inside. The stomach is about 12 inches (30.5 cm) long and is 6 inches. (15.2 cm) wide at its widest point. The stomach's capacity is about 1 qt (0.94 liters) in an adult. Food enters the stomach from the esophagus. The connection between the stomach and the esophagus is called the cardiac sphincter.
