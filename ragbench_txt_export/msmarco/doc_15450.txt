Color: The color of cactus flowers depends on the species of the cactus. The flower can bloom out in white or yellow but there are some that comes out in purple and reddish hues. It’s seen as a symbol of unconditional love because even in harsh conditions, it thrives. What the Cactus Means: Endurance. Looking at cactus spawns inspiration and helps you see life in a better perspective. Even there’s no drop of water in sight and amidst the excruciating heat (or freezing cold), the cactus endures.
