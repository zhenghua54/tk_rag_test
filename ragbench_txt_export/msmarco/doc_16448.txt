The Best time to Visit Komodo Island: June and July are great months to visit Komodo. The waters tend to be more calm for getting there and away. The winds are present, but not very strong, making the days very pleasant and the nights slightly cool. The Lesser Sunda Islands of Indonesia weather, is more distinct. During the months of January, February, March, and the beginning of April, is the rainy season. The Komodo weather during these months typically includes between thirty one and thirty nine inches of rain.
