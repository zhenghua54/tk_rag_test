HOCKLEY COUNTY. HOCKLEY COUNTY. Hockley County (B-9) is in northwestern Texas, south of the Panhandle and on the Llano Estacado, bordered on the east by Lubbock County, on the south by Terry County, on the west by Cochran County, and on the north by Lamb County.
