As you increase your specialty focus as an RN, you also increase your earning power. For example, an RN specializing in anesthesia can earn a starting salary of $110,000 to $130,000 annually, according to a 2009 study of wages reported by the Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetist website. The more education you have as an RN, the more you make starting out, according to the Journal of Nursing. An RN with a bachelor's degree can expect a starting salary of between $40,000 and $50,000 each year.
