Buff is the pale yellow-brown colour of the un-dyed leather of several animals. As an RYB quaternary colour, it is the darker colour produced by an equal mix of the tertiary colours citron and russet. Buff is the traditional colour of the U.S. Army Quartermaster Corps. The U.S. Army Institute of Heraldry specifies a buff tincture for certain coats of arms. Continental Army uniforms: The Buff and Blue. US Chevron-Quartermaster Sergeant Insignia (1902-1909) Buff on black with blue detail.
