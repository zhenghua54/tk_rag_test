1/4 tsp each salt and pepper. Dash or two of hot sauce. 12 boneless, skinless chicken thighs (approx. 2.5 lbs / 1 kg). To make marinade, in a small bowl combine lime juice, oil, molasses, soy sauce, cinnamon, garlic, onions, salt, pepper and hot sauce. Place chicken thigh in a large resealable plastic bag. Add marinade, seal bag and turn several times to coat chicken. Marinate for at least 2 hours.
