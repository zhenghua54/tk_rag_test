Cotton batiste is lightweight, sheer, and delicate fabric. The term batiste is a French word that refers to a fine quality cloth. This type of fabric is similar to cotton lawn, but it is thicker. Its appearance is often described as unassuming, yet elegant. Ad. Cotton batiste is easily found in white, black, and ivory at most craft and fabric stores, as well as from a number of online retailers. Specialty colors such as pastel pink, lemon yellow, baby blue, or seafoam green may be available in some locations.
