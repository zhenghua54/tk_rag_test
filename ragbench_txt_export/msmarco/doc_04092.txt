in laymen s terms a butte is a small flat topped or pointed hill or mountain a mesa is a medium size flat topped hill or mountain and a plateau is a really big flat topped hill or mountain however the true definitions are elegant and fascinating and encompass some of the most amazing landscape in the southwest buttes are geologic features up to hundreds of feet high with steep sides narrow pointed tops or very small flat tops the definition difference between a butte and a mesa most likely originated right here in the southwest early settlers said that if you could find game on top it was a mesa eventually that definition translated to the idea that if you could graze cattle and find water it was a mesa not a butte
