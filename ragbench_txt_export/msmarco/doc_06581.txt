To study in the UK as an adult student you will need to acquire a Tier 4 visa. If you are yet to be accepted into an institution, then you will need a prospective student visa, and if the duration of your stay will be less than six months, then you should apply for a Student Visitor visa. The UK is home to some of most prestigious institutions of higher education in the world, and it, therefore, is one of the most popular study abroad destinations in the world. More than 26,000 Indian students come to the UK to study each year.
