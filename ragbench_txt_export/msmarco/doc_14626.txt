Cerebral palsy life expectancy rates are hard to determine. Let's examine cerebral palsy life expectancy issues more closely. Life Expectancy for Those with Cerebral Palsy. The first thing that you need to understand when examining cerebral palsy and life expectancy rates is that there are no hard and fast statistics. The life expectancy for a person with cerebral palsy can range from 30 years of age to age 60 or 70.
