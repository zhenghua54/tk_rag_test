South Bend. Located on the scenic shores of the Willapa River, South Bend is the county seat of Pacific County. The beautiful Pacific County Courthouse overlooks the town and is open to the public for self-guided tours. Explore the unique history of Pacific County at the Pacific County Historical Society Museum. Fresh Willapa Bay oysters and seafood are readily available at South Bend restaurants, seafood stores and canneries. The town of South Bend had its beginnings in 1869 when the Riddell brothers, Valentine and John, built a sawmill on the bend of the Willapa River.
