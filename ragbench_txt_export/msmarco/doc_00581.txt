Kidney infection, also known as renal infection or pyelonephritis, is a type of urinary tract infection (UTI) that is usually caused caused by E. coli bacteria. The bacteria may have spread from the bladder or the urethra to one of the kidneys, infecting it. 1 Kidney stones-individuals with kidney stones have a higher risk of developing kidney infection. 2  Kidney stones are the result of a build-up of dissolved minerals on the inner lining of the kidneys. 3  Enlarged prostate-males with an enlarged prostate have a higher risk of developing kidney infections.
