When you look at what body system a organ belongs you look at its main function. Its main function is to produce hormones that produce white blood cells, therefore in my opinion the thymus as apart of the endocrine system. Alot of these functions overlap into different body systems The thymus converts lymphocytes into T-cells so it can look at is as apart of the immune system. Online medical dictionaries see the the thymus as part of the lymphatic system because it is made up of lymphatic tissue.
