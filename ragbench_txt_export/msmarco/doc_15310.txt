Vinyl Must be Thicker than Aluminum Windows. This is necessary in order to gain the strength they need. For noise abatement windows this is even more true. To stop as much noise as we do with our Soundproof Window frames (which are 1-1/8″ thick), there would have to be 3″ of vinyl frame. Vinyl and wood windows now have well over 70% of the residential window market. Aluminum windows have the overwhelming market share for commercial buildings.
