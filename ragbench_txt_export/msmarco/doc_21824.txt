Asian Ginseng Vs. The main difference between Asian Panax ginseng and American ginseng varieties is that Panax is a more warming Yang stimulant, whereas American is a more Yin, cooling and calming tonic. Both are energizing but in different ways. These qualities are largely a result of the temperature in which it grows. The Difference Between Varieties. Panax ginseng is one of the most prized Chi (Qi) tonics used in Chinese herbalism. Consumed for thousands of years as a superior herb, listed as the top major tonic in the herbal classic the Shennong Ben Cao Jing, it is one of the most widely researched of the Chinese tonics.
