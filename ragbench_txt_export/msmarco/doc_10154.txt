Here we will try to explain the cost of obtaining a U.S. passport. Passport fees can sometimes be confusing with different fee structure for different passport services such as routine and expedited passport services. The passport processing time for routine passport service is 4 – 6 weeks and expedited services 3 – 4 weeks. Routine Service is for U.S. citizen who are looking to get a passport and does not have a specific time to get the passport back.
