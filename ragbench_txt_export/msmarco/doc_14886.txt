Large amounts of money in India are often written in terms of crores. For example, 100,000,000 (one hundred million) Indian rupees is written as 'ten crore rupees ' or ' ₹ 10 crore' or 'Rs 10 crore'. Trillions of ₹ are often written or spoken of in terms of lakh crore.
