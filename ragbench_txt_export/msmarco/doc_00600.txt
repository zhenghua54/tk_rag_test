Additional public costs may also be incurred in the form of subsidized guardianship and residential treatment not covered through Medicaid. The total annual public cost for a child who has been adopted from care in Oregon is $8,964. This is close to one reported national average of $10,302 (Zill, 2011). The average annual net savings for one child being adopted out of foster care is $17,636 in Oregon, and it is $15,480 nationally. In 2010, AFFEC contributed to the work of getting approximately 132 children and siblings out of foster care and into homes with permanent and loving families.
