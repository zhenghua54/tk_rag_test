It was found in her Lambada album, which was released in 1990. Ivete Sangalo recorded a live version of the song as a bonus track for her 2005 album As Super Novas. It was released on November 2005 as the third single from the album. She also recorded the song on her second live album. The British girl group Spice Girls mention Lambada - either the song or the dance-in their 1998 hit single  Spice Up Your Life .
