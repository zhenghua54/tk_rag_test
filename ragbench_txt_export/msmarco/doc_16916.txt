Experts suspect that both viruses are transmitted to humans through an infected animal's bodily fluids. Examples include: 1  Blood. 2  Butchering or eating infected animals can spread the viruses. 3  Scientists who have operated on infected animals as part of their research have also contracted the virus. 4  Waste products. A milder strain of Ebola has been discovered in monkeys and pigs in the Philippines. Marburg virus has been found in monkeys, chimps and fruit bats in Africa.
