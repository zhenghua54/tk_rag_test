Diagnosing Yellow Leaves on Forsythia. A forsythia with yellow leaves may be occurring due to any number of diseases. Below are the more common ones: 1  Yellow veins may indicate tobacco ringspot virus or arabis mosaic virus. 2  Each is generally introduced through nematodes. 1 Yellow, black or brown spots that form a larger necrotic tissue can mean that forsythia with yellow leaves are caused by anthracnose, one of the most common fungal diseases on ornamental plants. 2  The yellow tissue may also be colonized by tiny fruiting bodies.
