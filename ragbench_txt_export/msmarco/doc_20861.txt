Job Description for Boilermaker. A boilermaker deals with all things related to boilers. This includes construction, assembly, maintenance, and repair of stationary steam boilers and boiler house accessories. Boilermakers must align components or plate sections while assembling vats and framer tank for boilers. Pay by Experience Level for. Median of all compensation (including tips, bonus, and overtime) by years of experience. Boilermakers with a lot of experience do not necessarily enjoy more money.
