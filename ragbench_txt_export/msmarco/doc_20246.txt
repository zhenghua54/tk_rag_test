Delaney /de-la-ney/ [3 sylls.] as a boys' name (also used more widely as girls' name Delaney) is pronounced de-LANE-ee. It is of Old French and Irish origin, and the meaning of Delaney is from the alder grove; black defiance. Can derive from the Norman French name del aunaie, or from an Irish surname from an old name Dubhshlaine, or from a place name referring to the Slaney river. Kreatif forms: Denlaney, Dolaney, Doulaney.
