Dextroamphetamine is a psychostimulant drug that is approved by the Food and Drug Administration for the treatment of Attention deficit-hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) and narcolepsy. The drug is available as a generic drug called Dextroamphetamine or under various brand names such as Dexedrine or Dextrostat. The dextroamphetamine salts constitute about 75% of the ADHD drug known as Adderall and is the active metabolite of the drug lisdexamfetamine also known by the brand name Vyvanse.
