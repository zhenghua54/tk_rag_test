Dealer Costs That Affect the MSRP. The invoice price is the total investment of the car dealership for the car, meaning the total amount of money they pay for the vehicle. The dealer invoice is the price the manufacturer bills the dealer for the vehicles they purchase, plus any options equipped. This is in essence the dealer's cost, and is not generally shared with the customer.
