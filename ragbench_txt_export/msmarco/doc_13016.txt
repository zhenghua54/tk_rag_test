1 Clipping: Also known as truncation or shortening, clipping is the word formation process which consists in the reduction of a word to one of its parts, e.g. fax (facsimile), flu (influenza) and bot (robot). 2  Clipped words may not retain their original meaning. A blend is a word formed by joining parts of two or more older words. An example is smog, which comes from smoke and fog, or brunch, which comes from 'breakfast' and 'lunch'.
