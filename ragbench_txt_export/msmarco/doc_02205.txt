Kaylee /kay-lee/ [2 sylls.] as a girls' name is pronounced KAY-lee. It is of Gaelic origin, and the meaning of Kaylee is slender. From Caollaidhe. Variant of Kayla with the popular-ley ending, much favored in spelling variants. May also be considered a combination of Kay with Lee. See also Cayla and Kaylin. Kaylee is a very popular first name for females (#1800 out of 4276, Top 42%) but a unique surname for all people. (2000 U.S. Census). Charted below is the birth name popularity of Kaylee for girls. Kaylee entered the list in 1980-1989 and reached its top rank of #26 in the U.S. in the year 2009, and is presently at #52.
