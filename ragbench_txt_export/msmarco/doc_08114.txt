Apple stores are the envy of many retails with $4,542 sales per square foot this fiscal year to date according to the Wall Street Journal and down from $4,754 this time last year. In 2012 Apple saw a sales per square foot of $5,971. In the clothing industry, according to RetailSails, sales per square foot for Nordstrom is $400, compared with just $173 for Macy’s. At $350, Saks Fifth Avenue’s sales per square foot approaches Nordstrom’s. But, then look at Ross stores where the figure was $355 in 2012.
