Annual Total Cost. The total list price for in-state California residents to go to San Diego State University is $23,373 for the 2014/2015 academic year. Out-of-state students who don't possess California residence can expect a one year cost of $34,533. San Diego State University tuition is $5,472 per year for in-state residents. This is 15% cheaper than the national average public four year tuition of $6,429.
