The Continuing Struggle for Civil Rights. The civil rights movement in the. American South was one of the most significant and successful social movements in the modern world. Black Georgians formed part of this southern movement for full civil rights and the wider national struggle for racial equality. In her book Praying for Sheetrock Melissa Fay Greene describes how in McIntosh County in the early 1970s, the epic of the civil rights movement was still a fabulous tale about distant places to the black people of McIntosh..
