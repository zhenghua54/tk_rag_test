Verizon's Share Everything family data plan is getting a little more affordable. Verizon's Share Everything plans add a new option Verizon. The carrier said that starting Tuesday, Verizon customers will be able to sign up for a $60 Share Everything plan, $20 less than the current cheapest plan. Customers will only get half as much data -- 250 megabytes vs. the $80, 500 MB plan.
