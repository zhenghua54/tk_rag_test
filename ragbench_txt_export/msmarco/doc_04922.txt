Undergraduate Tuition. The University of Texas at Austin tuition is $9,830 per year for in-state residents. This is 53% more expensive than the national average public four year tuition of $6,429. The cost is $4,736 and 33% cheaper than the average Texas tuition of $14,566 for 4 year colleges. Annual Total Cost. The total list price for in-state Texas residents to go to UT Austin is $22,036 for the 2014/2015 academic year. Out-of-state students who don't possess Texas residence can expect a one year cost of $47,042.
