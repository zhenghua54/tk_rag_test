Nurse practitioner and attorney who specializes in the legal issues affecting medical practices and nurse practitioners. She is the author of 5 books. Ms. Buppert counsels clients, and lectures extensively on reimbursement issues, how to avoid malpractice, and contract negotiation. She is also president of Better Life Health Care Systems, which contracts with businesses and educational institutions for nurse practitioner services. Her Web site is www.buppert.com. NPs should avoid prescribing legend (prescription) drugs for family members, unless the family member is enrolled at a practice where the NP regularly diagnoses and treats patients.
