The Flemish Region and the Walloon Region each comprise five provinces. The Brussels-Capital Region is not a province, nor does it contain any. The three regions are further subdivided into 589 municipalities, which in general consist of several sub-municipalities. The three communities are: 1  the Dutch-speaking Vlaamse Gemeenschap (Flemish Community). 2  the French-speaking Communaute Communauté (Francaise Française). 3  french Community-the German Speaking (Deutschsprachige-gemeinschaft German).  speaking community
