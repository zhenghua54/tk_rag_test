Aztec History. The Aztec were a Nahuatl-speaking people who in the 15th and early 16th centuries ruled a large empire in what is now central and southern Mexico. The Aztec are so called from Aztlan (“Aztlán White”), land an allusion to their place of, origin probably in Northern. mexico The most important official of Tenochtitlan government is often called The Aztec Emperor. The Nahuatl title, Huey Tlatoani (plural huey tlatoque), translates roughly as Great Speaker; the tlatoque (speakers) were an upper class.
