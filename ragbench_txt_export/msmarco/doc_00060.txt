Here’s what you need to know. If you’re interested in a “Netflix for ebooks,” but aren’t sure what to choose, here are a few factors to think about. Price: Scribd’s service is $8.99 per month, while Oyster’s is $9.95 per month. Oyster is in beta, invite-only and doesn’t offer a free trial, while Scribd offers a free one-month trial. There must be something in the water: Just under a month after startup Oyster launched a Netflix-like subscription service for ebooks, the six-year-old document sharing site Scribd officially rolled out its own ebook subscription service on Tuesday.
