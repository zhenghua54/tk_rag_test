Packing peanuts made out of corn. Here's another exciting new use for corn - packing peanuts made out of cornstarch using a revolutionary new process that makes conventional hard-to-get-rid-of polystyrene peanuts obsolete. New cornstarch peanuts are easy to dispose of since they dissolve in water.
