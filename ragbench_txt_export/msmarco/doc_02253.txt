Bermuda is not a party to any double tax agreements. LICENCE FEES. A company with an authorised capital up to US 12,000 pays the sum of US 1,680 per annum. Thereafter there is a sliding scale up to a maximum of US 25,000 on an authorised capital more than US 500 million. COMPLIANCE: AUTHORISED SHARE CAPITAL The standard authorised share capital of a Bermuda exempt company is US 12,000; divided in to 12,000 common voting shares of US 1 which is the maximum capital for the minimum capital duty payable on incorporation and annually thereafter.
