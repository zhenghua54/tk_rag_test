(CS) The OECD has ranked Luxembourg teachers in the number one spot when it comes to wages, with a maximum yearly salary of 128,181 USD, the equivalent of over 98,000 euros. This stands in stark comparison to an OECD average of just 47,721 USD or around 36,700 euros. The compiled data dates from 2010 or the latest available year. Starting salaries in Luxembourg were calculated at 65,171 USD for primary school teachers and 73,777 USD for secondary education. These levels climb to 95,043 USD and 101,775 USD respectively after 15 years of service
