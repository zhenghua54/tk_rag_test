Hummingbird favorites usually but not always have long, tubular blossoms that are red, orange, yellow or blue, especially blue Salvia species; the shape limits insect access to the nectar inside. Hummers like blossoms with lots of concentrated nectar, preferring sucrose. Hummingbird Flowers-The Best 18 Plant Families for Natural Nectar. This is Nature's way. Keep something in bloom continuously during the time hummingbirds are expected in your area.
