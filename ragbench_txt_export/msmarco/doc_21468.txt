Kidney Stones. Cranberry juice can contribute to increased risk of developing kidney stones. Cranberry juice contains high levels of chemicals, called oxalate, that are associated with the formation of certain types of kidney stones. Drinking large quantities of cranberry juice may cause upset stomach and diarrhea. Cranberry juice has a high sugar content, so if you have diabetes or glucose intolerance you should avoid excessive sugar intake by choosing sugar-free cranberry juice.
