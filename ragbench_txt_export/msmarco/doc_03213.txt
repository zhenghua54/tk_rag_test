1 Drain the potatoes and pat them dry thoroughly with a tea towel or paper towels. 2  Toss the potatoes with the remaining 1 teaspoon oil and mix evenly. 3  Arrange the fries in a single layer on a baking sheet and cover the sheet tightly with foil. 4  Bake for 5 minutes, then remove the foil and continue to bake for 10 minutes.
