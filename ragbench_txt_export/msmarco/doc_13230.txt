Permanent Magnet Generators are Highly Durable. As permanent magnet generators do not require a battery to work, these are ideal for homes which are located in remote regions where it is hard to gather spare parts and find qualified mechanics. Permanent magnet generators are relatively easy to set up and these are also small in size which further adds to the mobility and helps in saving space in a place where these are installed.
