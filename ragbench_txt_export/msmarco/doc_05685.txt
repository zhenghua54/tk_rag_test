Miso paste is created from a mixture of soybeans, sea salt and rice koji and then fermented. The fermentation process creates enzyme-rich compounds that are effective in detoxifying and eliminating industrial pollution, radioactivity and artificial chemicals from the soil and food system from your body. 1 Place water in a large pot and bring to a low simmer. 2  Add nori, tofu and mushrooms and simmer for 5-7 minutes. 3  In the meantime, place miso paste into a small bowl, add a little hot water and whisk until smooth. 4  Then add to the soup with green onions and stir.
