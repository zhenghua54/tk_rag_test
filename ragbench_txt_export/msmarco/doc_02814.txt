Read and understand the new legislation on deducting energy efficient appliances. The new legislation on the 2005 Energy Policy Act indicates that taxpayers who invest in energy-efficient appliances can claim tax credits or deductions when doing their taxes for 2011. Fill out Form 5695, which is called the Residential Energy Efficient Property Credit. In order to claim an appliance for a deduction in 2011, for example, the product must have been “placed in service” and used in 2011.
