The residents of Brussels have a fondness for this world renowned little boy. The city also has a rather humorous outlook with regards to Manneken Pis. Manneken Pis is a bronze statuette of a little boy urinating into the fountain basin, which reaches just 61cm tall. In the fifteenth century, the statue played an important role as the first drinking fountain in Brussels.
