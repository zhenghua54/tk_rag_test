The state of Arkansas charges an Individual Income Tax that applies to most forms of retirement income. The only retirement-income sources that are not are subject to income tax in Arkansas are Social Security and railroad retirement benefits. There is a $6,000 deduction on employer sponsored pension plans in Arkansas. All single Arkansas residents with an income of $10,682 a year or higher and married couples with an income of $18,012 a year must file a state income tax return. Taxpayers that make less than $50,000 a year will have to determine their income tax rate using the State Tax Tables.
