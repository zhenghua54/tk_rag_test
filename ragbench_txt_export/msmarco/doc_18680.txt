Komodo dragon-the largest lizard in the world (10 feet); found on Indonesian islands. dragon lizard, giant lizard, Komodo lizard, Varanus komodoensis. monitor lizard, varan, monitor-any of various large tropical carnivorous lizards of Africa and Asia and Australia; fabled to warn of crocodiles. Ko·mo·do dragon. An Indonesian monitor lizard that is the largest living lizard. It can grow up to 10 feet (3 meters) long, has dark skin, and feeds on eggs and small animals.
