In conducting their annual salary survey of 2011, Advance for NPs & PAs, a peer reviewed industry journal, showed that NPs in Syracuse and Buffalo earned an average of $86,711 and $85,227, respectively. Nurse Practitioners in New York City reported the highest salaries in the state, earning $117,775 on average. The American Academy of Nurse Practitioners reported in their 2011 National NP Compensation Survey that nurse practitioners in the Mideast region, including New York, earned an average base salary of $94,337 and enjoyed an average total income of $101,112.
