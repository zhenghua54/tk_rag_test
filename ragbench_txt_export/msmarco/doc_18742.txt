Confidence votes 11. Ethnic is a noun or adjective describing a social group that recognizes its members have a common national or cultural tradition. Ethnic cloths or other items are items with the distinct (usually decorative or styalistic) characteristics of those used by a particular ethnic group. The word ethnic can also denote origin by birth or family relationship rather than nationality (ethnic Turks). Word History: When it is said in a Middle English text written before 1400 that a part of a temple fell down and mad a gret distruccione of ethnykis, one wonders why ethnics were singled out for death.
