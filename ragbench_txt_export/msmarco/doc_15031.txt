The noun DOMINANCE has 4 senses: 1. superior development of one side of the body. 2. the state that exists when one person or group has power over another. 3. the organic phenomenon in which one of a pair of alleles present in a genotype is expressed in the phenotype and the other allele of the pair is not. 4. the power or right to give orders or make decisions.
