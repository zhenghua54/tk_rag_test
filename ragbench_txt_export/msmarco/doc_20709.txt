The Personal Trainer Certification Exam is offered 6 days a week, year-round at the Prometric Testing Centers throughout North, Central and South America and the Caribbean. Exam candidates are not constrained to only a few test dates at remote locations. Included in your package will be a certificate suitable for framing, which indicates that you have met the certification standards set forth by The National Council on Strength and Fitness Board for Certification and have earned the title of NCSF Certified Personal Trainer.
