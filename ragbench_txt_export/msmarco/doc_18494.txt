Depending on your income, you can claim a tax credit for up to 35 percent of your preschool expenses, up to $3,000 for one child or $6,000 for two or more children, applied directly, dollar-for-dollar, to your taxes owed for the year. Qualifying Person. Tax credits on preschool expenses must be for a qualifying person. This is generally your own child, natural born or adopted. Foster children, grandchildren, and other relatives also qualify, if you claim the person as an exemption on your income tax return.
