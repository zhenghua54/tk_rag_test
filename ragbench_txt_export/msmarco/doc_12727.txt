A: To become an RN (Registered Nurse) after high school you can choose from two paths. You can either go for an Associate's degree in Nursing or a Bachelor's of science degree in Nursing. Associate's degree is a two year long program. A: To become a nurse first you need to have the right aptitude as this is a demanding job. An associate degree can be pursued after high school but it only offers entry level jobs. An LPN degree or RN course is more extensive course and offers advanced job opportunities in the nursing profession.
