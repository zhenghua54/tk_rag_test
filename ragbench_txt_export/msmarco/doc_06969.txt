Hearing Internally. Most of the time when clairaudience occurs, the sounds are internal -- inside people's minds -- because the way messages are usually communicated from the spiritual realm is through telepathy (mind to mind). Music or Singing. You may also experience clairaudience in a musical way, such as by hearing heavenly music or the sounds of angels singing. While this doesn't happen as often as hearing spoken words or phrases, it's an extraordinarily beautiful experience to have.
