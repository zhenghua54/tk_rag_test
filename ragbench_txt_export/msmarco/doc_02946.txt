Infection of the skin and soft tissues results from traumatic inoculation of the spores into the dermis. The trauma leading to implantation can include venipuncture, spider bites, insulin injection, burns, and surgical wounds, dressings, or splints. Organism. : Mucor is a member of the Zygomycetes, a class of fungi that reproduce sexually by the formation of zygospores, and produce pauciseptate, wide hyphae. The Zygomycetes are subclassified into the orders Enthomophthorales and Mucorales, the latter of which Mucor is a member.
