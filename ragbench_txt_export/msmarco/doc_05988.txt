For example, coughing too hard or lifting something too heavy can cause broken blood vessel in eyes. And the time for heal the broken blood vessel depend on how bad your condition is. Usually, it will clear up in a week or a couple of weeks. If your eyes are hurt badly, it may also need three weeks or even more. In that case, broken blood vessel can not affect your eyesight. But if your broken blood vessel is caused by Embolism of retinal artery, venous embolism, phlebitis, diabetic retinopathy, nephritis, hematopathy and so on. It may takes a long to heal.
