Croup itself is not contagious. The cold that brings on croup is just like any other cold. Some children are prone to croup whether they get a cold or not and some will never get croup. Once she's exposed, it's probably too late. My daughter suffered from croup for years, she was very susceptible to it. If your daughter wakes up with an awful barking cough unlike nothing you've ever heard before, that's croup. Steam up your bathroom and take her in to let her breathe the air.
