PRESSURE COOKER PICKLED PORK Directions. WARNING: Please read your Pressure Cooker Manual to ascertain correct liquid levels, use & safety instructions. Combine all ingredients in the Pressure Cooker, then fill an 8-litre (32 cup) Pressure Cooker with water (approx. 10 cups) to just cover the pork and secure lid. You will need a boneless rolled shoulder of Pickled Pork or ask your butcher for a 'Hand'. The pressure cooker makes this traditional favourite so quick & easy!
