Arkansas's personal income tax system consists of six brackets and a top rate of 7%. That rate ranks 12th highest among states levying an individual income tax. Arkansas's state and local income tax collections per person were $816 in 2012 which ranked 20th lowest nationally. Arkansas's state and local governments collect $1271 per person in general sales taxes and $465 per person in excise taxes, for a combined figure of $1736, which ranks 14th high
