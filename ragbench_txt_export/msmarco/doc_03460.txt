Constantly sore and dry eyes may even water more, as the eyes respond to the dryness and irritation with excess tears. See the information on dry eyes and watery eyes for more details. Itchy, red, sore eyes Allergies and eye infections can both cause your eyes to feel sore, red and itchy. Often, itchy or irritated eyes can become sore after excessive rubbing. The eye infection conjunctivitis is a particularly common cause of sore, red eyes. Contact lens irritation can also cause sore, red eyes.
