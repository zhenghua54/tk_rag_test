The quarter is shown to allow you to compare the size of these organisms with that of a familiar object. Subphylum Cephalochordata (Amphioxus or Lancet). Amphioxus (Branchiostoma lanceolata) is the most commonly studied member of the Cephalochordata. Like the other members of this subphylum, it is a small, fish-like animal found in shallow marine waters in many parts of the world. Examine the preserved specimen of amphioxus and observe its external structure.
