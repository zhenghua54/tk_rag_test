Lemon curd is usually made with lemons, eggs and sugar, though some cooks add lemon zest, butter or whipped cream. A whisk may be helpful in making lemon curd. Oranges and tangerines may be used to create lemon curds. Tart lime juice can be added to lemon curd while cooking for additional citrus flavoring.
