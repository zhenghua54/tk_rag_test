Incidence Rate. Incidence is the number of new cases of a disease in a population. As when considering prevalence, because of different population sizes, it is usual to consider a rate. \[ Number of new cases of disease in given time period/total number in population at risk at that time period \]. The prevalence rate is a measure of the proportion of a population affected by a specific condition in a specified time period.
