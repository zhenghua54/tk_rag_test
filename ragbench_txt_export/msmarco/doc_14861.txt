Actually, the average high temperature is 103.7 or 2.2 degrees lower than in July. August still averages 23 days with temperatures of 100 or higher and 2 days of 110 or higher. It has had as many as 31 days of 100 or higher in 1973 and 1975 and in several earlier years. There were as few as 9 such days in 1955. The hot weather is not completely over as there usually are on the average 13 days with 100 or higher during the month. The average date of the last 100 degree temperature is the 27th. The lowest temperature ever recorded was 47 on the 20th and 21st in 1965.
