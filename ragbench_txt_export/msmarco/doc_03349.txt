The Silk Road, or Silk Routes, are an extensive interconnected network of trade routes across the Asian continent connecting East, South, and Western Asia with the Mediterrane … an world, including North Africa and Europe. Axum was located just south of Kush on a high plateau. Today the countries of Ethiopia and Eritrea are located here. Axum … had access to the Red Sea. By 50 CE, traders, using the Silk Road, came to Axum for its salt, gold, and gems.
