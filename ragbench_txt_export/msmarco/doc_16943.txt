The Aztec /ˈaeztɛk/ ˈæztɛk people were certain ethnic groups of Central, mexico particularly those groups who spoke The nahuatl language and who dominated large parts Of mesoamerica from the 14th to 16th. centuries From the 13th century, the Valley of Mexico was the heart of Aztec civilization: here the capital of the Aztec Triple Alliance, the city of Tenochtitlan, was built upon raised islets in Lake Texcoco.
