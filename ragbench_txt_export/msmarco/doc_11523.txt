Indoor air pollution is when pollutants contaminate air indoors and is a very dangerous problem, claiming more than 2 million lives each year. In developing countries, 1.6 million people die each year from indoor fuel burning, which is the leading source of air pollution in these areas. In this video lesson, you will learn the harmful effects of indoor air pollution. You will also gain an understanding of the sources of indoor air pollution and how they differ between developed and developing nations.
