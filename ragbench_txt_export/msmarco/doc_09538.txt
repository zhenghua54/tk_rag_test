Bureau of Labor Statistics information for states that an Occupational Therapist median salary in 2010 was $72,320 a year which is about $34.77 an hour. An occupational therapist typically requires a Master’s Degree in order to get this pay and a position in the job market. This will add around 36,400 jobs to the total occupational therapy job market from 2010 – 2020. Looking at the state by state salary information for Occupational Therapists you’ll see that the highest paying states pay as much as $100,000 year.
