When you're coming up with the concept for your business, an important component of your overall strategy plan is a mission statement. This brief statement declares the purpose of an organization and defines the reason for the company's existence. It provides the framework and context to help guide the company's strategies and actions by spelling out the business's overall goal. It is up to the mission statement to set the tone of the company and to outline concrete goals.
