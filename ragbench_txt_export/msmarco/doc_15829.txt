Understand [edit]. Eastbourne. Eastbourne is a popular and traditional sea-side resort on the south coast of England, about 110 km from London and has a population of just under 100,000, making it the second largest town in Sussex. By train [edit]. Southern Railway is the principal train company serving Eastbourne. It is linked by train to the west with Brighton, and to the east with Bexhill, Hastings and Ashford International (for Eurostar services to France and Belgium).
