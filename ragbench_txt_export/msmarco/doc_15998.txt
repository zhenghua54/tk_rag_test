Team Magma Hideout 1F. Go north from the entrance of the hideout and go through the door. Go north, then east. Step on the green tile to be warped to a small room where you can get a Nugget. Step on the green tile to exit this room. Go northwest and step on the tile there to go to another room. Use the next warp tile to return to the lab. From the lab, go south and then west. You can battle a Team Magma Grunt who has a level 37 Golbat. Go north from that Grunt and step on the warp tile to go to another room. In that room, you can battle a Grunt to the west who has a level 34 Mightyena and a level 36 Golbat. There is a Master Ball that you can get now
