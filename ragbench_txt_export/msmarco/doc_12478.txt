Alexi /ale-xi/ [3 sylls.] as a boys' name (also used as girls' name Alexi) is of Greek origin, and the meaning of Alexi is man's defender, warrior. Alexi is a variant form of Alexander (Greek) with the suffix-i. Alexi is also a derivative of Alexis (Greek). Kreatif forms: Aleri, Alxi. Alexi is a rare given name for males. Alexi is an equivalently unique surname too for both adults and children. (2000 U.S. Census). Prominent alternative forms of Alexi (outside Top 1000) appearing in the Top 1000 are Alexander (#8 a year ago) , Alex (#128) , Alexis (#302) , Alec (#469) , Allie, Al and Alek.
