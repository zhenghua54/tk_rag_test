Senetti® Flower Launch. Senetti® is the brand name given to a completely new collection of pericallis hybrids.The aim of this brand is to put across the fact that this is an entirely new group of varieties obtained by cross-breeding. Suntory® bred the first vegetatively propagated Trailing Petunia called Surfinia®, Verbena’s Tapien® and Temari®, Viola called Violina® and the very first calibrachoa hybrids to the world’s market place, called Million Bells®.
