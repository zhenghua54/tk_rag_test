The liver performs many essential functions related to digestion, metabolism, immunity, and the storage of nutrients within the body. The liver functions as an organ of the immune system through the function of the Kupffer cells that line the sinusoids. Kupffer cells are a type of fixed macrophage that form part of the mononuclear phagocyte system along with macrophages in the spleen and lymph nodes.
