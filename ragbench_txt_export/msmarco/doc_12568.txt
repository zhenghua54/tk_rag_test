Julia Morgan was born in San Francisco on January 20, 1872 and grew up in nearby Oakland. She was the second child of Charles Bill and Eliza Parmelee Morgan. Miss Morgan graduated with a degree in civil engineering from University of California at Berkeley.uring this time Morgan also designed the Hearst Greek Theater on the Berkeley campus. Julia Morgan opened her own architectural firm in 1904, quickly establishing herself as an outstanding residential architect, and securing a number of commissions in the Piedmont, Claremont and Berkeley neighborhoods.
