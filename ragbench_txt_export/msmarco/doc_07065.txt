Our children's physiotherapy service caters for children from birth until they leave school, who have a wide range of disabilities, disorders and delays including those with: 1  physical disability. 2  profound and complex learning disabilities. 3  motor coordination difficulties. Our children's physiotherapy team works closely with a range of specialists including: 1  child health specialists. 2  Child Development Service (CDS). 3  local authority educational and social services. 4  health visitors. 5  school nurses. 6  occupational therapists. 7  speech and language therapists. 8  paediatricians. 9  podiatrists.
