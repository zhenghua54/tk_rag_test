In natural gas distribution, an electrician charges an hourly rate of $33.87 per hour, those working in services to buildings and dwellings earn $32 an hour, and those working in pipeline transportation of natural gas earn $30.73 an hour. Hourly rates for electricians tend to be higher in other industries with fewer employment opportunities. Electricians in the motion picture and video industries earn an hourly average wage of $36.32, according to the BLS, and those in accounting, tax preparation, bookkeeping and payroll services earn $33.89 per hour.
