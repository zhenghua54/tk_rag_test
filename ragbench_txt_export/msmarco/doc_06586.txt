UK student visa applications are processed within 3 weeks (15 working days) of the completed application being lodged. Students can contact VFS, who manage visa application centres for the UK Border Agency in India, on (040) 66305600 (Monday-Friday between 8am to 5pm) to enquire about any delays. The UK is home to some of most prestigious institutions of higher education in the world, and it, therefore, is one of the most popular study abroad destinations in the world. More than 26,000 Indian students come to the UK to study each year.
