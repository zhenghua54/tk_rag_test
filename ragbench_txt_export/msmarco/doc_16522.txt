1 Answer. You can change the account you use with OneDrive, but you can't use the folder with multiple accounts at the same time. To change the account you use with OneDrive. Right-click the OneDrive icon (The OneDrive icon) in the notification area, at the far right of the taskbar, and then click Settings. On the Settings tab, click Unlink OneDrive. Restart OneDrive and sign in with the account you want to use. You'll need to select the location for your OneDrive folder again.
