Long-term disability (LTD) benefits pay a percentage of your salary or wages and can be increased by cost-of-living adjustments (COLAs) and/or decreased by offsets of other benefits, earnings from work, and taxes. Calculating Your Base LTD Payment. Depending on your policy, your long-term disability (LTD) plan will typically pay between 50% and 80% of your pre-disability earnings, up to a maximum.
