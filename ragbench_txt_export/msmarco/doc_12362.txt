The normal level of hemoglobin in blood varies in male and female. In male it ranges from 13.8 to 17.2 gm/dL and in female it ranges from 12.1 to 15.1 gm/dL.Low level of Hemoglobin-There are many reasons for the reduction of hemoglobin blood. Haemoglobin is tested by taking a sample of blood and the normal levels are as follows:Male: 13.8 to 17.2 gm/dlFemale: 12.1 to 15.1 gm/dlChildren: 11 to 16 gm/dlPregnant women:11 to 12 gm/dlwhere gm/dl = grams/decilitre.
