Temperatures. The average temperature in the Orlando area in March is 65 degrees Fahrenheit, with an average high of 79 and an average low of 56. Although there is obviously variance, visitors to Disney World will generally find mild temperatures in March. The average daily humidity is 69 percent, falling to 50 percent in the afternoons. Keep in mind that humidity effects the real-feel of the temperature, making warm air feel warmer and cool air feel cooler. March is typically rainy in the Disney area. Average rainfall for March is 3.5 inches, with an average of eight days of rainfall during the month.
