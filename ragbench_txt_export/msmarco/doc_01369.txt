1 Ringworm causes a scaly, crusted rash that may appear as round, red patches on the skin.Other symptoms and signs of ringworm include patches of hair loss or scaling on the scalp, itching, and blister-like lesions. 1 Ringworm is a common fungal infection of the skin and is not due to a worm. 2  The medical term for ringworm is tinea. 3  The condition is further named for the site of the body where the infection occurs.
