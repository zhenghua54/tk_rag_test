The given name Erika, or Erica, is a feminine form of Eric, deriving from the Old Norse name Eirikr (eiríkr Or erikr Eríkr In eastern scandinavia due). to monophthongization The name is thus usually taken to mean something along the lines of one ruler,  autocrat , eternal ruler or ever powerful. It is a common name in many Western societies. It is also a popular given name in Japan. Erica is also the name of a genus of approximately 860 species of flowering plants in the family Ericaceae, commonly known as heaths or heathers in English.
