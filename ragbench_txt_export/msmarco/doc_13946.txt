And there are plenty of other symbols besides words. Many graphic signs also show an arbitrary, conventional relationship to their meaning. For instance, the octagonal shape of a stop sign is a symbol, as is the white and red striped pole which signifies a barber shop. The shape of most numerical digits is arbitrary: 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, etc. (although one could argue that the shape of the digit 1 is somewhat iconic). 2.
