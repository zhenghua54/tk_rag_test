Pat was often a guest at Graceland and a good friend to Elvis and the guys. Pat today remains a hair stylist in Beverly Hills. Other people who briefly worked for Elvis were Arthur Hooten, Cliff Gleaves, Louis Harris, Bobby Smith, Jimmy Kingsley, Dean Nichopolous, Steve Smith and James Caughley. Marvin ' Gee Gee ' Gambill began working for Elvis about twelve months after he married Elvis's favorite female cousin, Patsy Presley. 'Gee Gee' a happy-go-lucky guy easily made friends with Elvis. Marvin was in charge of Elvis' wardrobe while he worked with the group.
