Exam Fees. The fee for each AP Exam is $92. The fee for exams administered at schools outside of the United States, U.S. territories and commonwealths, and Canada, with the exception of U.S. Department of Defense Dependents Schools (DoDDS), is $122 per exam. 1 If you paid for an AP Exam but then decided not to take it, you may ask your AP Coordinator for a refund. 2  Local school policy determines the amount of the refund. 3  You will probably be required to pay the $15 fee the school is charged for each unused exam.
