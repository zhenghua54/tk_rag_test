F.A.Q. DaaS is a site extension and can be accessed via your site’s SCM endpoint. To do so, adjust the SCM URL based on your own site’s name and browser to it: Note: You will need to replace YourSiteName with your actual site name. You will be prompted to login with your site admin credentials. You can think of this as your “something is wrong, help me now” button. This option will immediately start a diagnostic session with the default settings. By default, it will collect and analyze memory dumps, HTTP logs and event logs from all VM instances of your web site.
