Meningitis Vaccines. Meningococcal meningitis is a serious disease -- even with treatment. That's why prevention is a far better approach. The meningococcal vaccine can prevent meningitis infection. In the U.S., three types of meningococcal vaccines are used: 1  Meningococcal conjugate vaccine (MCV4) -- These include Menactra and Menveo. 2  Meningococcal polysaccharide vaccine (MPSV4) -- Menoimune
