School counselors also help students with study skills and individual learning plans. The BLS reports that as of May 2010, the median pay for school counselors was $53,380 per year. Counselors working in elementary and secondary schools earned a median of $60,000 per year. Professional school counselors in the bottom 10 percent earned $31,630 or less. The highest-paid earners commanded average salaries of $86,250 or more.
