To Do and Notice. Fly your paper plane. Trim it until it glides in a straight line smoothly to the floor. All paper airplane gliders have a natural speed and angle at which they will steadily glide down to the ground. Introduction. A paper airplane falls down due to gravity and glides forward through the air at the same time. We'll measure the glide ratio the ratio of the distance glided to the distance fallen.
