Naruto   Naruto is a very successful Anime/Manga series produced in Japan by Masashi Kishimoto. It is now shown worldwide and is successful on both USA and Europe. Development   Naruto started much like all of Japan's Animes, Manga. Naruto's Manga consists of 403 chapters (Up to 12th June 2008) and growing, the Anime is based on that Manga. Based on the Anime, video games and movies were produced. Making it very popular even in the US and many other countries.
