Overview. Chickweed: Kill, Control & Prevent It. Chickweed, also known as starwort, satin flower, and other names, is an annual that grows from seeds that sprout in the fall. It is found throughout North America most commonly as a lawn weed. Treat chickweed in your lawn with a lawn weed control product.
