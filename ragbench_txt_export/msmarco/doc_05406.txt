A journeyman electrical lineman's wages are determined by such factors as his geographic location, employment and union membership. Hourly wages for a journeyman outside lineman with the Northwest Line were $44.73 as of February 2012, resulting in an annual wage of $93,038 for full-time workers. The national median annual income for all electricians was $49,320 as of May 2011, according to the BLS. The middle 50 percent earned between $37,570 and $65,260 per year. The top 10 percent made at least $82,680 per year, while the bottom 10 percent earned annual wages of $30,390.
