Vincent is a form of the name Vincere. The names Bence, Bikendi, Bingen, Bixintxo, Uinseann, Vicente, Vikenti, Vince, Vincentas, Vincente, Vinko are all forms of Vincent. Vincent has the diminutives (nicknames) Vic, Vin, Vinnie. Vincent is generally used as a boy's name. It consists of 7 letters and 2 syllables and is pronounced Vin-cent. In the U.S. in 2014, it ranked 104 in baby name popularity for boys with 3744 occurrences. Less than 5 girls were given the name.
