A persuasive definition of a term is favorable to one argument or unfavorable to the other argument, but is presented as if it were neutral and well-accepted, and the listener is expected to accept such a definition without question. The term persuasive definition was introduced by philosopher C.L. Examples of persuasive definitions include: 1  atheist – someone who doesn't yet realize that God exists. 2  Democrat – a leftist who desires to overtax the corporations and abolish freedom in the economic sphere. 3  Republican – an old white man who feels threatened by change..
