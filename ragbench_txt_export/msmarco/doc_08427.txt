In comparison, total hospice spending for Medicare in 2010 was $13 billion, with an average cost of approximately $10,700 per patient. Medicare does not limit payments for hospice care for individuals, but enforces an aggregate cap for agencies of about $24,500 per beneficiary. Medicare is required to pay for all medically necessary hospital and doctor care under Parts A and B, regardless of cost or the condition of the patient. Of the 2.5 million Americans who died in 2011, 80 percent were Medicare beneficiaries at the time of death.
