TV Host Salary Information. According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), radio and television announcers, including TV hosts, earned a mean annual wage of $41,800 in May 2013 (www.bls.gov). Announcers working specifically in the radio and television broadcasting industry earned a mean salary of $39,980 a year. Announcers working in spectators sports earned an average of $92,360 a year. TV hosts typically concentrate on one type of show, such as talk, sports, finance, or business
