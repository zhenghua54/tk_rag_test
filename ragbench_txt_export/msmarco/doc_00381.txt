Salary By Region. In 2013, average salaries for small company executive vice presidents varied considerably in all U.S. regions, according to Indeed. In the South region, they earned the highest salaries of $124,000 in Washington, D.C., and lowest of $89,000 in Louisiana. They earn salaries averaging above $100,000 annually. The average annual salary for a small company executive vice president was $105,000 as of 2013, according to the job site Indeed.
