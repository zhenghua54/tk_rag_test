Definition: A bisque is a rich, creamy soup made with shellfish — specifically, bisque is traditionally made with the pureed puréed meat of, crustaceans including, lobster, crab shrimp or. crayfish When making bisque, the shells are roasted and then simmered to produce a highly flavorful stock which is then thickened. The meat itself is added to the bisque near the end of cooking. Bisque can be thickened with rice, which is the traditional method.
