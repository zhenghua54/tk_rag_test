Labor Costs. When homeowners are thinking about remodeling their outdoor pool area, one of the first things to consider is how much it is going to cost to hire a contractor or pool professional. When determining labor costs, there are several things to keep in mind. If the pool is inground or above ground and has a liner, the cost of the liner as well as any pool additions or features such as a waterfall, table or stairs will have to be added to the final cost estimate.
