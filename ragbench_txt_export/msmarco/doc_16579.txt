To parboil chicken pieces before grilling, place them in cold water and bring the water to a boil. When the water comes to a boil, turn down the heat and let them simmer for about 20 to 22 minutes. Then skim the impurities, that will form the top layer of water. Here's a useful recipe for a brine/marinade you can use for your chicken before you grill or BBQ it. --------------------------------------... Basic Chicken Brine Recipe 1 gallon water 1 cup kosher salt 2/3 cup white sugar Bring the water to a boil, then add the sugar and salt, stirring until completely dissolved.
