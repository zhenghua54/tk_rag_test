Chicken gizzards are stewed and served in soup in some cultures. A gizzard is an organ found in the digestive tract of a chicken. Similar to a stomach, the gizzard is used to grind up the foods the bird eats. Gizzards are considered a delicacy in certain cultures, and provide a healthy dose of certain vitamins and minerals. There are drawbacks to eating chicken gizzards, which decreases their nutritional value.
