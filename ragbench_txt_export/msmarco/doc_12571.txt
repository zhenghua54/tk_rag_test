Hines credits Julia Morgan with the design of Hearst's Wyntoon, but it was designed and built (and later destroyed by fire) by Bernard Maybeck in 1902-03. Julia Morgan was 25 at the time and just returning from her European schooling.ara Boutelle, an architectural historian and author who rediscovered the work of Julia Morgan, the architect who designed Hearst Castle in San Simeon, and brought it to wide public notice, has died.
