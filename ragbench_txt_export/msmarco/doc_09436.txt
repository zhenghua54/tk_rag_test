The Lake Washington waterfront in Medina is nicknamed “Medina’s Gold Coast”, and is some of the most-sought after waterfront in the Pacific Northwest. Medina today is a small, very quiet, very private community. Medina’s schools are part of the larger City of Bellevue School District, which is one of the top ranking and award winning school districts in the nation. The City takes safety and security seriously, with highly visible police officers using current technology to create and maintain a safe small town
