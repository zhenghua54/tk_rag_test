Summary. University of Oregon is a public institution that was founded in 1876. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 20,559, its setting is urban, and the campus size is 295 acres. It utilizes a quarter-based academic calendar. University of Oregon's ranking in the 2016 edition of Best Colleges is National Universities, 103. Its in-state tuition and fees are $9,918 (2014-15); out-of-state tuition and fees are $30,888 (2014-15)
