Faruq redirects here. For the village in Iran, see Faruq, Iran. Farooq (also transliterated as Farouk, Farook, Faruk, Faroeq, Faruq, or Farouq; Arabic: فاروق ‎, Fārūq) is a common Arabic given and family name derived from an honorific of Umar, an early Muslim leader. Al-Farooq literally means, the one who distinguishes between right and wrong.
