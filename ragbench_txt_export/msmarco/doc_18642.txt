Surety Bond Definition: A guarantee made by an insurance carrier (bonding company backing the bond) that is a hybrid of insurance and credit. It is a form of insurance to the obligee (party requiring the bond protection), as they are the beneficiary that can file a claim if the bond's promise is not met.
