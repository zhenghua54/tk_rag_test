The traffic on the bridge is monitored by a video camera that is now connected to the Internet. 16 March 2005 - A several month long project of repainting the Menai Bridge has begun, with the paint being completely stripped down to the bare metal for the first time in many decades and repainted. You can find the bridges with this map or an aerial image on Google Maps. If you plan to visit North Wales to see the Menai Strait bridges make sure you stop by to see the Menai Bridges Exhibition, run by the Menai Bridge Community Heritage Trust.
