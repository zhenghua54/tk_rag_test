Tamoxifen is the generic form of the brand-name drug Soltamox, which is used to treat some types of breast cancer in men and women. Tamoxifen is used for breast cancer and in women who are at a high risk of developing breast cancer. (http://www.everydayhealth.com/breast-cancer/understanding.aspx). Common side effects of tamoxifen include hot flashes, depressed mood, nausea, headache and thinning of the hair.
