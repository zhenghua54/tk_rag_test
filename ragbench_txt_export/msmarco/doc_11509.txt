Now the font is compatible with Itranslator-2003 and Sanskrit2003 font. Now font is provided in two versions-Chandas and Uttara. Chandas font represents Southern (most commonly used today) style of Devanagari script. And Uttara font represents Northern style of De vanagari Script. These styles are sometimes also called Bombay (Southern, contemporary) and Calcutta (Northern, old) pen families accordingly.
