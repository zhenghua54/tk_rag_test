In 2014, Virginia ranked 11th among the states with an average per capita personal income of $49,710 (in 2014 dollars); this was a slight increase from the previous year's average income of $48,838. The nation's highest 2014 per capita income was again in Connecticut, at $62,467. Within Virginia, the Northern region again had the highest per capita personal income in 2013 at $63,125 (in 2013 dollars), while the Central region had the second-highest ($45,812).
