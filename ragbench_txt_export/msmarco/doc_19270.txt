...But Not All Boats Need Titles. Titles show proof of ownership and are required for all boats 14 feet or greater, outboard motors 10HP or greater, and PWCs (ie. Jetski, Sea-Doo, etc.). For those boats that require a title, the title must be an Ohio title in order to get an Ohio registration. Canoes and kayaks do not need to be titled. 1  All About Titles.
