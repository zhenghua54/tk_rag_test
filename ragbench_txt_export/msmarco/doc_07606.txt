– The general fee for money orders can range from 50 cents to $10 and up, depending on where you get one. – Western Union fees: could range anywhere from 50 cents to $1.50 and more. – Most banks charge $5, $10, or 10 percent of the total amount on the money order. Domestic money orders can only be purchased for up to $1,000. Anyone that needs to send more than $1,000 will need to get two separate money orders. Tip: If you need to send more than $1,000 on a regular basis, consider opening a checking account at a bank so you can obtain cashier’s checks.
