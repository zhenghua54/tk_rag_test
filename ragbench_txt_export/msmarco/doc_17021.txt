PETG Tubing. Chemical Resistance Chart. PETG has a substantially higher success rate when machining than cast acrylic. With excellent impact strength and easy bondability, Petg tubes and profiles are the premier choice for any fabrication or display assembly. Petg stands up to the demands and rigors of fabrication. Drill it, thread it, mill it, machine it and polish it. PETG is the heavy wall mechanical tubing that is less expensive than cast acrylic and does not require any pre or post-annealing, saving time and money. Also available in a UV stabilized formulation.
