In a nut shell we can say that Basel iii is the global regulatory standard (agreed upon by the members of the Basel Committee on Banking Supervision) on bank capital adequacy, stress testing and market liquidity risk. What are the objectives / aims of the Basel III measures? Basel 3 measures aim to: → improve the banking sector's ability to absorb shocks arising from financial and economic stress, whatever the source → improve risk management and governance → strengthen banks' transparency and disclosures.
