Organelles are tiny structures that perform very specific functions within cells. The term is a reference to organs, likening the way these structures operate in cells to the way organs function in the body. A number of different organelles can be found inside various types of plant, animal, and bacteria cells. Organelles are the tiny structures that perform specific functions within a cell. Mitochondria are often called the power plant of the cell. The rough endoplasmic reticulum is an organelle. Mitochondrial disorders can cause digestive problems.
