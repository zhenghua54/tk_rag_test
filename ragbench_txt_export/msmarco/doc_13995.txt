Entry requirements. To become a social worker in England, you will need to take a three-year undergraduate degree or a two-year postgraduate degree in social work that is approved by the Health and Care Professions Council (HCPC). You will typically need the following qualifications in order to study for an undergraduate degree in social work: 1  five GCSEs (A-C) including English and maths. 2  at least two A levels, or an equivalent qualification such as a BTEC National Diploma or NVQ Level 3 in Health and Social Care.
