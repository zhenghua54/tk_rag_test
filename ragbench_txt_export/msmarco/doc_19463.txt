How to Harvest Kale. Learning how to pick kale for your needs allows it to be used fresh. You may use the baby kale harvest for leaves in a few salads. Harvesting kale for use in soups, stews and mixed greens that are cooked allows use of the larger leaves. Harvesting kale may include taking a few tender inner leaves or removing the entire bunch by cutting at the roots. Using as a garnish may take a large or small part of the kale harvest. Advertisement.
