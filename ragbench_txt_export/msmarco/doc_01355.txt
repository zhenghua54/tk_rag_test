According to the BLS, urban and regional planners earned a mean $67,350 per year, or $32.38 per hour, as of May 2011. The lowest paid made less than $41,040 annually, or $19.73 hourly. The highest earning received more than $98,060 yearly, or $47.14 per hour. Postsecondary Teachers. Postsecondary teachers made a mean $72,300 per year, with an annual range of $40,110 to $113,600, as of May 2011, according to the BLS.
