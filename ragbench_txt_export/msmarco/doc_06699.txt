Collagen is a protein that is naturally produced in the bodies of animals (especially mammals) and is the main component of connective tissues like cartilage (found in humans in places like the ears, the tip of the nose and between bones). Extracting. In order to be collected for use, collagen is taken from other dead mammals (usually livestock for commercial use). The basic extraction is generally done using a process of cooking cartilaginous animal materials, such as bones, connective tissues and skin.
