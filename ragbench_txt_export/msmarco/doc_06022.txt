The staple food of a population is what they rely on, usually a source of carbohydrate i.e. cereals, bread, pasta, rice, potatoes. Staple foood is what people of a specific region or country mostly eat as their main diet. In canada and usa the staple food is bread. In some asian countries the staple food is rice. A country might have rice as a staple food because they might provide rice in their country or their ancesters have eaten rice.
