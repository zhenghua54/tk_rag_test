Candle the eggs after 7 to 10 days. Candling the eggs is when you use a light source to view how much space the embryo occupies within an egg. After 7 to 10 days, you should see development of the embryo. Candling allows you to remove eggs with embryos that are not viable. 1 The eggs you find in grocery stores are not fertile and cannot be hatched. 2  For disease prevention and health reasons, it's best to get all your eggs from one source. 3  If you are looking for a particular or rare breed of chicken, you may need to contact a specialized hatchery.
