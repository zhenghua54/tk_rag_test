Farooq /fa-rooq/ [2 sylls.] as a boys' name. Farooq's name origin is Arabic, and Farooq means distinguisher, judge. Farooq is an alternate form of Farouk (Arabic): the last king of Egypt. Farooqui is a familiar surname. Farooq has 2 variants: Faruq and Faruqh. Farooq is a rare given name for men but a very popular last name for both adults and children (#24350 out of 150436, Top 16%).
