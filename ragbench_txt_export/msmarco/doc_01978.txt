Aphrodite (/aefrəˈdaɪti/ æfrəˈdaɪti-af-Rə-dy; Tee: GREEK) ἀφροδίτη is The greek goddess of, love, beauty, pleasure and. Procreation Her roman equivalent is the Goddess. Venus she is identified with the Planet. Venus as with many Ancient greek, deities there is more than one story about her. origins Aphrodite is also known as Cytherea (Lady of Cythera) and Cypris (Lady of Cyprus) after the two cult sites, Cythera and Cyprus, which claimed to be her place of birth. Myrtle, doves, sparrows, horses, and swans were said to be sacred to her.
