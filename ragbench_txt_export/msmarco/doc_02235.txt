Pure Names. These boy names were at the height of their popularity in the year 2012 (usage of 0.1451%) and have remained as popular to this day (usage 0.1412%, down 2.7%), but with names like Candido, Augustine and Malcolm becoming less stylish. .. from connla meaning  pure, chaste, sensible ..
