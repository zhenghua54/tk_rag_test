Who originally sang the westlife song flying without wings. “Flying Without Wings” was originally sung by Westlife, it was a huge hit and was voted Song Of The Year in 1999 in the ITV end-of-year phone music poll. The song was written in 3 hours by Steve Mac and Wayne Hector. American Idol winner Ruben Studdard also sang tthe song on the finale of the second season and turned it into a #2 hit in America. Just recently, Nicholas McDonald and Shane performed the hit Westlife song “Flying Without Wings” in Wembley Arena.
