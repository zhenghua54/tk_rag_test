Reel Name Meaning. Dutch: from a shortened form of the Germanic name Radilo, itself a short form of a compound name formed with rad ‘counsel’, ‘advice’ as the first element, for example Radolf. Compare Ralph. Source: Dictionary of American Family Names ©2013, Oxford University Press.
