Salaried Nurse-Midwives. Midwives who are salaried employees of a hospital or practice earn annual incomes which vary considerably. Based on a survey taken in 2007, the American College of Nurse-Midwives reports that the median salary of a nurse-midwife who works 35 hours or more per week is between $79,093 and $89,916. The Average Salary of Midwives. A midwife helps to care for a woman during pregnancy, assists with childbirth and offers support to new mothers during the post-birth period.
