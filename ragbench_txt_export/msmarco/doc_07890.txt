Allotropes are different forms of the same chemical element that exhibit different physical and chemical properties. Different bonding arrangement between the atoms of an element ,result in a new molecular configuration, with new physical as well as chemical properties. Dioxygen. Dioxygen (O 2) is the most common form of elemental oxygen. It is a colorless gas at room temperature and forms about 21% of the earth's atmosphere. It exists as a diradical, and is the only allotrope that has unpaired electrons.
