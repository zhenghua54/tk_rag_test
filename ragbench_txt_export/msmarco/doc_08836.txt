croup is an infection of the voice box larynx and windpipe trachea these are the upper and lower parts of the breathing tube that connects the mouth to the top of the lungs the usual cause of croup is a viral infection several viruses can cause croup this croupy cough is due to inflammation and swelling of the vocal cords in the voice box larynx breathing symptoms the infection causes inflammation on the inside lining of the breathing tube there may also be a lot of thick mucus
