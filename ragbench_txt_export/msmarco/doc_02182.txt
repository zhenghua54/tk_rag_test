protea-any tropical African shrub of the genus Protea having alternate rigid leaves and dense colorful flower heads resembling cones. genus Protea-type genus of Proteaceae; tropical African shrubs. honeypot, king protea, Protea cynaroides-South African shrub whose flowers when open are cup-shaped resembling artichokes. 1. (Plants) any shrub or small tree of the genus Protea, of tropical and southern Africa, having flowers with coloured bracts arranged in showy heads: family Proteaceae.
