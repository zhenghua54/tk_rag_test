The cost of heating oil follows the recent path of other fossil fuels, which have also plunged, benefiting consumers this year. For example, gasoline prices now average $2.41 a gallon in Bergen and Passaic counties, down from $3.30 a year ago, according to AAA. Several factors in play. The lower consumer prices reflect turmoil in the oil industry, where prices have fallen to about $60 a barrel for the first time in five years.
