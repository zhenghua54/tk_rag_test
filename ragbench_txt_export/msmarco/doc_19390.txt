Here are some of the chain stores open on New Year’s Day 2015 and their hours. When in doubt, call your store location for details. Best Buy: Stores will open at 9 a.m. on Thursday, but many will close earlier than normal on New Year’s Day as well as New Year’s Eve, at 7 p.m. Check your store for hours. [ Store locator ]. Big Lots: Stores will be open New Year’s Day, many of them during regular hours. Check your store location for changes. [ Store locator ]. B.J.’s: Stores will be open Thursday from 11 a.m. to 7 p.m. [ Store locator ]. Costco: Stores close early New Year’s Eve, at 6 p.m., and will remain closed Jan. 1.
