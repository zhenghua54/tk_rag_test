How to reset your iPhone or iPad. 1  Press and hold down the On/Off button on the right side (iPhone 6 or later) or top (all other iPhones, iPods, and iPads). 2  Press and hold down the Home button on the front of your iPhone, iPod touch, or iPad while still holding down the On/Off button. How to power off or restart your iPhone or iPad. 1  Press and hold down the On/Off button on the right side (iPhone 6 or later) or top (all other iPhones, iPods, and iPads) for 3 seconds. 2  Swipe the slide to power off confirmation slider to the right.
