One cup of whole strawberries provides about 3 percent of the recommended daily value of these three B vitamins. The same serving size also contains 5 percent of the recommended daily intake for vitamin B-6 and 9 percent of folate. Vitamin B-6 helps the body produce serotonin and steroid hormones. Vitamin E and Vitamin K. Vitamin E and vitamin K are fat-soluble vitamins, which means they're stored in the body. Over-consumption of vitamin E may lead to toxicity, so you shouldn't have more than 1,000 milligrams a day.
