Gotham had long been a well-known nickname for New York City even prior to Batman's 1939 introduction, which explains why Gotham Jewelers and many other businesses in New York City have the word Gotham in them. Gotham City's skyline, as it appears in the Batman (1989) movie. 1  Batman (1989), the look of Gotham was designed by production designer Anton Furst, who won an Oscar for his work on the film. 2  Tim Burton's sequel, Batman Returns (1992), filmed city scenes entirely on soundstages.
