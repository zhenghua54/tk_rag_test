HCG Pregnyl is a prescription medication that was approved over 25 years ago for female fertility issues and pediatric hormone therapy. HCG Pregnyl is distributed by the Schering-Plough Corporation and is referred to as Pregnyl. Pregnyl contains the luteinizing hormone (LH) and the follicle stimulating hormone (FSH) which, when produced naturally in the female human body during the course of a normal menstrual cycle, trigger ovulation.
