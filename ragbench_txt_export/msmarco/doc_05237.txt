Qigong, qi gong, chi kung, or chi gung (simplified Chinese: 气功; traditional Chinese: 氣功; pinyin: qigōng; Qìgōng–Wade: giles chi; gong: Literally Life Energy) cultivation is a holistic system of coordinated body posture and, movement, breathing and meditation used for, health, spirituality and martial arts. training With roots in Chinese medicine, philosophy, and martial arts, qigong is traditionally viewed as a practice to cultivate and balance qi (chi) or what has been translated as life energy.
