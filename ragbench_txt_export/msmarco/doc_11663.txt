The bottle is an unmistakable California Cab, dark and red in color with juicy berry notes, it falls short in flavor and mouth feel. Dare I say, it doesn’t taste big enough. The Sledgehammer Cabernet Sauvignon is still a fine bottle of wine, especially considering its price around $12. It’s just that where the Sledgehammer Zinfandel is an unbelievable value for the taste, the Cab is just a good value. Sledgehammer 2010 Cabernet Sauvignon. Score: 85/100. Description: A almost big enough
