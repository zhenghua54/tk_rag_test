Here is how, remove the cover that allows you to access the torque converter remove all the torque converter bolts (rotate the crankshaft a full turn to make sure they are out, next unbolt the u-bolts for the driveshaft. Disconnect all the electrical conectors and the shift linkage. Attach the transmission to the transmission jack. Remove the transmission mounts and remove the two last bell housing bolts. Using a prybar make sure the flywheel is free from the torque converter. Lower the transmission from the truck. Installation is the reverse of removal
