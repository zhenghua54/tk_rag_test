Lambada (pronunciation (help·info)) is a dance from Para, Pará. Brazil lambada is An-Afro brazilian dance authentic to people Of/Black african descent who brought much of their music and dance culture with them Into brazil upon arrival into the country during The Transatlantic Slave. trade Although it was recognized as a summer hit, the Lambada was not yet a true worldwide success. Many of the first lambaterias (a place to dance Lambada) which opened in 1988 couldn't survive the low tourism of the winter season, and closed a few months later.
