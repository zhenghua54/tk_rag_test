As the other gods have symbol Aphrodite also has her own symbol, she is depicted with dove and swan. Some believe that the third bird is the sparrow. It is believed that the tree of Aphrodite was myrtle. Aphrodite is connected with gold and other finer things. Aphrodite was called the goddess of love, beauty and desire. She is considered as a goddess of fertility, victory and good fortune. She is believed as the only Olympian who had no parents. Aphrodite along with family lived on the mount Olympus.
