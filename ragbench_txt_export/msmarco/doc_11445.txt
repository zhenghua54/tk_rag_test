Dual Eligible Special Needs Plans (D-SNPs) enroll beneficiaries who are entitled to both Medicare (Title XVIII) and Medical Assistance from a State Plan under Title XIX (Medicaid), and offer the opportunity of enhanced benefits by combining those available through Medicare and Medicaid. An all-dual D-SNP enrolls beneficiaries who are eligible for Medicare Advantage and who are entitled to Medicaid assistance under a State/Territorial plan.
