Costs of Concrete Foundations. A-On small projects such as single or extensions houses it is just not worth the bother of site mixing concrete. Ready mixed, delivered to the site, will cost more but will save you both time and back ache. How much of a problem would you expect this to be and what should I be paying for 60 square metres of slab foundations and drains? A-For a 60m2 of concrete slab foundations sized-house you should expect to pay £5,000 to £6,000 for the foundations and slab and about £3,500 for the drains in normal ground conditions.
