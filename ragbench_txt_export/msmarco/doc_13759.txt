Apple uses a centralized accounts mechanism in Settings, and since Outlook.com is a full Exchange ActiveSync (EAS) service, you can use the Exchange account type to configure this account. To do so, visit Settings and then Mail, Contacts, Calendars. Then, tap Add Account. In the Add Account screen, tap Microsoft Exchange. In this screen, enter your email address (whatever@outlook.com), username (whatever@outlook.com, same as the email address field), password, and a description (like “Outlook.com” or whatever).
