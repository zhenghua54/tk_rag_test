Some babies might get 4 doses, for example, if a combination vaccine containing hepatitis B is used. (This is a single shot containing several vaccines.) The extra dose is not harmful. 1  Anyone through 18 years of age who didn't get the vaccine when they were younger should also be vaccinated. Hepatitis B virus is easily spread through contact with the blood or other body fluids of an infected person.
