Include at least two items from your cultural background for each of the parts of a culture. You may draw symbols that are unique to your culture. We all belong to a culture. One definition of culture is the combination of human knowledge, beliefs and behavior that we learn and pass on to future generations. We usually express our culture in certain ways, like how we dress, what we eat, what we believe, and how we play.
