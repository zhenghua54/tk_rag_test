The Lakota, meaning friend, ally, are also known as the Teton Thithunwan meaning prairie dwellers as they are the westenmost Sioux living in North and South Dakota. The Lakota People, are known for their warrior and hunting culture. The Lakota People are part of a confederation of seven related Sioux tribes, who speak Lakota. They are also known as Teton, or Titunwan, meaning prairie dwellers. Lakota is one of the three language dialects spoken by the Sioux people. The other two are Nakota and Dakota.
