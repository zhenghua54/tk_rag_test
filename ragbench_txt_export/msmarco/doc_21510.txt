Women whose infections are related to sexual activity may be given a small dose of antibiotics to take each time they have intercourse. Remember that frequently recurring bladder symptoms can sometimes be caused by other conditions, such as interstitial cystitis, and are not always the result of an infection. While being treated for a bladder infection, drink plenty of fluids to help flush any bacteria out of the bladder.
