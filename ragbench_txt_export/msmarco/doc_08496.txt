Investopedia is a resource for investing education, personal finance, market analysis, and free trading simulators. With a comprehensive financial dictionary, exam preparation materials, and active trading strategies, it is a leading destination for investors. On September 9, 2011 Investopedia released a free iPhone App. The App gives users access to Investopedia's full financial dictionary, articles, and other content. Investopedia's App has over 211,000 downloads and is the #25 free Finance App in iTunes.
