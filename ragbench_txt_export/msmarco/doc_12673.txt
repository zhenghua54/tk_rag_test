How Long to Cook a Whole Chicken. 1  2-1/2 to 3 pounds: Roast at 375 degrees F for 1 to 1-1/4 hours. 2  3 to 3-1/2 pounds: Roast at 375 degrees F for 1-1/4 to 1-1/2 hours. 3  3-1/2 to 4 pounds: Roast at 375 degrees F for 1-1/4 to 1-3/4 hours. 4  4-1/2 to 5 pounds: Roast at 375 degrees F for 1-1/2 to 2 hours.
