No matter what the original cause is, renal parenchymal diseases are characterized by scarring of the kidneys. That is the gradual process of renal fibrosis and the normal renal cells and tissues are replaced by scars. Renal parenchyma can be divided into renal medulla and renal cortex. Bilateral renal parenchymal disease means that the renal impairments are in the medullar and cortical areas affecting both the two kidneys.
