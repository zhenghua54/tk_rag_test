Architecture can mean: 1  A general term to describe buildings and other physical structures. 2  The art and science of designing buildings and (some) nonbuilding structures. 3  The style of design and method of construction of buildings and other physical structures. Section of Brunelleschi 's dome drawn by the architect Cigoli (c. 1600). Architecture (Latin architectura, from the Greek ἀρχιτέκτων arkhitekton architect, from ἀρχι- chief and τέκτων builder) is both the process and the product of planning, designing, and constructing buildings and other physical structures.
