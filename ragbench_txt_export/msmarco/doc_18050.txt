White Rabbit Creamy Candies (Template:Zh-cp) are perhaps the best known brand of Chinese-made candy in China, and the only one to be marketed significantly outside of that country. In the Philippines in particular it has dominated the creamy candy or toffee market. White Rabbit sweets were advertised with the line, Seven White Rabbit candies is equivalent to one cup of milk , and was seen as a nutritional product. The candies hence accompanied the growth of a generation.
