Amoxicillin. Amoxicillin is an antibiotic from the penicillin group of drugs, used for many types of infections. Ear infections, gonorrhea and pneumonia can all be cured with amoxicillin. It can be prescribed as a suspension liquid or a tablet, both to be taken orally. Nitrofurantoin is an antibiotic used to treat bladder infections and is sold under the names Macrobid and Macrodantin. It prevents bacteria from forming cell walls and limits their ability to survive and reproduce.
