Appearance Edit. Smoker is a muscular white-haired (light blue-green in the pre-timeskip anime) man, with a distinct trait of always being seen smoking two cigars at once. He wears a large thick white and blue specialist marine jacket which he keeps open, with greenish fur lining the neck, wrists and hem. Smoker the White Hunter is a Marine officer stationed at the G-5 base. He was first introduced as a captain (大佐, Taisa) in Loguetown, where he made it his mission to capture Luffy, and was later promoted to the rank of commodore (准将, Junshō) , due to the events in Alabasta.
