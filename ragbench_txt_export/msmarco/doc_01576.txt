In Vitro Fertilization is a one assisted reproductive technology (ART) commonly referred to as IVF. IVF is the process of fertilization by manually combining an egg and sperm in a laboratory dish, and then transferring the embryo to the uterus. There are five basic steps in the IVF and embryo transfer process: 1  Monitor and stimulate the development of healthy egg(s) in the ovaries. 2  Collect the eggs. 3  Secure the sperm. 4  Combine the eggs and sperm together in the laboratory and provide the appropriate environment for fertilization and early embryo growth.
