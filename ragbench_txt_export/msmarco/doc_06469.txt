The common inch-pound coating weight designations (ordered as A1046) are, in oz/ft 2: ZM30 ZM40 ZM60 ZM75 ZM90 These designations specify the minimum average of a TST, total-both-sides, tested per A924/A924M, e.g., ZM60 requires a minimum average TST of 0.60 oz/ft 2 total-both-sides. This alloy-coated sheet has a coating that consists of zinc with 5-13% aluminum, and 2-4% magnesium. The designation systems for coating weight and coating mass are given in ASTM Standard A1046/A1046M.
