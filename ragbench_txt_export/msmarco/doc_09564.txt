Assuming we're talking about a serviceable iPad (not obsolete) it is relatively dependent on whether you have AppleCare Plus coverage. AppleCare Plus offers up to two accidental damage incidents for the iPad at $49 apiece (US pricing) This includes cracked screen repair. Outside of AppleCare coverage, because this is classified as accidental damage, the pricing goes up. Your iPad screen can be replaced for a price dependent on its model. In the US, iPad 2 screen repair is priced at 249 USD-https://www.apple.com/sup port/ip... This excludes tax. Prices will vary by country, so look to your regional Apple Store website if you're in a different country.
