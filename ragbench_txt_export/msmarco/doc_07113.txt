Simply divide the fees by your square footage to see how much you'll be paying per square foot. For example, $250 per month / 700 square feet = $0.36 per square foot. The size of the building that your unit belongs to can play a very important factor in the cost of your monthly condo fees. In Toronto, the average is estimated to be close to $0.50 per square foot but can reach as high as $1.00 or more. Fees in Vancouver, Calgary and Montreal tend to be slightly lower than that.
