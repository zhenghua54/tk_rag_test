Engineering Dominates List of Top-Paid Majors for Class of 2013 Grads Seven of the 10 top-paid majors for Class of 2013 bachelor’s degree graduates are engineering majors, according to NACE’s January 2014 Salary Survey—the final report on starting salaries for the college Class of 2013.
