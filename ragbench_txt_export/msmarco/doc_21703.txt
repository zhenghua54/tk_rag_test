Dustin Johnson is a professional golfer. Dustin played collegiate golf at Coastal Carolina University. As an amateur, Dustin won the Monroe Invitational and the Northeast Amateur in 2007 and played on the winning 2007 Walker Cup and Palmer Cup teams. Dustin Johnson is the first player since Tiger Woods to win at least once in each of his first seven seasons coming out of college.
