The Espagnole Sauce, also sometimes called Brown Sauce, is a slightly more complex mother sauce. Espagnole is made by thickening brown stock with roux. So in that sense it's similar to a veloute. Velouté the difference is that espagnole is made with tomato puree purée and mirepoix for deeper color. and flavor
