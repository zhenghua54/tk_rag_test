Carpal Tunnel Syndrome-Treatment Overview. The goal of treatment for carpal tunnel syndrome is to allow you to return to your normal function and activities and to prevent nerve damage and loss of muscle strength in your fingers and hand. Treatment options include: 1  Home treatment, such as changing or avoiding activities that may be causing symptoms and wearing a wrist splint. 2  Physical therapy. 3  Medicines, such as nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) to relieve pain and reduce inflammation. 4  Surgery.
