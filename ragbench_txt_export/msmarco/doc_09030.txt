According to the bureau's May 2011 Occupational Employment Statistics report on wages for the occupation, casino surveillance operators earned an average annual wage of $33,210 nationwide, which equals an average hourly wage of $15.97. The median annual wage for the group was $30,410 nationwide. According to the bureau, the type of employer is a major salary factor. Local governments employed 2,900 casino surveillance operators at an annual average wage of $33,350.
