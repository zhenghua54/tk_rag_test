Polybutylene is simply a type of plumbing material that was manufactured between the late 70's and early 90's. It is thought that it was installed in over 6 million homes during that time period. Polybutylene was originally thought to be a versatile and inexpensive product to use. If the PB bursts it can cause major, immediate water damage.
