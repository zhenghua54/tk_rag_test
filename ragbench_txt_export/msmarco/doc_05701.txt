Many of the usability companies pay testers $10 for every test that they successfully complete. Tests usually take 15-20 minutes to complete. That is not bad for 20 minutes of work. The downside is that you may not receive many opportunities to test websites. It is quite easy to become a website tester. All that you need to do is join some of the usability companies that recruit paid website testers. Here are few good ones to get you started. 1  UserTesting. 2  UserFeel. 3  Userlytics. 4  Analysia.
