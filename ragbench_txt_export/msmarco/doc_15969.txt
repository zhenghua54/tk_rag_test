The CW is shooting The Messengers. The freshman drama, which followed a group of individuals on a quest to find the Devil and prevent the Apocalypse, will not return for a second season, TVLine has learned. The Messengers premiered April 17 to 1.2 million viewers and a 0.3 rating, down a tenth from Hart of Dixie ‘s final Friday premiere and finale. TVLine readers gave the supernatural drama a C+ average. BuzzFeed was the first to report The Messengers ‘ cancellation.
