The main characters of the series are the Messengers (real term: Angels of the Apocalypse), a group of ordinary humans chosen by God to determine if humanity is worth saving. The series was officially picked up on May 8, 2014, and premiered on April 17, 2015. The series was canceled by the CW on May 7, 2015, but aired all of its episodes, and concluded on July 24, 2015.
