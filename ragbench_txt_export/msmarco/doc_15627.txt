Vitamin D is good for bone health and it aids the body in absorbing calcium. Of course, everyone knows that it is healthy calcium levels that lead to strong bones, which is why vitamin D is good for bone health. Vitamin D has been attributed to providing further benefits. Some of the symptoms of too much vitamin D include: 1  Loss of appetite. 2  Vomiting. 3  Nausea. 4  Constipation. 5  Weight loss. 6  Weakness. 7  Increase of blood calcium levels.
