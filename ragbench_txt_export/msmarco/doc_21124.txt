PREPARATION. • To clean rock shrimp use one of these methods: • For broiling in the shell: Place the rock shrimp on a cutting board, dorsal side down and the swimmerets up. With a sharp knife, cut from the base of the tail to the other end, but not through the shell. • Rock shrimp cook more quickly than other shrimp. • To boil: Drop in a pot of boiling salted water, stir, and after 35 seconds pour into a colander and rinse with cold water. • To broil: Place 4 inches from the source of heat for 2 minutes or until the meat turns opaque.
