An execution fee of $25 is mandatory when submitting Form DS-11. This is paid to the agent that adjudicates the application. The fees for some of the optional services are $60 for expedited service, $14.85 for overnight delivery of your new passport and $150 for a file search. Current passport fee for expediting at Fastport Passport ranges from $99 for processing in 10 to 12 business days to $299 for same day passports. By applying early, you can avoid these extra expenses.
