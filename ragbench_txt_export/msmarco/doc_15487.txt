While on Whidbey Island, you'll discover interesting history, delicious local foods, unique shops and galleries, picturesque gardens and farms, and water, island, and mountain views in every direction. You'll also have the chance to enjoy a full range of outdoor recreation, including hiking, kayaking, and boating. Whidbey Island has a wiggle-y and twisty shoreline, resulting in interesting coves and harbors. There are also several freshwater lakes on Whidbey Island. 1  Boating. 2  Marinas are located at Deception Pass State Park, Oak Harbor, and Langley.
