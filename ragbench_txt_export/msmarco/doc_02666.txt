ACT No. XXV., 1909. An Act to provide for the protection and care of aborigines; to repeal the Supply of Liquors Aborigines Prevention Act; to amend the Vagrancy Act. 1902, and the Police Offences (Amendment) Act, 1908; and for purposes con sequent thereon or incidental thereto. [Assented to, 20th December, 1909.]. 1. This Act may be cited as the 'Aborigines Protection Act, short title. 1909, and shall come into force on a date to be fixed by proclama tion of the Governor in the Gazette. 2.
