Kombucha Bubbles: How to Increase Carbonation…for Beginners. One of the most common questions homebrewers have is how to get more carbonation (i.e. fun bubbles) in their Kombucha. Yes, bubbles are fun, and there is something inherently exciting about seeing a fizzy glass of iced Kombucha froth over the edge as you pour. Bubbles add flavor and texture, scientists say. It emphasizes the “living” energy of the drink, and because it’s natural, it feels and tastes different than CO2 that is added.
