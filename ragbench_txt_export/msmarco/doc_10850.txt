The thicker the growth ring,the coarser the texture.As a carpenter, you’ll often hear the terms wood grain and wood texture. Wood grain most often refers to the thickness of the annular rings. The term fine grain refers to thinnerannular rings, while coarse grain refers to thicker rings. Theterm wood grain is also used to indicate the direction of thewood fibers, as in straight grain, spiral grain, and curly grain.
