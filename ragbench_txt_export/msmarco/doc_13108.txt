Studies show vitamins A, E, zinc and the phytochemicals lutein and zeaxanthin can help maintain the health of your eyes and possibly prevent cataracts. Good sources of vitamin A include sweet potatoes, carrots, fortified milk and dark, leafy greens. For tasty sources of vitamin E, try spinach, vegetable oils and nuts, including almonds, hazelnuts, pecans and sunflower seeds. Lutein and zeaxanthin are found in egg yolks and dark green vegetables.
