Location-Sea Rim State Park is situated within the Gulf Coast region of Texas. The park is located 20 miles south of Port Arthur on State Highway 87. State Highway 87 is closed between Sea Rim and High Island, which necessitates a detour onto Highway 73. An American Alligator. This park has been affected by Hurricane Rita and is temporarily closed for repairs. Sea Rim State Park, 4141.1 acres of marshland with 5.2 miles of Gulf beach shoreline, is located in Jefferson County south of Port Arthur. The park was acquired by purchase from Planet Oil and Mineral Corporation and Horizon Sales Corporation in 1972 and was opened to the public in 1977
