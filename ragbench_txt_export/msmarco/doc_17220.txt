Several LPN programs are available in every state with tuition ranging from $1,000 to $15,000 depending on the program. Although some program options are noteworthy, not all meet the criteria for approval by your state's Board of Nursing, the governmental agency that regulates nursing and determines license eligibility. Programs vary in the types of fees, but expect to pay several of the following: 1  Application Fee: $20-$50. 2  Admission Exam Fee: $20-$60. 3  Course registration: $15-$30. 4  Lab: $20-$50.
