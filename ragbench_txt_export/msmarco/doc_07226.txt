BMR= Lean body mass (lbs) x 13.8 calories. You can obtain your lean body mass from body fat measurements. Calculate lean muscle mass vs. fat mass: Body fat % x scale weight= fat mass. Scale weight-fat mass= lean body mass. Once you calculate your BMR factor in activity to account for calories burned during exercise. BMR x 1.2 for low intensity activities and leisure activities (primarily sedentary). BMR x 1.375 for light exercise (leisurely walking for 30-50 minutes 3-4 days/week, golfing, house chores).
