A widely accepted definition of obesity is body weight that is 20% or more in excess of ideal weight:height ratio according to actuarial tables. By this definition, 34% of adults in the U.S. are obese. Hypertrophic obesity results from an increase in the size of the fat cells in the increased adipose tissue mass. observations Manifestations are excess body weight for height. Overweight is determined by a body mass index (BMI) of 25 to 29.9 kg/m 2, and obesity is a BMI = 30 kg/m 2.
