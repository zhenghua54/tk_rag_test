Zendesk is an SaaS Suite developed to be a platform for customer service support, help desk ticketing and issue tracking. It is the creation of a company of the same name founded in 2007. The company Zendesk inc. was founded by Morten Primdahl, Alexander Aghassipour and Mikkel Svane. By 2008 it had received $500,000 in funding. In 2009 a second series of funding came in the amount of $6 million allowing the company to move to San Francisco and establishing its headquarters.
