Cover cut watermelon and store in the refrigerator. Once the watermelon has been cut, cover the cut side with two pieces of plastic wrap tightly secured in a criss-cross pattern. Place in the refrigerate with cut side up, to prevent juices from escaping or place covered cut side down on a plate. Store uncut watermelons at room temperature. Store uncut watermelon at 55°F to 70°F (13°C to 21°C). Whole melons will keep for two weeks at 60°F (16°C), 7 to 10 days at average room temperature and up to a week in hot weather.
