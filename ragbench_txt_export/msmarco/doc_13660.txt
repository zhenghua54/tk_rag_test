Weihai Export & Processing Zone (EPZ) was set up by the approval of the State Council on April 27, 2000. Weihai EPZ is located in Weihai Economic & Technological Development Zone with programmed area of 2.6 km 2 (1.0 sq mi). Weihai EPZ belongs to comprehensive export & processing zone. Weihai Torch Hi-Tech Science Park is a state-level development zone approved by the State Council on March 1991. Located in Weihai's northwest zone of culture, education and science, the Park has the total area of 111.9 km 2 (43.2 sq mi), a coastline of 30.5 km (19.0 mi) and 150,000 residents.
