Finish cooking the brisket: Turn up the temperature of the smoker grill to 180 degrees F. Pour 1 1/2 to 2 cups of apple cider or apple juice into the roasting pan until entire bottom of roasting pan is covered with the liquid. Insert the meat thermometer probe into the center of the brisket. Brisket is the meat of choice for barbecuing in the great state of Texas. In fact, it is considered the National Dish of Texas.. Brisket are a very tough piece of meat and difficult to eat unless it is cooked at a very low and slow temperature.
