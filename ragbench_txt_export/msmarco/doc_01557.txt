I n T h e S p o t l i g h t. . . . Magnolia has been awarded Recognized Scenic City Certification by Scenic Texas. A newly certified city, Magnolia is one of 20 Texas cities earning a five-year certification from 2015-2020. The Scenic City Certification Program is a project of Scenic Texas and its statewide program partners. It is the first program of its kind in the U.S. to incorporate a comprehensive set of model standards for design and development of public roadways and public spaces into one program. Texas cities apply to the Program for evaluation of existing standards against the Scenic City model.
