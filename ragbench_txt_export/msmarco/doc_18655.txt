Men have more years in consulting than women, with the female average being 13 years (two less than the average). About 1 in 5 consultants (21%) charge $200 or more per hour. Median hourly wage correlates positively with number of years of experience in consulting. The rate is $117 for consultants with less than 5 years experience, and increases 28.2% to $150, for those with 25 years of experience or more.
