The normal mean temperature for the month is 88.2 and has ranged from 93.8 in 1990 down to 79.0 in 1965. In early June, the normal daily high temperature reaches 100 or higher and stays there until the middle of September. Average nighttime temperatures rise from 68 on the 1st to 78 on the 30th. The hot weather is not completely over as there usually are on the average 13 days with 100 or higher during the month. The average date of the last 100 degree temperature is the 27th. The lowest temperature ever recorded was 47 on the 20th and 21st in 1965.
