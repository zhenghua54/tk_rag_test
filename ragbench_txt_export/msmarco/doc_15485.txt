You'll want to have a general idea of the things you want to do and the sights you want to see, but relax and plan for spontaneity. Whidbey Island is long but narrow, so you really can't get lost. Whidbey Island has a wiggle-y and twisty shoreline, resulting in interesting coves and harbors. There are also several freshwater lakes on Whidbey Island. 1  Boating. 2  Marinas are located at Deception Pass State Park, Oak Harbor, and Langley.
