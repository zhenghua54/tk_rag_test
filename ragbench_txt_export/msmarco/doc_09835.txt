Amery is an unusual given name for men but a somewhat prominent surname for both adults and children (#94227 out of 150436, Top 63%). (2000 U.S. Census). Amery /ame-ry/ [3 sylls.] as a boys' name is of Old German derivation, and Amery means brave power. Amery is an alternate spelling of Amory (Old German): transcription of Emery. Compare surname Amero
