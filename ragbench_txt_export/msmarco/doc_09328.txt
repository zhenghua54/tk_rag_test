Yes. Sometimes ferret owners use a high quality kitten food in part of their ferret food mix. If the ferret eats some of the cat food it's ok. Just make sure you're not only feeding a low quality cat food as a ferrets main diet. It won't have enough protein for the ferret. Report Abuse. It's not formulated for ferrets who need a good 50% protein level. Some ferret owners use Wellness Core, EVO dry and canned, and a few other foods which do have a 50% protein level, but they still mix it with ferret food. Cat food isn't nutritionally complete for a ferret.
