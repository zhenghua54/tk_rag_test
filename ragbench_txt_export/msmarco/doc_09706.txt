MacDonald, Macdonald, and McDonald are Anglicised forms of the Scottish Gaelic and Irish Gaelic name MacDhòmhnaill, macdhòmhnaill which in Modern gaelic is approximated McConnell. mcconnell Frequency data from England of 1891 shows a concentration of families bearing the Macdonald surname in Lancashire and Yorkshire with a lower frequency in the northernmost counties, but overall widespread distribution throughout the country.
