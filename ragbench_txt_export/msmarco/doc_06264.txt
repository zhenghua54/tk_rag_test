Bipolar Types. There are several types of bipolar disorder; all involve episodes of depression and mania to a degree. They include bipolar I, bipolar II, cyclothymic disorder, mixed bipolar, and rapid-cycling bipolar disorder. Bipolar I. A person affected by bipolar I disorder has had at least one manic episode in his or her life. There are many different symptoms -- and several different types -- of bipolar disorder.
