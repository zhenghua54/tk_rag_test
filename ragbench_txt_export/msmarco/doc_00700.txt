Computer engineers received average starting pay of $70,300 annually, while annual wages for chemical engineers averaged $66,900. Electrical or electronic engineers earned average starting salaries of $62,500 per year, and engineering technology majors got average annual pay of $60,900. While civil engineers averaged $58,000 annually, industrial engineers started out at $54,500 per year on average. The second-highest starting salaries went to graduates in computer science, who received an average of $59,084 per year, while humanities and social science majors were in last place, averaging $38,045.
