The Earth is closest to the Sun or at its Perihelion two weeks after the December solstice and farthest from the Sun or Aphelion, two weeks after the June Solstice. In 2015, the Earth will reach its Perihelion at 22:49 UTC on January 2, 2016. Conversely, the Earth is farthest away from the Sun, at the Aphelion point, two weeks after the June Solstice, when the Northern Hemisphere is enjoying warm summer months. Perihelion in Seattle, Washington, U.S.A. is on. Saturday, January 2, 2016 at 2:49 PM PST (Change city).
