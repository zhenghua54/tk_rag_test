Posted on. (Answer #1). Jacob Marley was Scrooge's business partner and probably his only friend in his later years. Marley's spirit is the first to visit Scrooge to warn him that he will be visited by three more spirits that night. Scrooge notices that Marley is bound by a chain and asks the spirit about it. Posted on. (Answer #3). Jacob Marley is Scrooge's old business partner who died. In the story, Marley returns from the dead to warn Scrooge of the visits of the three ghosts that he will encounter.
