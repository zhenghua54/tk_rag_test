It should be noted that Siberian ginseng (Eleutherococcus senticosis) is not a true ginseng and doesn't belong to the genus Panax . It does belong to the Araliaceae family of plants, but consumers should be aware that it is not the same as American or Asian ginseng. Knowledge center. Ginseng is any of eleven different varieties of short, slow growing perennial plants with fleshy roots. Ginseng is believed to restore and enhance normal well-being and has become one of the most popular herbal remedies in the world today.
