Zen (Chinese: 禪; pinyin: Chan, Chán Middle: chinese) dʑjen is a school Of Mahayana buddhism that originated In china during The tang dynasty As. chan chán Contrary to the popular image, literature does play a role in the Zen-training. Zen is deeply rooted in the teachings and doctrines of Mahāyāna Buddhism. Unsui, Zen-monks, are expected to become familiar with the classics of the Zen canon.
