MikroTik RouterOS is the operating system of MikroTik RouterBOARD hardware. It can also be installed on a PC and will turn it into a router with all the necessary features-firewall, routing, wireless access point, bandwidth management, hotspot gateway, backhaul link, VPN server and more.
