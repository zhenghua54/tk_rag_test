Science Direct claims to be the world's largest full-text database, offering more than 1500 scientific, technical & medical peer-reviewed journals, over 59 million abstracts, two million full-text scientific journal articles, and an ever-expanding suite of bibliographic databases. In addition, links to another one million full-text articles by providing references and links to other publishers' platforms. The database is available only through subscription, and there are various tiers of services, depending on the price and type of subscription.
