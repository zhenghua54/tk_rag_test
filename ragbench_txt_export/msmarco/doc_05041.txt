How to become a Cardiovascular Technician. Cardiovascular technicians and technologists can be trained in a number of different ways. Some cardiovascular technicians and technologists receive on the job training, but most receive training in a vocational school or a community college. Two to four year programs are common, with two year programs at a community or junior college being the way that most cardiovascular technicians and technologists are trained. Four year programs are becoming much more popular, however.
