Does the bacterial meningitis vaccine prevent bacterial or viral meningitis. We are having a serious problem with meningtis in our area. My students are very concerned about their risk factors and if the vaccine will prevent either viral or bacterial meningitis. Rating Newest Oldest. Best Answer: The currently available meningococcal meningitis vaccine protects against four types of the bacterium Neisseria meningitidis, a very important cause of bacterial meningitis and sepsis in adolescents and young adults in the United States.
