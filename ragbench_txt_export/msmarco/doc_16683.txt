Tomato classification is done on the basis of its family, order, genus and species. Scientific classification of Tomato is needed for its detailed study. Scientific classification of Tomato tells about its basic characteristics and also gives information about its relation with other fruit. Tomato Family. Family is a subdivision of order. Tomato family is Solanaceae. Scientific classification of Tomato is done on the basis of its characteristics. Shape of leaves, fruit form, type of flower, etc. determine the scientific classification of Tomato. Species having similar characteristics are placed in a particular genus. Tomatoes are placed under Solanum genus. The basic category for scientific classification of Tomato is species. All fruits belonging to one specie are same ecologically
