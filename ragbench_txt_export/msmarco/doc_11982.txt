There are approximately 33,000 apartments with Section 8 project-based multifamily assistance in Massachusetts. A number of apartments are subsidized under the Section 8 Moderate Rehabilitation (or mod rehab) program. In exchange for providing a moderate level of rehabilitation to a property, the owner is provided a rent subsidy for a number of years by the local housing authority or a regional nonprofit housing agency.
