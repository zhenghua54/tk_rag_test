These are foods that contain vitamin A or its precursor called beta-carotene. Animal foods contain vitamin A whereas no plant food provides vitamin A but instead they have beta-carotene which is converted to vitamin A in the liver. The foods listed in this article are good sources of vitamin A or beta-carotene. Vitamin A is a vitamin and antioxidant that’s associated with healthy eyes.
