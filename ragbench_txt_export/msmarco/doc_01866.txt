Definition of ELOQUENCE for Kids. 1. : speaking or writing that is forceful and convincing. 2. : the ability to speak or write with force and in a convincing way. Full Definition of ELOQUENCE. 1. : discourse marked by force and persuasiveness; also: the art or power of using such discourse. 2. : the quality of forceful or persuasive expressiveness.
