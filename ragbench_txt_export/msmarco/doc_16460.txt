The Babylonians were the first to form an empire that would encompass all of Mesopotamia. Rise of the Babylonians and King Hammurabi. The city of Babylon had been a city-state in Mesopotamia for many years. After the fall of the Akkadian Empire, the city was taken over and settled by the Amorites. The city began its rise to power in 1792 BC when King Hammurabi took the throne.
