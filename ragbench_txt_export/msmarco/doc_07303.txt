Awareness Ribbon's are a great way to show support and bring public attention to a specific Cause. Each Color Ribbon has a meaning which is connected to it. Most colors have several meanings although some are more commonly known than others. Teal Ribbon-This color is a symbol for ovarian, cervical, and uterine cancers as well as substance abuse, sexual assault, PTSD, Myasthenia Gravis and polycystic ovarian syndrome, syringomylia, Tourette Syndrome, usher syndrome, spina bifida (can be trimmed in blue).
