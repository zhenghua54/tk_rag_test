Program Sponsorship. Advertising sponsorships includes several common types. Program sponsorship is when an advertiser sponsors a program as opposed to buying a few ad placements during the program. With a program sponsorship, the advertiser takes on a stronger partnership with the program and medium. Strong collaboration between advertiser and medium is a sign of a sponsorship.
