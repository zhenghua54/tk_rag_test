In Lanzarote winter begins in December and lasts until March. The winter season remains warm with cool nights. The average high temperatures range from 21°C to 23°C. Night-time remains cool with 16°C in December, 14°C in January and 13°C in February, and again getting back up to 15°C in March. Lanzarote enjoys a pleasant climate all year round which helps make it one of the most visited tourist destinations in the whole of Europe, being only 127km away from the Sahara Desert.
