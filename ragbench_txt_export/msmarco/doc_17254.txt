Pay for Chemical Engineers in the United States sits in the ballpark of $73K per year. Final cash compensation to Chemical Engineers varies from around $51K to approximately $123K; choice pay grades include potential for bonuses and profit sharing as high as $15K and $12K, respectively. Chemical Engineers with a rich background of experience are typically rewarded with larger paychecks. Salaries of relatively inexperienced workers fall in the neighborhood of $68K, but folks who have racked up five to 10 years see a notably higher median of $85K.
