Immune system disorders cause abnormally low activity or over activity of the immune system. In cases of immune system over activity, the body attacks and damages its own tissues (autoimmune diseases). Immune deficiency diseases decrease the body's ability to fight invaders, causing vulnerability to infections. 1 The immune system attacks and damages blood vessels in this group of autoimmune diseases. 2  Vasculitis can affect any organ, so symptoms vary widely and can occur almost anywhere in the body. 3  Treatment includes reducing immune system activity, usually with prednisone or another corticosteroid.
