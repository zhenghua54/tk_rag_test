Tizer. From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia. http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tizer. Tizer is a red-coloured soft drink sold in the United Kingdom. The name originally comes from the phrase 'Tizer the Appetizer'. It was launched in 1924 by Fred Pickup of Manchester when it was known as 'Pickup's Appetizer'. Please try again later.
