Preparation of cis (+) sertraline hydrochloride form II: The purified cis mandelate salt (45Og) is suspended in ethyl acetate (2.25L). The suspension is treated with 10% aqueous NaOH solution (IL) to obtain a clear solution. SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION. The present invention discloses a novel method for the preparation of racemic sertraline starting from the known tetralone and cis(+) sertraline hydrochloride form II from cis(+) sertraline mandelate.
