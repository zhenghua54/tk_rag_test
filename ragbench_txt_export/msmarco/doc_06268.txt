For the purposes of diagnosis, the types of bipolar disorder are defined by the patterns and severity of symptoms. 1. 1  Bipolar I disorder (also noted as bipolar 1) is characterized by one or more manic or mixed episodes and one or more major depressive episodes. The types of bipolar disorder are differentiated by the presence and severity of specific symptoms, but all include cycling between high mood states (like mania or manic symptoms) and low mood states (depression).
