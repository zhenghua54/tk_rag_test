Zendesk, Inc. is a software development company based in San Francisco, California. The company provides a cloud-based customer service platform, also called Zendesk, that includes ticketing, self-service options, and customer support features. Founded in 2007, the company now employs 1,000 people and serves 60,000 paid customers in 150 countries and territories. In 2012, Zendesk raised $60 million in a combination of $45 million in equity and a $15 million credit facility, bringing the total investment to $86 million. Zendesk became a public company in May 2014 traded on the New York Stock Exchange.
