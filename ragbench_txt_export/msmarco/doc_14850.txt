Vitamins A, C and E provide Aloe Vera’s antioxidant activity and neutralise free radicals. The “B” Vitamins and Choline are involved in amino acid metabolism, Vitamin B12 is required for the production of red blood cells, Folic Acid is required in the development of blood cells. Several different minerals have been found in Aloe Vera, following are the 10 most important ones: 1  Calcium. 2  Chromium. 3  Copper. 4  Iron. 5  Magnesium. 6  Manganese. 7  Potassium. 8  Phosphorous.
