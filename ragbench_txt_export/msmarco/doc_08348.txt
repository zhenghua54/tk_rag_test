Salary. In 2012, the median salary for chiropractors was $66,160, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics. This means that half of chiropractors made more than this amount and half of those working in the profession made less than the median amount. 10 percent of chiropractors had a salary of less than $31,030; however, the top 10 percent made $142,950 or more. Experience and length of time in the profession are key factors in how much a chiropractor will earn
