Apprentices in the Northwest Line's Outside Electrical Lineman Apprenticeship receive an increase from 60 percent to 63 percent of a journey worker's wages after completing 1,000 hours of training. Those enrolled in the Northeastern Joint Apprenticeship program get a five percent pay raise after 1,000 hours. Wages for lineman apprentices at the TVA increase from $36,450 to $38,370 after completing their first step of training. A first-year apprentice lineman's wages varies based on the program where he is enrolled. Starting wages for apprentices in the Tennessee Valley Authority's Lineman Apprentice Program are $36,450 per year.
