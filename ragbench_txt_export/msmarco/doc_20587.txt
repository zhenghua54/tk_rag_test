Azithromycin may also be used to treat a number of other more uncommon bacterial infections. Azithromycin is not effective against any infection caused by a virus, such as the flu, stomach flu, or common cold. Bacterial Infections. Azithromycin is most commonly used to treat the following infections: 1  Respiratory infections, such as bronchitis. 2  Ear infections (otitis media). 3  Sinus infections (sinusitis).
