When the Missouri Territory was organized in 1812, St. Louis was Missouri's seat of government. St. Charles would serve as the next capital. In the middle of the state, Jefferson City was chosen as the new capital in 1821 when Thomas Jefferson was still living.efferson City is home to Lincoln University, a historically black college founded in 1866 and later designated as a land-grant university. In 2013, Jefferson City was named America's Most Beautiful Small Town by Rand McNally.
