A cost of $2.40 per feeder pig sold includes sow and pig health costs. Labor costs are based on pay rate of $22 per hour, benefits included. The labor cost per finished hog sold is $11.40, or if feeder pigs are sold, labor would cost $8.59 per pig sold. Administrative costs are estimated to be $3.50 per hog sold, or $2 per feeder pig sold. Some of the other production costs include manure handling, feed delivery, and utilities. Manure handling costs are based on a custom handling price of $0.01 per gallon.
