Bake the Brisket. 1  Place a fat-trimmed 3- to 3-1/2-pound fresh beef brisket in a 13x9x2-inch baking pan. 2  Cover the pan with foil. 3  Bake in a 325 degrees F oven about 3 hours or until tender, turning once halfway through cooking time. 1 Pour the sauce over the brisket. 2  Cover the slow cooker and cook on the low-heat setting for 12 to 14 hours. 3  Or cook on the high-heat setting for 6 to 7 hours. 4  Serve the sliced brisket with the cooking liquid.
