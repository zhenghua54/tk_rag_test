Use Keyboard. Another easy way to make your text bold in Microsoft Word is with the keyboard. To make text bold by using the keyboard, do the following. 1  Highlight the text you want to make bold. 2  Hold down the Ctrl key on your keyboard. 3  Keep the Ctrl key held down. 4  Press the letter B on your keyboard. 5  To switch off the bold text, keep the Ctrl key held down and press the B key again. 1 Highlight the text you want to make bold. 2  Hold down the Ctrl key on your keyboard. 3  Keep the Ctrl key held down. 4  Press the letter B on your keyboard. 5  To switch off the bold text, keep the Ctrl key held down and press the B key again.
