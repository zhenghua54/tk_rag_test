Generally the cost of FUE hair transplants can range between US $4 per graft to US $15 per graft. Cost of FUT hair transplant in Dubai, on the other hand, can range between US $2 and US $10 per graft. Some clinics also offer discount for larger number of grafts, but you need to discuss this during initial consultation. There are two major types of hair transplant depending on the extraction of graft strips or follicle units from the donor area. The first one is Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) while the other one is Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE).
