Consequently if Myles Standish was descended from either of the Standish families of the Pele or Burgh he would have been born upon the Manor of Chorley within the parish of Chorley and his birth lawfully registered for the year 1584 in the register of the Parish Church of Chorley St. Laurence. In the year 1584 when Myles Standish was born, both of the Standish families who were the major landowners of the Manor of Duxbury resided upon the Manor of Chorley within the Parish of Chorley. Consequently if Myles Standish were born at the Pele Tower or the Burgh in the year 1584 his birth would have been entered into the register of the church of St. Laurence in common with other members of these families.
