The Nurse tells Juliet to forget Romeo and marry Paris. When Juliet's mother and father have left, Juliet asks the Nurse what she should do. The Nurse says to forget Romeo and just marry Paris without owning up. Capulet is in a good mood. Juliet tells her father that she has been to see the Friar to confess her sins and asks for her dad's forgiveness. Even the Nurse believes her. Juliet says good night to her mum then starts to panic about what she is about to do.
