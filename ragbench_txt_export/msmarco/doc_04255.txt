Davina /dav-ina/ [3 sylls.] as a girls' name is pronounced dah-VEE-nah. It is of Scottish and Hebrew origin, and the meaning of Davina is beloved. Feminine of David. Kreatif forms: Deavina, Duvina. Davina is alike in pronunciation to Davonna, Devana and Devona.
