Satomi Murano (村野 里美 lit. Murano Satomi) is a deuteragonist in the series. Shinichi's best friend and sometime companion. Background Edit. A young Satomi is shown to approach Shinichi during the results of their high school entrance exams, after he found out that he was accepted. A brief misunderstanding happened between the two of them when she thought that Shinichi wasn't the person she thought him to be.
