Lanzarote: Annual Weather Averages. August is the hottest month in Lanzarote with an average temperature of 25°C (77°F) and the coldest is January at 17°C (63°F) with the most daily sunshine hours at 10 in August. The wettest month is December with an average of 29mm of rain. The best month to swim in the sea is in August when the average sea temperature is 22°C (72°F).
