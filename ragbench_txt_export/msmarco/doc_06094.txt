Opioids may act just like opiates in the human body, because of the similar molecules. opiate-narcotic analgesic derived from a opium poppy (natural). opioid-narcotic analgesic that is at least part synthetic, not found in nature. The terms are often used interchangeably. On the street, heroin may mean synthetic, natural, or semi-synthetic compounds. Common opiates include morphine and codeine, both made directly from poppy plants. An opioid is a substance (molecule) that is synthetic or partly synthetic, meaning the active ingredients (molecules) are manufactured via chemical synthesis.
