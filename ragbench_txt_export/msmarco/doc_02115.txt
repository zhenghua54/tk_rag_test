The recommended dose in adults is 1.2 mg to 2.4 mg daily. The dose should be increased as needed to control disease and as tolerated in increments of 0.3 mg/day to a maximum recommended daily dose. The maximum recommended dose for prophylaxis of gout flares is 1.2 mg/day. Treatment of Gout Flares: 1.2 mg (2 tablets) at the first sign of the flare followed by 0.6 mg (1 tablet) one hour later. The maximum recommended dose for treatment of gout flares is 1.8 mg over a 1 hour period.
