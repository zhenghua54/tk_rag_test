The temperature rises most days in March to over 70 °F (21 °C) in Orlando. March temperatures above 90 °F (32 °C) happen here once every few years. The thermometer dips to 50 °F (10 °C) or below on an average of 7 nights. Occasionally March does get as cold as 40 °F (4 °C). Sunlight in March at Orlando lasts for 12 hours and 1 minute a day on average. The shortest day of the month is the 1st with 11 hours and 35 minutes of daylight. By the end of the month, the days have lengthened to 12:27 from sunrise to sunset.
