ramification. 1  a ramifying or being ramified; specif., the arrangement of branches or offshoots, as on a plant. 2  the result of ramifying; specif., 3  a branch or offshoot. 4  a derived effect, consequence, or result: the ramifications of an act. Noun. (plural ramifications). ( 1 botany, anatomy) A branching-out, the act or result of developing branches; specifically the divergence of the stem and limbs of a plant into smaller ones, or of similar developments in blood vessels, anatomical structures etc.
