A sonnet is a form of poem originated from Europe-traditionally it is composed of 14 lines that follows strict rhyme scheme and specific structure. Rhyme structure could be abab cdcd efef gg... but conventions associated with the sonnet have evolved over its history. 11 people found this useful. The Shakespearean sonnet has three quartets and a couplet, and a rhyme scheme of abab,cdcd,efef,gg. The sonnet you will most often see when you study literature in a high school English class is the Shakespearean sonnet.
