Complications of diabetes include: 1  Heart Disease – People with diabetes have a higher risk for heart attack and stroke. 2  Eye Complications – People with diabetes have a higher risk of blindness and other vision problems. 3  Kidney Disease – Diabetes can damage the kidneys and may lead to kidney failure. 1 Foot Problems – Nerve damage, infections of the feet, and problems with blood flow to the feet can be caused by diabetes. 2  Skin Complications – Diabetes can cause skin problems, such as infections, sores, and itching. 3  Skin problems are sometimes a first sign that someone has diabetes.
