First-year associates earned a median pay of $72,000 a year at firms with 25 or fewer attorneys, notes NALP. Those working at firms with up to 50 lawyers earned $95,000, while those working for law firms of 100 to 250 lawyers earned $105,000 a year. Associates with five years of experience earned $90,000 a year at firms with 25 or fewer attorneys, while these same lawyers could earn $120,000 at firms with 100 to 250 lawyers.
