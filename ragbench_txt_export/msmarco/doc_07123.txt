Though being a communist Laos was also referred to as the democracy of people and had a strong hold on factors such as traditions, politics and social behavior. The rich and influential people of the land ruled over the country and the people of lower strata served them. Every five years a president is elected on the basis of two-thirds of votes of the National Assembly and the President has the power to appoint or dismiss the prime minister or any other members of the government with the approval of the National Assembly.
