Ayden /ay-den/ [2 sylls.] as a boys' name (also used less widely as girls' name Ayden) is of Gaelic origin, and Ayden means fire. Ayden is an alternate spelling of Aidan (Gaelic). Adon is a conventional surname. Kreatif forms: Aadden, Anden, Aydenno. Charted below is the birth name popularity of Ayden for boys. Ayden was first listed in 1999 and reached its top position of #69 in the U.S. in 2012, and is presently at #74. (Top 1000 Baby Names Statistics, 2014).
