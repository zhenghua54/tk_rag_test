Our kidney consists of renal parenchyma and renal pelvis, and renal parenchyma consists of renal cortex and renal medulla, which are the parts that are in charge of regulating our internal body balance. Renal parenchymal disease means kidney disease appears and causes damage in their renal parenchyma. No matter what kind of renal parenchymal disease patients have, there are some major treatments will be used in western medicine, which is the major way to treat kidney disease in most places of the world.
