Onychomycosis is a fungal infection of the toenails or fingernails that can be painful, disfiguring, and emotionally disturbing. The most common form, distal subungual, is shown in the image on the left. Note that the fungus invades the nail bed and inflammation occurs. Cutaneous fungal infections are noninvasive infections of the skin, hair, or nails that cause pathologic changes in the host. The fungi can be yeast-like, causing infections such as candidiasis, or mold-like, causing tinea infection
