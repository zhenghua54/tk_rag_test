To gamble in the casino, passengers must be 21 or older. Those 18 and younger must share a cabin with a parent or guardian 21 or older. Disney Cruise Line (www.disneycruiseline.com) All passengers must be 21 years old to drink alcohol and you must be at least 18 to occupy a cabin without an adult. Norwegian Cruise Line (www.ncl.com) Passengers ages 18 to 20, with the written consent of their parents or guardian, can purchase beer or wine for themselves only when the ship is sailing in international waters (except in Alaska and Hawaii, where you must be 21 years of age to consume or purchase alcohol or any kind).
