The standard unit of measuring land in the United States is the ACRE. An Acre is equal to 43,560 square feet (Sq. Ft.) of area. An acre is also equal to 10 square chains (66 x 66 x 10 = 43560 Sq. Ft.) or 160 square rods (16.5 x 16.5 x 160 = 43,560 Sq. Ft.) There are exactly 640 Acres in one square mile.
