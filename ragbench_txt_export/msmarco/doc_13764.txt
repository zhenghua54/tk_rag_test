Android. To access your Outlook.com email on an Android-based smart phone or tablet, you’ll need Microsoft’s free Hotmail app, which is available both from Google Play and from Amazon’s AppStore for Android. So let's start with that. Apple uses a centralized accounts mechanism in Settings, and since Outlook.com is a full Exchange ActiveSync (EAS) service, you can use the Exchange account type to configure this account. To do so, visit Settings and then Mail, Contacts, Calendars. Then, tap Add Account. In the Add Account screen, tap Microsoft Exchange. In this screen, enter your email address (whatever@outlook.com), username (whatever@outlook.com, same as the email address field), password, and a description (like “Outlook.com” or whatever).
