Salary Overview. According to PayScale.com, the median wage for a CNA was $22,918 per year as of January 2014. Salaries in the 10th through 90th percentiles ranged from $18,044-$31,514 annually. Important factors that can affect your salary as a CNA include your location, experience and your industry within the nursing field. Salaries by Experience. January 2014 data from PayScale.com showed that the reported salaries for CNAs varied by number of years of experience. The salary range for those possessing less than a year of experience was $17,544-$43,359, while those with 1-4 years of experience reported salaries of $14,371-$38,701.
