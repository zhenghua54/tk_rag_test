Degenerate Art also was the title of an exhibition, held by the Nazis in Munich in 1937, consisting of modernist artworks chaotically hung and accompanied by text labels deriding the art. A large amount of 'degenerate art' by Picasso, Dali, Dalí, Ernst, Klee leger Léger and miro miró was destroyed in a bonfire on The night, of, july 27 1942 in the Gardens of the Galerie nationale Du jeu De. paume in paris
