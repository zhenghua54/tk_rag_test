What is the available temperature range on Viking Undercounter Refrigeration Products? For the Undercounter Beverage Center (VBCI) : temperature can be adjusted from 32 to 48 degrees F (0-8.9 degrees C); factory set at 42 degrees F (5.6 degrees C). One. Viking refrigerators are powered by Viking ProChill™ Temperature Management System. This includes and advanced DC Overdrive™ Compressor, multi-fan air distribution and electronic temperature control.
