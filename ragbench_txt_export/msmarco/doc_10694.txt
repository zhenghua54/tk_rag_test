If you’re healthy, over-consumption of protein likely won’t hurt you, as long as you are not eating too many foods high in saturated fat and skipping healthful carbohydrate-rich foods such as vegetables and fruit. Under-consumption of protein is rare in the United States, and can lead to malnutrition and serious illness. Foods rich in proteins include animal-based foods such as meats, poultry, fish, eggs and dairy products. Among plant-based foods, the best protein sources include legumes such as beans, peas, peanuts and especially soybeans and soy-based foods
