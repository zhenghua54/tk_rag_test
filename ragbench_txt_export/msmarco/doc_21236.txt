starch is digested in the mouth by the enzyme carbohydrase (amylase), which digests them into dextrines (sugars or glocuse). protein is digested in the stomach by the enzyme … protease, which converts them into amino acids. fats are digested in the duodenum (small intestine) with bile (from liver) and the enzyme lipase, which digests them into glycerol and fatty acids. The digestion of fat will begin in the small intestine, whereas the digestion of starch which begin in the mouth (the mouth contains the enzyme amylase, which helps break down … starch).
