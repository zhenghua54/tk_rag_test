Boilermaker Wages. Boilermaker apprentices are typically paid a percentage of a journeyman boilermaker's wages. Hour wages vary regionally, but the national mean hourly wage for boilermakers was $27.23 as of May 2011, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics. Apprentices in the International Brotherhood of Boilermakers Union Local No. 7 in Orchard Park, New York, start at 65 percent of a journeyman's wages, and receive a 5 percent increase after each six-month period.
