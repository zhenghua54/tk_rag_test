On-campus housing and dining at Henderson State University has changed an average of 4.9% for each of the past five years. If current growth trends in room, board and other expenses continue, we expect this year's incoming freshmen to pay $11,933 their first year of college. By their final year they will pay approximately $12,234 for an associate degree seeker, and $12,859 for a bachelor degree seeker to cover these expenses Over the entire degree (assuming you graduate on-time) the total will come to $49,569 for a four year stay or $24,168 for a two year stay.
