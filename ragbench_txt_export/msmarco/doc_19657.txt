Many travellers flying into Beijing Airport (PEK) plan to visit the amazing Great Wall of China, a famous World Heritage Site and one of the great wonders of the world. The city of Beijing is one of the most convenient bases for trips to the Great Wall, which lies directly north. Beijing Capital International Airport terminal is located around 27 km / 17 miles from Beijing city centre and close to the suburbs of Kailiying, Shunihsien and Shunyi.
