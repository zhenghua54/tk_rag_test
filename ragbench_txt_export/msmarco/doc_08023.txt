In Pakistan, this system is used in Urdu and other regional language media, but not in English-language media. The terms lakh (100,000 – that is to say 1,00,000 in the Indian system) and crore (10,000,000 – that is to say 1,00,00,000 in the Indian system) are used in Indian English to express large numbers. 1 In Telugu, a lakh is called లక్ష laksha and a crore is called కోటి koti. 2  In Urdu, a lakh is called لاکھ lākh and a crore is called کروڑ karoṛ. 3  Lakh has entered the Swahili language as  laki  and is in common use.
