Origin of the name Vincent: French form of the Late Latin Vincentius (conquering), which is derived from vincere (to conquer). Var: Vince. From A World of Baby Names by Teresa Norman. Vincent s derived from the Latin name Vincentius meaning conquering (from Latin vincere - to conquer). The French brought the name to the English and the Irish. The Gaelic forms of Vincent are “Uinseann” and “Uinsionn.”.
