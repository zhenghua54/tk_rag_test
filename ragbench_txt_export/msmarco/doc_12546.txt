Shrimp Cindy 2015-04-21T07:21:57+00:00. How long does shrimp last? The shelf life of shrimp depends on a variety of factors, such as the sell by date, the preparation method and how they were stored. You can help shrimp keep fresh longer by storing it in your refrigerator immediately after purchase. Once prepared, shrimp should be stored in a tightly closed container to keep out moisture and other contaminants.
