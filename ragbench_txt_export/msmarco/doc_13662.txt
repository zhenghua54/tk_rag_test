Distinguishing Features: Broadleaf plantain has green, oval to egg-shaped leaves that grow in a rosette. These leaves have thick stems that meet at a base. When these stems are broken, they reveal string-like veins that resemble those in celery. Fields of Nutrition has medicinal benefits and vitamin/mineral content of Broadleaf Plantain.
