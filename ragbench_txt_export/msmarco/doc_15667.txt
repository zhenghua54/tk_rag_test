The acquisition cost for intensity-modulated radiation therapy (IMRT) systems is far less pricey, ranging from $1.8 to $5.4 million, according to a 2009 report by the Institute for Clinical and Economic Review on management options for low-risk prostate cancer. The treatment also has a much higher price tag. The major issue with proton therapy is the cost of the treatment and of the treatment facility. The cost to equip and construct a suitable proton center can range from $25 to $150 million.
