Here's my tried-and-true method for cooking blue crab: 1  Put an inch or two of water into a big, sturdy pot. 2  Cover the pot, put it on the stove or over a hot campfire, and bring the water to a rolling boil. 3  When the water is boiling, drop your crabs into the pot. 4  Be sure not to pack them in too tightly.
