Chattahoochee Technical College is public, 2-4 years located in Marietta, Georgia. For academic year 2014-2015, the undergraduate tuition & fees at Chattahoochee Technical College is $2,682 for in-state and $4,818 for out-of-state students. Average living costs is $5,899 for students living off campus. On-campus living costs stayed same from the previous year
