The study comes from MSU's College Employment Research Institute, and charts the average starting salaries for new college graduates in a variety of majors. According to the MSU study, the average starting salary among all new college graduates is $39,045. The lowest average starting salary — advertising — is $36,638. Perhaps unsurprisingly, the MSU list is dominated by engineering majors.
