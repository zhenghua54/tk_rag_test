Singulair is indicated for the treatment of asthma and allergies. Common side effects reported with Singulair include headache, upset stomach, nausea and diarrhea, tiredness, sore throat and cough. A search of prescribing information for Singulair did not specifically list weight gain as a side effect. Most people who use Singulair do not have serious side effects. Singulair does have some common side effects that should decrease or go away in time. These include: 1  Headache. 2  Stomach pain, heartburn, upset stomach, nausea, diarrhea. 3  Tooth pain. 4  Tired feeling. 5  Dizziness. 6  Fever, stuffy nose, sore throat, cough, hoarseness. 7  Mild rash
