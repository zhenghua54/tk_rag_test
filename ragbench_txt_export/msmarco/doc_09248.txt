Directions. Cook. Ready In. 1  In a small, heavy saucepan, heat cream and vanilla until bubbles form at edges. 2  While cream is heating, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until smooth. 3  Slowly pour 1/2 cup of hot milk mixture into egg yolks, whisking constantly. Creme Anglaise II. This simple custard sauce is made with half-and-half, egg yolks, butter and sugar and flavored with a vanilla bean.
