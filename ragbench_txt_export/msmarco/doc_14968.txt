Generic Name: Amphetamine-Dextroamphetamine. D-amphetamine Salt Combo is a combination of dextroamphetamine and amphetamine, stimulants used for Attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) and for narcolepsy. Amphetamine and dextroamphetamine can cause side effects that may impair your thinking or reactions. Be careful if you drive or do anything that requires you to be awake and alert. Do not take amphetamine and dextroamphetamine late in the day. A dose taken too late in the day can cause sleep problems (insomnia).
