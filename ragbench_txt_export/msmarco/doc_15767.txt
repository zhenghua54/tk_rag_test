The most important features of aluminium profile are installation, practicability, solidity and property of design. By the means of connection accessories which are designed according to the canals of extruded aluminium profiles that have best appropriate cuts, it is possible to install quicker. There is no need painting or stoning later like needed in steel construction in the means of those factors. A more stablel and harder connection against to static and dynamic charges is possible by the means of 8 mm and 10 mm canal sizes of specially designed profiles according to the practice concepts.
