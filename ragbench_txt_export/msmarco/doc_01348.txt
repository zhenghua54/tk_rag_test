Re: Judgements. A Nevada Judgment is enforceable for six (6) years, and can be renewed by following the procedure set forth in the Nevada Revised Statutes here: http://www.leg.state.nv.us/NRS/NRS-017.html#NRS017Sec214. NRS 17.214 Filing and contents of affidavit; recording affidavit of renewal; notice to judgment debtor. 1.
