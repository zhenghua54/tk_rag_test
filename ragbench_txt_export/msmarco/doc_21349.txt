Overview. Medium-chain triglycerides, or MCT, are triglycerides with a fatty acid chain length of six to 10 carbon atoms, according to a 2010 study in the International Journal of Food Sciences and Nutrition.. Photo Credit joannawnuk/iStock/Getty Images. Coconut oil is a good source of medium-chain triglycerides, according to the Center for Science in the Public Interest. A report in Medical News Today states that coconut oil is not stored in the fat and also may boost metabolism.
