Put the onion and garlic powder, black pepper and parsley into a bowl. Stir with either your fingers or a spoon to combine the spices together. Line the roasting pan with aluminum foil to keep cleaning the pan to a minimum. Place the picnic shoulder in the pan, keeping the side with the most fat facing up. Add a cup of cold water to the pan.
