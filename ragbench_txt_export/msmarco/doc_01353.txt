Cartographers and photogrammetrists earned a mean $60,110 per year, or $28.90 per hour, as of May 2011, according to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics. The lowest paid 10 percent made less than $34,810 annually, or $16.74 hourly, while the best paid 10 percent received more than $92,310 per year, or $44.38 per hour. Postsecondary Teachers. Postsecondary teachers made a mean $72,300 per year, with an annual range of $40,110 to $113,600, as of May 2011, according to the BLS.
