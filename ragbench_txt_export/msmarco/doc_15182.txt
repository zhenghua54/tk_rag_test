Ralph Standish of Standish, mentioned above, would belong to the same generation as Myles of Plymouth, one born about 1576, or a little later, the other in 1585. The great-grandfather of Myles would belong, then, to the same generation as Alexander, who died 1542. He had two brothers, Roger and George. Myles Standish, born Abt. 1629; died Aft. 03/20/1660-61 in Disappeared at Sea.He married Sarah Winslow, Standish, Payne 07/19/1660 in Boston, Suffolk County, Massachusetts, New England; born Abt.
