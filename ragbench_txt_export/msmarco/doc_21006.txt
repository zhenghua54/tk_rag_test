The AFCT is a version of the ASVAB, and it is administered in the same manner as the ASVAB's paper-version. NPS applicants with AFQT scores less than 50 must have a Tier I education status (i.e... Traditional high school diploma graduate, 15 semester hours of accredited college credit or other approved avenues-check with your Navy Recruiter for your education Tier status). Check with your local Navy Recruiter for updates. ASVAB line score data current as of June 1, 2015 according to COMNAVCRUITCOMINST 1130.8J latest change, Change 8.
