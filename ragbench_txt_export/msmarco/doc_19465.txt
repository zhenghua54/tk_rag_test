Kale is basically a cabbage type vegetable that does not form a head. Kale is tasty when cooked or kept small to use in salads. Learn how to harvest kale at the right time to encourage the most flavorful leaves. Harvesting kale for use in soups, stews and mixed greens that are cooked allows use of the larger leaves. Harvesting kale may include taking a few tender inner leaves or removing the entire bunch by cutting at the roots. Using as a garnish may take a large or small part of the kale harvest. Advertisement.
