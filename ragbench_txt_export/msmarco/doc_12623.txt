Pay by Experience Level for Pharmacist. Median of all compensation (including tips, bonus, and overtime) by years of experience. Experience and pay tend to be weakly linked for Pharmacists — those with more experience do not necessarily bring in higher earnings. Even the relatively untried can take home bulky six-figure salaries; employees who have worked for fewer than five years report earning $108K on average. In fact, this transition is more common than anything else, and median pay for the role is $105K. Another common career choice for Pharmacists is to move into a Pharmacist in Charge role — this promotion usually pays $116K.
