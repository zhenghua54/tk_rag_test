Poured Concrete. According to the Concrete Network, the majority of the cost of a concrete foundation is the concrete itself. The price of concrete varies by region, but as of 2008, it averaged around $75 per cubic yard, or just under $3 per cubic foot. Concrete also requires forms and finishing work at between $1 and $1.50 per square foot and reinforcement with rebar or mesh at a cost of about $.10 per square foot.
