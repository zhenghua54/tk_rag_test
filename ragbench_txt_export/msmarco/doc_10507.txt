Obesity traditionally has been defined as a weight at least 20% above the weight corresponding to the lowest death rate for individuals of a specific height, gender, and age (ideal weight). Hypertrophic obesity results from an increase in the size of the fat cells in the increased adipose tissue mass. observations Manifestations are excess body weight for height. Overweight is determined by a body mass index (BMI) of 25 to 29.9 kg/m 2, and obesity is a BMI = 30 kg/m 2.
