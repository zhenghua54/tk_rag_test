Phuket has its own international airport located in the northern end of the island, and a number of major airlines offer daily flights to Phuket. It is also possible to reach Phuket by boat, with a ferries linking Phuket Town and Ko Phi-Phi three times a day. Phuket's most popular beaches sit on the western side of the island. These include Patong Beach, Karon Beach, Kata Beach, Kata Noi Beach and Nai Harn and Rawai on the southern tip
