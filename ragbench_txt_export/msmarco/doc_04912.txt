Mutaflor is a probiotic comprised of viable non-pathogenic bacteria strain named Escherichia coli Nissle 1917. Mutaflor has been on the market for almost a century and has been clinically studied to show effectiveness in preventing relapse and maintenance of remission of ulcerative colitis. The usual dosage of Mutaflor for Chronic Constipation in adolesecents (12-17 years old) is: One capsule per day for the first 4 days. Two capsules daily started on day 5.
