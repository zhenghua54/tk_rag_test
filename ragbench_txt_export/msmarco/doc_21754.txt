What does Vincent mean, popularity, numerology and more. The name Vincent is of Latin origin. The meaning of Vincent is conquering. It is also of English origin, where its meaning is to conquer. Vincent is generally used as a boy's name. It consists of 7 letters and 2 syllables and is pronounced Vin-cent. In the U.S. in 2014, it ranked 104 in baby name popularity for boys with 3744 occurrences. Less than 5 girls were given the name.
