1,200 DIFFERENT FISH SPECIES IN THE MEKONG BASIN The problem is that no matter how many species you find, you can always go out tomorrow and find others that you have not seen before, says Dr Rainboth, working for the MRC Secretariat on the production of a new guide to fish species in the Mekong River. As reported in the February issue of Catch and Culture, a field guide to identify Mekong fish species in Cambodia is being prepared for the Mekong River Commission by Dr Walter Rainboth of the Department of Biology, University of Wisconsin, Oshkosh, USA.
