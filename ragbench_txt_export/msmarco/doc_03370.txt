Registered Nurse Salary in New York. A survey conducted by the US Bureau of Labor Statistics reveals that the mean wage of a registered nurse in New York was $75,470 and per hour salary was $36.29 in May 2013. New York employed 169,820 RNs in May 2013. Employment per 1000 jobs was 19.666. The figures in indeed.com reveal that the average RN salary in NY was. $80,000 as of Oct 2014. It was 21% greater than the average registered nurse salary for job postings throughout the nation
