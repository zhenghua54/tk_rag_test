Coat the primed plastic with acrylic spray paint. Again, spray in a sweeping, side-to-side motion, maintaining a distance of 8 inches between the plastic and the spray nozzle as you apply. Coat the abraded plastic with acrylic spray primer. Spray in a sweeping, side-to-side motion. Maintain a distance of 8 inches between the plastic and the spray nozzle as you apply.
