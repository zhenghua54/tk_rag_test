Rhinoplasty is a plastic surgery procedure that re-shapes the outside of the nose to provide better balance with the rest of the face. It involves correcting deformities be removing, re-arranging or re-shaping the bone and cartilage. # of rhinoplasty procedures done on patients ages 20-29 — 78,051. # of rhinoplasty procedures done on patients ages 30-39 — 63,850. # of rhinoplasty procedures done on patients ages ≥40 — 55,204. Source: The American Board of Plastic Surgery.
