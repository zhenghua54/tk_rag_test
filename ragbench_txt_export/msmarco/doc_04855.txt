A single dry coconut, place on a white plate in front of eleggua or behind the front door of the house. Light on a candle (white or red) above the coconut for three days at the same time. Asking Elegua order to remove the envy off the house and yourself. After three days throw at a crossroad of four corners with 3 or 21 cents (coins. TO PREPARE A LETTER TO THE FOOT OF Elegua.
