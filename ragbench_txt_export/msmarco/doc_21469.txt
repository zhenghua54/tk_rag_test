Side Effects and Cautions. 1  Drinking cranberry juice products appears to be safe, although excessive amounts could cause gastrointestinal upset or diarrhea. 2  People who think they have a urinary tract infection should see a health care provider for proper diagnosis and treatment. These red berries are used in foods and in herbal products. Historically, cranberry fruits and leaves were used for a variety of problems, such as wounds, urinary disorders, diarrhea, diabetes, stomach ailments, and liver problems.
