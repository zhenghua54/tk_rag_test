Equipment that qualifies for energy tax credits. Residential geothermal heat pumps, wind turbines and solar-power systems installed before the end of 2016 also qualify for a separate 30 percent credit. You can claim this for new or existing buildings, and for your vacation home as well as your primary residence. OVERVIEW. In past years, as an incentive to conserve energy at home, the federal government has offered tax credits to homeowners who purchase energy-efficient appliances.
