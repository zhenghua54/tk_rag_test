Chorando Se Foi (Lambada) was the 37th best-selling single of the United Kingdom during 1989. In France, where it topped the chart for 12 weeks and sold almost two million copies, the single was the number-one on the year-end list. It was released on November 2005 as the third single from the album. She also recorded the song on her second live album. The British girl group Spice Girls mention Lambada - either the song or the dance-in their 1998 hit single  Spice Up Your Life .
