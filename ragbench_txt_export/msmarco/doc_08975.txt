When To Be At The Gate Once you have checked in for your flight and, if necessary, checked bags, the only time that really matters is the “Boarding Time” printed on your boarding pass, usually between 30-60 minutes before scheduled departure. Generally speaking, the boarding gate closes 15-30 minutes before departure. Depending on how you checked in, where your final destination is and whether you have a connection (to name a few), you might need to show up earlier or later than the suggested time.
