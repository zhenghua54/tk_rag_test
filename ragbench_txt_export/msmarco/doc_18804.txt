Charles Richard Ricky Wilson (born 17 January 1978) is the lead singer of English band Kaiser Chiefs, a five-piece band from Menston and Guiseley in Leeds, West Yorkshire. Life and career [edit]. Wilson was born in Keighley, West Yorkshire. He attended Leeds Grammar School after Ghyll Royd primary school. He later attended Leeds Metropolitan University and completed his BA (Hons) degree in Graphic Arts and Design in 2000. He then taught at Leeds College of Art for a year before the band's big break
