Geographic information systems (GIS) is a term used to describe the collection and collation of various types of data in order to visually represent that data, most often in the form of charts or maps. These systems are used by a vast array of organizations for an even larger number of applications. A Geographic Information Systems (GIS) Specialist earns an average salary of $48,926 per year. People in this job generally don't have more than 20 years' experience.
