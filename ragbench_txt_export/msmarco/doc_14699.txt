Salary by State or District. Average salaries for service coordinators vary somewhat by state or district. They earned an average salary of $68,000 in Washington, D.C., according to SimplyHired.com. They also made comparatively high salaries in Massachusetts and New York -- $52,000 and $51,000 per year, respectively. Those who have been with a company 20 years, for example, might make $50,000 or more on average -- versus $35,000 for service coordinators just starting their careers.
