Definition of COELOMODUCT. : an excretory and genital duct that is typical of certain invertebrates (as annelid worms) and has a wide and usually ciliated lumen opening into the coelom by a broad funnel and terminating externally by a small pore in the body wall. ADVERTISEMENT. Origin of COELOMODUCT. coelom + -o- + duct.
