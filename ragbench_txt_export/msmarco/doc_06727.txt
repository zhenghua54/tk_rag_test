The Goosebumps series falls under the children's fiction, horror and thriller genres, although Stine characterizes the series as scary books that are also funny.. Each book features different child characters and settings. The primary protagonists are middle class and can be either male or female. Goosebumps has spawned a television series and merchandise, as well as a feature film, starring Jack Black as R. L. Stine. Since the release of its first novel, Welcome to Dead House, in July 1992, the series has sold over 350 million books worldwide in 32 languages.
