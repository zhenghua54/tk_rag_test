Skin lotions and topical anti-inflammatory treatments tout aloe vera as a healing and soothing wonder gel. The gel comes from the thick leaves of the aloe vera plant. It does more than just leave skin soft, however. Aloe vera is used to heal first-and second-degree burns, protect against radiation damage, rev up the immune system, make bowels move, and fight viruses and bacteria
