Granite is one of the most widespread rocks in earth crust. Rocks (mountain breeds) – dense and loose masses making the Earth’s crust. Rocks (mountain breeds) consist of one material. Originally rocks (mountain breeds) are divided into igneous or magmatic. The exceptionally decorative variety of granite is amazonite (= amazon-stone) granite of green and blue-green color, containing in the structure green feldspar-amazonite (on the name of the river of Amazon, where it was first found out).
