Employers. The largest employers of social work supervisors were organizations that provide individual and family services, according to the BLS. The average pay here was $58,820 yearly as of May 2011. Other major employers were local governments and state governments. Locations. The state with the most opportunities for social work supervisors was California, with 14,560 jobs. Supervisors there averaged $68,930 per year as of May 2011, the BLS reports.
