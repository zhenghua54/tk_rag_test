Do-It-Yourself. If you are willing to cut the grass yourself, the cost is nothing. If you don't already own a lawn mower, you will have to invest in one but the cost of the mower will quickly pay for itself if you compare the cost of hiring a professional landscaper for the job. The Neighborhood Lawn Mower. Another option is hire a teenager in the neighborhood to mow your lawn. He will cost far less than a professional landscaper, but you still have the added convenience. Some may charge as little as $10 to $15 for an average size lawn but maybe not even more than $25 or $30 for a large lawn.
