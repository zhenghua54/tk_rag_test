These systems are typically used for moving liquids and gases in buildings, marine vessels, aircraft, and industrial process plants. Unlike a plumber, a pipefitter can fabricate pipes, as well as install and maintain them. Some pipefitters are are specially trained to work in distilleries and breweries. A person who wants to become a pipefitter must complete all classroom training requirements, which includes welding.
