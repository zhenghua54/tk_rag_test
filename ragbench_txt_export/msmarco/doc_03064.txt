Step 2. Place the slices of ham on the bottom of the skillet and season as desired. For example, add a glaze or sprinkle on garlic powder. There is no need to use oil in the skillet when frying ham steak. As the ham heats, the juices in the meat will prevent sticking. Pre-sliced ham can mean one of two things: ham slices or a full ham cut to aid in carving. Ham steak is individual ham slices cut to varying thicknesses. Utilizing these slices provides an economical way to enjoy ham without purchasing a whole roast.
