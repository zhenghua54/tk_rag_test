Fluticasone furoate is a prescription medication used to prevent symptoms of asthma. Fluticasone furoate can also treat the symptoms of allergic and non-allergic rhinitis. Fluticasone furoate is in a class of medications called corticosteroids. Back to Top. Fluticasone furoate is a prescription medication used to prevent symptoms of asthma, including difficulty breathing, chest tightness, wheezing, and coughing, in adults and children aged 12 years and older.
