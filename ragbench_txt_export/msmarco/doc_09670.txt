What is Airbus? Airbus is an aircraft manufacturing subsidiary of a European aerospace company called EADS and is based in Toulouse, France next to Toulouse-Blagnac Airport (2009) . Airbus is one of the leading manufacturers of aircraft in the world and supplies about half of all the world’s jet airliners. Their mission is to “provide the aircraft best suited to the markets needs and to support these aircraft with the highest quality of service” (2009) . Although Airbus’ headquarters are in France, they also have subsidiaries in North America, China and Japan.
