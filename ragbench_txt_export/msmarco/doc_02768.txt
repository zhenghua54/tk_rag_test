Apply Online. You can apply online for Tennessee Food Stamp program. All you have to do is submit an online application. After your application is received and viewed by DHS, you will be giving a time and date for interview. Here are different requirements of the program: 1  All applicants should be residing in the state of Tennessee to receive Food Stamps in Tennessee. 2  Children under the age of 21 and living with their parents will have to apply for the program as one household.
