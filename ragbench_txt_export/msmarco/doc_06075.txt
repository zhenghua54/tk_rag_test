Report Abuse. It depends on the breed of quail. Bobwhite quail take 23 - 24 days to hatch, give or take a couple of days, Pharaoh quail eggs take 17 days, give or take. You should remember to turn them 3 times a day and stop turning them 3 days before they hatch and don't remove them from the incubator too early.
