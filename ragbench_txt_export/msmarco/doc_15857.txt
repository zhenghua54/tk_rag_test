NBA Basketball Court Dimensions. As an independent basketball organization, the NBA has its very own basketball court dimensions. In terms of length, the court is set at 94 feet. When it comes to width, it is supposed to have a measurement of 50 feet. The restraining circle’s diameter is 4 feet. The center circle’s diameter is 12 feet. The dimensions of a basketball court are different under the rules of FIBA, which basically stands for Federation Internationale de Basketball. When it comes to the distance of the 3-point line, the sides are 20.5 feet away from the middle of the basket.
