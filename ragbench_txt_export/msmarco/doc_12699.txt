Check the internal temperature of the boneless chicken thighs by inserting an instant-read thermometer about halfway through the chicken thighs. When the chicken is finished cooking, the thermometer should read 165 degrees Fahrenheit. Tom Smaldone. Brush the boneless chicken thighs lightly all over with extra virgin olive oil.
