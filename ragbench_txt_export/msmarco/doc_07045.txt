Indirect Heat Grilling on Gas Grills. Once the ham is prepared and the gas grill have been heated properly (see Gas Grill Setup), there are several easy steps to follow in order to cook ham using indirect heat on a gas grill. 1  All of the burners, except one, are turned off. Grilling Ham Slices. 1  Preheat the grill to medium heat. 2  To help prevent the ham slices from sticking to the grate on which they will be placed, the grate can be brushed with oil and the grill should be properly preheated before placing the meat on the grate.
