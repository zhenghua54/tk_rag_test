State of the Union 2015. “Our European Union is not in a good state. There is not enough Europe in this Union. And there is not enough Union in this Union.” This morning, European Commission President Jean-Claude Juncker delivered his first State of the Union address before the European Parliament in Strasbourg. The EU, winner of the 2012 Nobel Peace Prize, is represented in the United States by the Delegation of the European Union in Washington, DC.
