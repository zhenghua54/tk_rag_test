The nice thing is, the cactus is really easy to grow, there is really just one key to growing the cactus plant and that is the amount of water that is given to it. You will find that there are a few other names people know it by; one of the most entertaining is “Mother-in-law’s Cushion.” As a floral meaning, the cactus represents longevity and endurance; this is a plant that can handle what is thrown at it in terms of the elements.
