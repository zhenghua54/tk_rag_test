Several of the most popular species include: 1  Hydrangea arborescens (Wild hydrangea). 2  Hydrangea macrophylla (Bigleaf hydrangea). 3  Hydrangea paniculata (Panicled hydrangea). 4  Hydrangea paniculata 'Grandiflora' (Pee Gee hydrangea). Hydrangea macrophylla also known as the bigleaf hydrangea, florist hydrangea or French hydrangea, is the most popular species. This diverse group includes more than 600 named cultivars encompassing both lace cap and mophead subtypes.
