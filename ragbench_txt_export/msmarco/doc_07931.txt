1 Pound the steaks to about 1/4-inch thickness. 2  Place 2 cups of flour in a shallow bowl. 3  Stir together the baking powder, baking soda, pepper, and salt in a separate shallow bowl; stir in the buttermilk, egg, Tabasco Sauce, and garlic. 4  Dredge each steak first in the flour, then in the batter, and again in the flour.
