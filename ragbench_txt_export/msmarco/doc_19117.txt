Parsley is the most commonly used platter garnish and sandwich filling around the world. Its mild flavor enhances the taste of the dishes. It makes a significant part of daily American, European and Middle Eastern cuisines. It is a species of Petroselinum and belongs to the family of Apiaceae. 1 It is widely and most commonly used as a garnish. 2  It can be used in salads. 3  It contains carminative and anti-septic properties, hence, used a poultice. 4  Parsley leaves extraction can be used for its diuretics effects.
