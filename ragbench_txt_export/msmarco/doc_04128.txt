Heritage Natural Hydraulic Lime Stucco/Plaster is a dry material with aggregate preblended into the product. The product is applied in three coats: a scratch coat, a brown coat and a skim coat. This product is ready to use; just add water and mix at the site before applying the stucco. Heritage Hydraulic Lime Stucco/Plaster. This product is a traditional material made from 100% all-natural ingredients, and it is breathable and flexible. Hydraulic lime is distinguished from non-hydraulic lime by its ability to cure in damp/wet conditions.
