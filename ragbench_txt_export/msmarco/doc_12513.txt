The visceral pericardium is the inner-most layer of the pericardium, and is also known as the Epicardium as it lays upon the heart. The Pericardial Cavity lies between the visceral pericardium and the parietal pericardium. The pericardium is a sac of fibrous tissue that surrounds the heart and the roots of the large blood vessels. Visceral pericardium is the inner layer of the pericardium sac.
