Dark corn syrup in commercial packaging. Corn syrup is a food syrup which is made from the starch of maize (called corn in some countries) and contains varying amounts of maltose and higher oligosaccharides, depending on the grade. 1 Dark corn syrup is a combination of corn syrup and molasses (or Refiners' syrup), caramel color and flavor, salt, and the preservative sodium benzoate. 2  Dark corn syrup is a warm brown color and tastes much stronger than light corn syrup. 3  Molasses in dark corn syrup enhances its flavor and color
