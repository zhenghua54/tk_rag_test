Communities in Hockley County include Levelland (1990 population, 13,986), the largest city and the location of South Plains College; Anton (1,212), which bills itself as the Rabbit Capital of Texas; and Sundown (1,759), Smyer, Ropesville, Arnett, Claudene, Lockettville, Pep, and Roundup.
