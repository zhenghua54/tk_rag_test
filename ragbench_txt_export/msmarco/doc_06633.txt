the reduced 15 % tax rate on qualified dividends and long term capital gains previously scheduled to expire in 2008 was extended through 2010 as a result of the tax increase prevention and reconciliation act of 2005 signed into law by president george w bush short term capital gains are taxed at the investor s ordinary income tax rate and are defined as investments held for a year or less before being sold long term capital gains which are gains on dispositions of assets held for more than one year are taxed at a lower rate than short term gains
