Type 192.168.0.1 into your address bar. This is the default internet protocol address for most home broadband routers, such as the D-Link router. This address will allow you access into your router's configurations. Enter admin as the user ID and leave the password field blank when directed to log in. Enter 192.168.0.1 into your address bar. Log in to your router using admin as the user ID and leave the password field blank. Click the Tools tab located at the top of the page. A page titled Admin will pop up.
