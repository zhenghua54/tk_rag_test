Nurse Midwife Salaries. Salaries for certified nurse midwives have increased steadily. A 2010 survey by consulting firm Integrated Healthcare Services reported median yearly pay of $96,418 for nurse midwives, with additional benefits raising their total compensation to $100,480. In comparison, a 2007 survey by the American College of Nurse Midwives reported mid-range salaries of $79,093 to $89,916.
