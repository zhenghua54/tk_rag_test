The borders and anatomical relations of the bony orbit are as follows: 1  Roof (superior wall) – Formed by the frontal bone and the lesser wing of the sphenoid. 2  The frontal bone separates the orbit from the anterior cranial fossa. 3  Floor (inferior wall) – Formed by the maxilla, palatine and zygomatic bones. 1 The frontal bone separates the orbit from the anterior cranial fossa. 2  Floor (inferior wall) – Formed by the maxilla, palatine and zygomatic bones. 3  The maxilla separates the orbit from the underlying maxillary sinus. 4  Medial wall – Formed by the ethmoid, maxilla, lacrimal and sphenoid bones.
