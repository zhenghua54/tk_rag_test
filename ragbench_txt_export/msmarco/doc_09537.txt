In Ohio, as the number of occupational therapy professionals is growing, the salaries earned by occupational therapy professionals are increasing. The mean salary for occupational therapy professionals in 2006 was $45,213 per year. And in 2010 the mean salary was $54,677 per year. This growth is mirrored by state trends for all professionals. There is a 109% pay differential between the highest paid occupational therapy professionals and those in the bottom 10% of the pay bracket. Thus, there is huge variation in pay, within the field of occupational therapy in Ohio.
