Ferrous sulfate is a mineral. It supplies iron to your body, which is needed to help your blood carry oxygen to your organs. The medicine is often used to help people with iron-deficiency anemia, whose bodies have too few red blood cells because of poor diet, excess bleeding, other disorders, or pregnancy. Feosol is the brand name of ferrous sulfate, an over-the-counter (OTC) nutritional supplement used to prevent or treat low levels of iron in the blood. Iron deficiency is the most common nutritional deficiency in the United States
