The scale of mineral hardness is referred to as the Mohs scale.  They run from 1 which is Talc to 10, which is diamond. The other  units are 2: Gypsum, 3: Calcite, 4: Fluori … te, 5: Apatite, 6:  Feldspar, 7: Quartz, 8: Topaz, and 9: Corundum. The smallest unit of space on a hard drive is a bit. Eight bits make a byte, which is the basic unit. Half a byte is (appropriately) called a nibble. One thousand and twen … ty-four bytes make up a kilobyte, 1024 kilobytes make up a megabyte, 1024MB equals a gigabyte, and 1024GB equals one terabyte.
