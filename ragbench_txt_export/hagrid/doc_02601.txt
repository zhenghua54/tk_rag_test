Cho Seung-woo (born March 28, 1980) is a South Korean actor. He is best known for his leading roles in the films "The Classic" (2003), "Marathon" (2005), "" (2006) and "Inside Men" (2015) as well as in the stage musicals "Jekyll & Hyde", "Hedwig and the Angry Inch" and "Man of La Mancha". He is also known for his leading roles in television dramas "The King's Doctor" (2012), "Stranger" (2017) and Life (2018).
