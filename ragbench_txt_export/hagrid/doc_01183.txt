The larger species of "Dimetrodon" were among the largest predators of the Early Permian, although the closely related "Tappenosaurus", known from skeletal fragments in slightly younger rocks, may have been even larger at an estimated in total body length. Although some "Dimetrodon" species could grow very large, many juvenile specimens are known.
