The first "Death Star"'s construction is the subject of Michael Reaves and Steve Perry's novel "Death Star". In LucasArts's "", the player participates in a mission to secure crystals used in the Death Star's superlaser. The first Death Star under construction acts as the final stage in the video game, "". Kevin J. Anderson's "Jedi Academy" trilogy introduces the Maw Cluster of black holes that protect a laboratory where the "Death Star" prototype was built (consisting of the super structure, power core, and superlaser).
