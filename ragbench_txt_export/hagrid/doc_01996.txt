The Catholic Bible is composed of the 46 books of the Old Testament and the 27 books of the New Testament.Of these books, Tobit, Judith, 1 and 2 Maccabees, Wisdom, Sirach, Baruch, parts of Esther and parts of Daniel are deuterocanonical, and are found in the Bibles of Eastern Christianity.
