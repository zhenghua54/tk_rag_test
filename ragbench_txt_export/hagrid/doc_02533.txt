The Dolly Teen Choice Awards are hosted by "Dolly magazine".
The "Inside Soap" Awards are voted for by readers of "Inside Soap" magazine. The awards have been running since 1996. 
"Neighbours" is the second most successful recipient of Logie Awards, having won thirty-one Logies to date. Only "Home and Away", has more awards with forty-six. At the Logie Awards, "Neighbours" has won Most Popular Drama Series two times in 1987 and 1988, with a further eight nominations (2004, 2005, 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010 and 2011). The show was inducted into the Logie Hall of Fame in 2005. In 1988, Kylie Minogue became the youngest person, at nineteen, to be awarded a Gold Logie.
