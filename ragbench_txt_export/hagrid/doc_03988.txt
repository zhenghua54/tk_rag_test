Divizio is perhaps best known for his portrayal of Kano in the original "Mortal Kombat" in 1992. He also appeared in "Mortal Kombat II" as Baraka, and reprised the role of Kano for both "Mortal Kombat II" in 1993 (as a chained up prisoner) and "Mortal Kombat 3" in 1995, making his playable return. In "MK3", he also played the masked hook swordsman Kabal and the shadowy fighter Noob Saibot. In 1997, he portrayed Quan Chi in both the game and full motion video sequences in the platform title "", and provided motion capture footage for the character as well in "Mortal Kombat 4". He returned to the series in 2002, contributing to "" as a motion capture actor.
