Using Bixby. A guide to using Bixby to control your TV. To use Bixby , your TV must be connected to the Internet and you must use the microphone on your Samsung Smart Remote. To use Bixby , press and hold the button on the Samsung Smart Remote, say a command into the microphone on the remote, and then release the button to run Bixby The existing functional specifications may be changed if Bixby is updated.
