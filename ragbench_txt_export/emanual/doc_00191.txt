Deleting a recorded program. To delete a recorded program, go to the list of Recordings . Use the up and down directional buttons to move to the recording you want to delete. Press the right directional button to move to a list of 3 options. Press the down directional button to move to the Delete option, and then press Select. You will be asked if you want to remove the selected item. Press the left directional button to move to the Delete button, and then press Select to delete the program title. The program will be deleted and you will be returned to the list of Recordings
