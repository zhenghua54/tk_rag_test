Listening to the TV through Bluetooth devices (for the hearing impaired). Settings General Accessibility Multi-output Audio Try Now You can turn on both the TV speaker and Bluetooth devices at the same time. When this function is active, you can set the volume of the Bluetooth devices higher than the volume of the TV speaker. This function may not be supported depending on the model or geographical area. When you connect Bluetooth devices to the TV, Multi-output Audio is activated. For more information about connecting Bluetooth devices to the TV, refer to to the TV through Bluetooth
