The Schedule Recording/Timeshift Function Isn't Working. When Timeshift or Schedule recording aren't working, these steps may help resolve the problem. The problem Try this! Schedule Recording cannot be used. This function is not available in the U.S.A. and Canada. This function may not be supported depending on the model or geographical area. Make sure the TV has a network connection. Settings General Network Network Status Contact your Internet service provider. Position your wireless router, modem router, or access point in a central location. Avoid putting it in a corner. Use a wireless repeater to get an instant boost in your wireless signal strength. Place the repeater halfway between your wireless router and your TV. Check if there is a USB device connected to the TV. Recording will automatically stop if the signal becomes too weak. Check the free space on the USB device. The function will not work if there isn't enough storage space on the USB device.
