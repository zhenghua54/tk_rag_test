Connecting an IP control device to the TV. Settings General Network Expert Settings IP Remote You can connect an IP control device to the TV remotely to manage your IP device. To use this function, Power On with Mobile must be turned on. Turning this feature on may allow other IP control devices to control your TV. We recommend turning this feature on only if an authorized third-party custom controller is installed and configured specifically for a Samsung TV and if your Wi-Fi network is password protected. This function may not be supported depending on the model or geographical area.
